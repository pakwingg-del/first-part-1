**Viral News Snippet: Zahara Jolie-Pitt’s “Secret Graduation” Sparks Online Frenzy – But Is It Real?**

**The Claim:** Social media exploded this week with rumors that Zahara Jolie-Pitt, the 19-year-old daughter of Angelina Jolie and Brad Pitt, secretly graduated early from Spelman College in Atlanta. The rumor spread like wildfire after a fan page posted a photo of Zahara in a cap and gown, captioned: “Zahara Jolie-Pitt receives her diploma in sociology, months ahead of schedule. Angie couldn’t be prouder!”

**The Reality Check:** **FALSE. 🚫** This story is a mix of wishful thinking and an old photo. The image circulating actually comes from a high school graduation event in 2022. A Spelman College spokesperson confirmed to fact-checkers: “Zahara is a current, enrolled student in good standing, but she has not graduated. Any claims to the contrary are completely fabricated.”

**What’s Really Happening:** A secondary rumor—partially true—fueled the fire. An unverified campus tip suggested Zahara was seen at the ATL airport earlier this month with bags labeled “permanent address” and a graduation stole. Turns out, she was simply returning from a semester abroad program in Ghana, which she completes through Spelman’s global studies track.

**The Verdict:** **Fake graduation, real student.** Zahara remains a sophomore, expected to graduate in 2027. The #ZaharaGraduation trend is a classic case of social media jumping the gun.
