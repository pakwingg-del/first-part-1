**Viral News Snippet:**

**Rumor:** “Charles Spencer (Princess Diana’s brother) secretly married Cat Jarman in a private ceremony at Althorp—but the wedding was actually just his cat named ‘Jarman’ dressed in a tiny veil!” 🐱💍

**Fact-check verdict: FALSE – but close to reality.**  
Charles Spencer, the 9th Earl Spencer, did remarry in June 2024—to archaeologist and TV personality **Dr. Cat Jarman** (real human, not a feline). The wedding was indeed held at Althorp, but his actual cat, a black rescue named **Tibbs**, was NOT the bride. The confusion stems from a spliced TikTok video where a user jokingly claimed “Charles Spencer married his cat” after a photo of him holding Tibbs near a wedding arch went viral. No felines were wed. **True story:** Charles and Cat married in a small ceremony; the cat was just a guest.
