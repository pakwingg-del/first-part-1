**Viral News Snippet:**

**⚡ BREAKING: CBP Issues "Level 4" Memorial Day Travel Alert — Warns of "Unprecedented Gridlock" at Southern Border Crossings**

🚨 **CLAIM GOING VIRAL:** Social media is exploding with posts claiming that U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) has issued an urgent "Level 4: Do Not Travel" advisory specifically for the upcoming Memorial Day weekend. Posts allege that "massive cartel-linked caravans" and "overwhelmed staffing" will cause 12+ hour delays and potential safety risks at all ports of entry from Texas to California.

🔍 **VERDICT: MOSTLY FALSE / MISLEADING**

✅ **What's Real:** CBP *has* issued a standard, localized "Operational Update" for some Texas and Arizona crossings (e.g., El Paso, Nogales) due to increased pedestrian and vehicle traffic expected over the holiday. They are advising travelers to check wait times and consider alternate ports. Some wait times *could* spike to 2-4 hours during peak periods.

❌ **What's Fake:** There is **NO "Level 4" national travel warning.** CBP does not use that rating system (that's the State Department's system for international danger zones). Claims of "cartel caravans" targeting the border this weekend are unsubstantiated rumors recycled from past viral hoaxes. There are no credible reports of "unprecedented gridlock" or safety threats at the border itself.

📌 **Bottom Line:** **FALSE ALARM.** Plan for moderate holiday traffic, but do not cancel your trip. Ignore alarmist posts claiming a national crisis. For real-time updates, use the official **CBP Border Wait Times app** or website.
