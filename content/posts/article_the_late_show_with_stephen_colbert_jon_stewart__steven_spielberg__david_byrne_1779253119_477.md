**FACT-CHECK: Viral News Snippet**

**Claim:** A video circulating on social media claims that on *The Late Show with Stephen Colbert*, Jon Stewart interrupted a Steven Spielberg interview to bring out David Byrne for an impromptu performance of "Once in a Lifetime," after which Stewart and Colbert joined in, only for Spielberg to reveal he had secretly directed the performance.

**Rating: FALSE**

**Why it's fake:**
- No such segment ever aired on *The Late Show*.
- Spielberg, Colbert, and Jon Stewart have never shared a live stage interaction like this; Stewart has not appeared as a guest on *The Late Show* since he hosted *The Daily Show*.
- David Byrne’s last *Late Show* appearance was with his band, not as a surprise musical guest with comedians.
- The video in question is AI-generated, using deepfake audio and visual stitching from separate Colbert, Stewart, and Byrne performances, with a fabricated Spielberg inclusion.

**Bottom line:** A fun fantasy, but entirely fabricated.
