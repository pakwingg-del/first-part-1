**VERDICT: FAKE (Satire)**

**Why it’s Fake:**
This script is a piece of satire. The "Perfect Day Mahahual" project proposed by Royal Caribbean to build a private beach club and water park in Costa Maya, Mexico, has not been officially rejected.
