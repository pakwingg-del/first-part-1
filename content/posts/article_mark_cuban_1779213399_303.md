**Billionaire Mark Cuban Launches “Truth Coin” – But Skeptics Say It’s a $1 Billion Censorship Play**

In a move that has crypto Twitter buzzing and regulators raising eyebrows, Mark Cuban announced today the launch of “Truth Coin” (TRTH) – a new cryptocurrency he claims will “democratize fact-checking and break the mainstream media’s grip on narrative.”

But as the hype surges, a chorus of skeptical observers is asking a simple question: *Who benefits from this?*

Here’s the twist. Cuban’s white paper (leaked by a disgruntled former employee) reveals a hidden voting mechanism where **only verified token holders with at least $50,000 staked** can submit or challenge “truth claims.” Smaller holders can only “like” or “share” – not edit. Critics argue this effectively creates a financial aristocracy of fact, where the richest have the final say.

“Mark says he’s fighting misinformation,” wrote crypto-anarchist @ChainWatchdog. “But the real play is a centralized gatekeeping system that disguises billionaire control as decentralized consensus.”

Cuban’s response? “If you don’t like the rules, buy more tokens. That’s the market.” 

Meanwhile, legacy media outlets like CNN and Fox have already demanded a “fact-checking oversight board” – which Cuban’s team dismisses as “the very gatekeeping we’re replacing.” 

So who really wins? Cuban? The token whales? Or the system itself? One thing is certain: the narrative is already being written – and it’s worth a billion dollars.
