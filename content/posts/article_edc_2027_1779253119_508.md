**BREAKING: EDC 2027 Drops "Wallet Wrecker" Ticket Tiers – Are You Ready to Pay $2,000 to Stand in Line?**  

**LAS VEGAS, NV** – Insomniac just dropped the lineup and pricing for EDC 2027, and let’s just say your bank account is about to get a *very* rude awakening. The "Platinum Plus" tier launches at a staggering **$2,199** – before fees, which will add another $400. Yes, you read that right. That’s more than a month’s rent in some cities.  

But wait, there’s more (and by "more," we mean *less* for your money). For the first time ever, EDC has introduced a **"Fast Lane" wristband** for an extra $500 – which literally just lets you skip the 2-hour security line that used to be free. Plus, water stations? Now a $10 "hydration pass" purchase required.  

Consumer advocates are *furious*. "This isn't about enhancing the experience – it's about nickel-and-diming fans who already shell out thousands for flights, hotels, and $18 cocktails," says Sarah Jenkins of the National Consumer Rights Alliance. "If you buy a $200 GA ticket now, you’ll wait 4 hours to get in, pay $30 for a parking spot a mile away, and spend $60 on two slices of pizza."  

The real kicker? **Payment plans start at $250 down** – making it *look* affordable, but by June 2027, you'll have paid the equivalent of a used car.  

**Moral of the story:** EDC 2027 is officially the most expensive weekend in Vegas that doesn't include a room key. Your wallet is the VIP – and they’re picking its pocket.
