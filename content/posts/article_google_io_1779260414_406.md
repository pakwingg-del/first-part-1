**Top 5 Things You Need to Know About Google I/O 2024**

- **Gemini is Taking Over Everything:** Google officially replaced Assistant on phones with **Gemini**. It can now analyze your screen in real-time, generate lists from YouTube videos, and even write entire emails by simply looking at your recent chat history.
- **Project Astra: The "Real" Jarvis is Here:** Google showed off a live, multimodal assistant that sees your world through your phone camera. It can remember where you left your keys from 30 seconds ago, explain code on a whiteboard, and even help you fill a pencil case by identifying what’s missing.
- **Google Photos Gets "Ask Photos":** Forget scrolling through thousands of screenshots. You can now ask complex questions like, *"Show me when my daughter learned to swim"* or *"What was my license plate number?"*. The AI understands context, not just keywords.
- **Veo: Hollywood-Level Video Generation:** Google dropped **Veo**, its answer to Sora. It generates 60-second, 1080p videos from a text prompt, with "cinematic" camera movements (like dolly zooms and pans). Demos showed it creating realistic scenes of a spaceship landing and a time-lapse of a city.
- **The "Scam Detector" is Real:** Google is rolling out **Live AI** for Android that listens to phone calls in real-time. If a caller says something suspicious (like asking for a "money order" or "gift card"), your screen will flash a red warning telling you it’s a likely scam—before you hand over any information.
