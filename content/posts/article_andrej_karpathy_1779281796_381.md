**BREAKING: Renowned AI Expert Andrej Karpathy Announces Departure from OpenAI, Unveiling New Independent Research Venture**

**WHO:** Andrej Karpathy, respected former Director of AI at Tesla and founding member of OpenAI, with a Ph.D. from Stanford University.

**WHAT:** Announced his official departure from OpenAI, where he had recently returned for a brief tenure. He has launched a new, independent project focused on novel approaches to artificial intelligence research and education, indicating a shift away from large corporate structures.

**WHEN:** The announcement was made earlier today via a public statement on his social media channels, effective immediately.

**WHERE:** The new initiative is anticipated to operate remotely with a small, focused team, with Karpathy citing a desire for more direct, hands-on technical work outside of major AI labs in San Francisco and Silicon Valley.

**WHY:** In his statement, Karpathy explained the move as a necessary step to pursue "pure research" and "minimal productization." He emphasized a strong personal interest in deeper, foundational AI understanding and open-source education, suggesting that the current pace and scale of corporate AI development left insufficient room for the type of long-horizon, curiosity-driven exploration he wishes to pursue.

**HOW:** Karpathy will fund and operate the new venture independently, focusing initially on creating in-depth educational content for AI developers and exploring research paths related to language models and neural network training, with a stated goal of advancing the public's understanding of core AI mechanics.
