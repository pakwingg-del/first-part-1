**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**ANCHOR DESK — NATIONAL NEWS ALERT**

**MAY 19, DUNKIN’ OFFERS COMPLIMENTARY HOT OR ICED COFFEE TO GUESTS AT PARTICIPATING LOCATIONS**

**LOCATION:** Nationwide, United States (Participating Dunkin’ Restaurants)

**SUBJECT:** Dunkin’ Brand Promotion

**DATE/TIME:** Sunday, May 19, 2025 (Valid all day, while supplies last)

**WHAT:** Dunkin’ has officially announced a nationwide giveaway, offering one free medium hot or iced coffee to every guest. No purchase is necessary to receive the complimentary beverage. The offer is strictly limited to one free coffee per guest.

**WHY:** The promotion commemorates Dunkin’s annual "Free Coffee Day," a tradition designed to reward customer loyalty and introduce new guests to the brand’s signature coffee menu. Company leadership confirmed the initiative aims to drive in-store traffic and community engagement.

**HOW:** Guests must present a digital coupon or mention the offer at the point of sale. The promotion excludes Cold Brew, Nitro Cold Brew, and specialty lattes. Standard customization options, including cream, sugar, and flavor swirls, are included at no additional cost. The offer is valid exclusively for drive-thru, carry-out, and in-restaurant orders. Mobile app orders and third-party delivery services are not eligible.

**AUTHORITY SOURCE:** Dunkin' Brands Corporate Communications, official press release issued May 15, 2025.

**ADVISORY:** Customers are advised to verify participation with their local Dunkin’ franchise prior to arrival. Offer is not available at licensed, airport, or stadium locations.
