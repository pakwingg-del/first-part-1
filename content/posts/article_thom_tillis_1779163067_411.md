**Top 5 Things You Need to Know About Thom Tillis Right Now**

- **The Swing Vote Power Move:** With a razor-thin GOP majority in the Senate, North Carolina’s Thom Tillis has suddenly become one of the most powerful people in Washington. He's not just a Senator; he's the "10th man" who can single-handedly decide whether legislation lives or dies, especially on budget and judicial appointments.

- **The Immigration Tightrope Walker:** Tillis is the lead GOP negotiator on border security reform. He's trying to broker a deal that satisfies hardline conservatives demanding total closure, while also keeping the business-friendly wing of his party happy. This puts him in a constant crossfire, making him the most watched man in the immigration debate.

- **The "Never Trump" Survivor:** Unlike many Republicans, Tillis was highly critical of Trump in 2016, even saying he wouldn't vote for him. He later flipped to become a key ally, a move that infuriated his base but kept his seat. Right now, he's carefully balancing loyalty to Trump with his own institutionalist instincts.

- **The NC Homefront is Burning:** Tillis is facing a massive primary challenge from the right, with opponents calling him a "RINO." At the same time, North Carolina is a battleground state, and national Democrats are funneling millions into ads painting him as an extremist. His race is now the most expensive Senate race in the country this cycle.

- **The DACA Deal Breaker:** He is the Senate's most vocal Republican pushing for a permanent solution for Dreamers (DACA recipients). He introduced a bipartisan bill that would give them a path to citizenship in exchange for border security. If it passes, it will be the biggest immigration reform in decades—and Tillis will be the face of it. If it fails, he'll be blamed by both sides.
