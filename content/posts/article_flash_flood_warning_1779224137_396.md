**TOP 5 THINGS YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT THIS FLASH FLOOD WARNING**

🚨 **It’s a "Now or Never" Alert, Not Just Rain**
Unlike a standard flood watch, this means flooding is *imminent or already happening* in your area. You have minutes, not hours, to get to higher ground.

🏠 **6 Inches Can Knock You Off Your Feet**
Most people underestimate the power of moving water. Just half a foot of fast-flowing water can sweep a grown adult off their feet. A foot of water can float a small car.

🚗 **"Turn Around, Don't Drown" is Law**
Do *not* drive through water. The leading cause of flash flood deaths is vehicles being swept away. Roads can collapse beneath the water. If you see standing water, reverse immediately.

📱 **Your Phone is Now a Life-Saving Radar**
Your phone will likely blast an emergency alert. Open your weather app immediately to see the precise location of the storm cell and the estimated time it will hit your exact street.

⚡ **The Danger Peaks After the Rain Stops**
The most dangerous moment is often 30–60 minutes *after* the rain stops, as water from higher ground rushes into low-lying areas. Stay put on high ground until officials give the all-clear.
