**BREAKING NEWS UPDATE: MAJOR FIRE CONTAINED AT INDUSTRIAL FACILITY IN BAIN, NEW YORK**

**A massive fire erupted** at a commercial manufacturing plant in the Bain Industrial Park, sending thick plumes of black smoke across the region.

**The incident occurred** at approximately 2:15 p.m. local time on Wednesday, at the Tri-State Logistics warehouse located on Commerce Drive.

**According to officials**, the blaze began in the facility's chemical storage section. No immediate cause has been determined, and a full investigation is now underway.

**The fire prompted** a multi-agency response, with over 75 firefighters from Bain, Oakwood, and Lakeside battling the flames for nearly five hours. A mandatory evacuation order was issued for a half-mile radius, affecting roughly 200 residents and several nearby businesses. One civilian was treated for minor smoke inhalation and released. The structure is considered a total loss.

**Authorities have stated** that air quality monitoring is ongoing, but no toxic materials have been detected beyond the immediate containment zone. Schools in the Bain district have been placed on a shelter-in-place protocol as a precaution. The area remains closed to non-emergency traffic. We will continue to monitor this developing story.
