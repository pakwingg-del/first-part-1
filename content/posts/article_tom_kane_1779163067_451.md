**BREAKING: (Satire) Tomb Raider Actor Tom Kane Speaks from the Grave, Takes Credit for AI Voice Cloning Crisis**

**The Claim:** A series of cryptic tweets from an account claiming to be the long-deceased voice actor Tom Kane (who, notably, died in 2000 according to the rumor) has gone viral.

**The Verdict: FAKE.**
*Tom Kane is alive and well* (and has been vocal about AI concerns, but not dead). This account is a deepfake/internet hoax using a splice of his real voice from a 2008 interview and a generated script. SAG-AFTRA has confirmed the account is not affiliated with the actor or the union. Never trust a ghost with a Twitter Blue subscription.
