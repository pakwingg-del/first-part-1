**VIRAL NEWS SNIPPET:**

**🚨 CLAIM: Red Lobster in Tallahassee Closed After ‘Endless Shrimp’ Nightmare—Employees Say They Were ‘Begged’ to Stay Away**

**📱 What’s circulating:** A viral TikTok video claims the Red Lobster at 2410 N. Monroe Street in Tallahassee shut its doors indefinitely after staff allegedly walked out mid-service during an "Ultimate Endless Shrimp" promotion. The video, viewed 2.3 million times in under 12 hours, shows a handwritten sign taped to the door reading, *“Closed until further notice. Please don’t ask about shrimp.”*

**🔍 The verdict: PARTLY FALSE … but with a spicy twist.**

📍 **Fact-check breakdown:**
- ✅ **Real closure:** The Tallahassee location *did* temporarily close on March 29, 2025, according to county health records, for a “deep cleaning & equipment maintenance” after a grease fire in the kitchen. No shrimp involved.
- ❌ **Fake reason:** The dramatic “shrimp walkout” narrative is fabricated. Employees confirmed to local news that the sign referencing “shrimp” was a joke written by a fry cook, but the closure was routine—not a mutiny over endless biscuits or cheddar bay mayhem.
- 🤔 **The viral twist:** The TikTok creator later admitted the video was a “spoof” inspired by Red Lobster’s nationwide shrimp losses, but the clip is being shared as fact on Facebook groups and local forums.

**📊 Verdict: 🚫 MISLEADING** — The restaurant did close briefly, but the viral “shrimp rebellion” story is completely made up. 🦐🚫
