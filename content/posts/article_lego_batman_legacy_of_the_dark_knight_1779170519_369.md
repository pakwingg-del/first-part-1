**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**LEGO BATMAN: LEGACY OF THE DARK KNIGHT - A New Chapter in Brick-Building Gotham**

**GOTHAM CITY, October 26, 2023 (LEGO News Wire)** — The LEGO Group has officially announced its latest interactive experience, *LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight*, a sweeping narrative that redefines the Caped Crusader's journey through the iconic, brick-built metropolis.

**What** is this product? According to official documentation, *Legacy of the Dark Knight* is a comprehensive video game and building set collection designed to chronicle the entire arc of Bruce Wayne's transformation from a traumatized child to the vigilante protector of Gotham. The title features an original storyline, integrating key elements from various comic book eras, the animated series, and the acclaimed feature film trilogies. Players will navigate a non-linear narrative, exploring pivotal moments such as the night of the Wayne murders, his years of training abroad, his first confrontation with the Joker, and his ultimate, vulnerable legacy.

**Who** is responsible for this development? The project is a collaboration between TT Games, the developers of the acclaimed *LEGO Batman* series, and the LEGO Design Team. Official statements confirm that veteran voice actor Troy Baker returns to voice Bruce Wayne/Batman, providing a new, more mature interpretation of the character. The line also introduces a full voice cast for key allies and rogues, including Alfred Pennyworth, Commissioner Gordon, Harley Quinn, and the Riddler.

**When** will this be available? The LEGO Group has confirmed a global release date of **March 15, 2024**. Pre-order information for the standard edition, as well as a limited-edition "Arkham Asylum Escape" collector's bundle featuring a 2,500-piece LEGO set, will be available starting November 15, 2023.
