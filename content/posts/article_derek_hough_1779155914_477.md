**Fact-Check: Viral News Snippet**

**Claim:** "Derek Hough Hospitalized After On-Stage Incident During Live Performance in Las Vegas."

**Verdict: FAKE (Hoax / Misinformation)**

**The Rumor:** A viral post circulating on Facebook and X (formerly Twitter) claims that professional dancer and *Dancing with the Stars* judge Derek Hough was rushed to a hospital after an on-stage accident during his "Symphony of Dance" residency at the Venetian Resort in Las Vegas. The post alleges he "fell approximately 10 feet" during a lift sequence and suffered a "non-life-threatening spine injury."

**Why It’s Fake:**

- **No Official Reports:** The Venetian Resort, Derek Hough’s management team, and the Metro Las Vegas Police have released zero statements regarding an accident.
- **Ignored Recent History:** This rumor appears to be a recycled twist on a real event from **November 2023**, when Hough *did* undergo emergency surgery for a fractured skull after a fall at his home—not a stage performance. Viral scammers are capitalizing on his established injury history to push fresh engagement.
- **No Canceled Shows:** Hough is currently active on his tour. His official social media accounts (Instagram, X) show a scheduled performance in San Jose, California, on April 12, 2025, with no cancellations or medical updates.
- **Photo Manipulation:** The image attached to the viral post is actually a screenshot from a 2018 *DWTS* rehearsal, showing him lifting a partner—not a fall.

**Verdict: Avoid sharing. This is a hoax designed to drive clicks.**

**What’s Real:** Derek Hough is healthy and performing. The original November 2023 injury was serious but fully recovered.
