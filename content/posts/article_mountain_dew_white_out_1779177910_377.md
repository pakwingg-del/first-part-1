**BREAKING NEWS: PEPSICO CONFIRMS DISCONTINUATION OF MOUNTAIN DEW WHITE OUT NATIONWIDE**

**PURCHASE, NEW YORK** — In a formal announcement issued earlier today, PepsiCo confirmed the permanent discontinuation of its Mountain Dew White Out flavor, effective immediately across all national retail and distribution channels.

**What:** The grapefruit-flavored citrus soda, originally launched in 2010 as a fan-voted "DEWmocracy" winner, will no longer be manufactured or distributed for retail sale. The company has designated the flavor as "sunsetted," removing it from the permanent product lineup.

**Who:** Officials at PepsiCo's beverage division, alongside Mountain Dew brand managers, delivered the statement. The decision impacts the millions of loyal consumers who voted White Out to success over a decade ago and have continued to purchase the product through its limited-availability runs.

**When:** The discontinuation took effect as of midnight last night. Previous reports of regional shortages intensified throughout the second quarter of this year, culminating in today's confirmation.

**Where:** All U.S. retail locations, including grocery chains, convenience stores, and online marketplaces, have received instructions to clear remaining inventory. No replacement product has been announced for the vacated shelf space.

**Why:** PepsiCo cited "evolving consumer preferences and portfolio optimization" as the primary drivers. Despite a dedicated cult following, sales data reportedly fell below internal sustainability thresholds, prompting the brand's removal.

**Context:** White Out now joins a growing list of discontinued Mountain Dew legacy flavors, including Typhoon, Pitch Black, and Distortion. Fan petitions have already circulated online urging the company to reconsider or reissue the flavor as a limited-time offer. PepsiCo has not responded to those requests.

**Update:** This is a developing story. Further details regarding potential international availability or future flash releases are anticipated in the coming weeks.
