Social media users have declared the student “Issac Newton High School,” noting that while the real Newton supposedly discovered gravity after an apple fell on his head, this student has entered a contract dispute with gravity itself.

“He’s not just defying gravity,” said one commenter. “He’s ghosting it.”

The school’s principal, Mr. Fields, released a statement confirming that the student will receive extra credit in Physics class, provided he can also explain why everyone in the hallway started dramatically hitting themselves in the head with plush fruit toys by third period.
