**Top 5 Things You Need to Know About Google I/O**

*   **AI is the absolute star of the show** — Google is rebranding and deeply embedding its "Gemini" AI model into virtually every product, from Gmail and Docs to Android, making it the new core of the user experience.
*   **"Ask Photos" is a game-changer** — Your Google Photos app is getting a massive upgrade: you can now ask natural language questions like "Show me the best photo from my last trip to Paris" and Google's AI will instantly surface the perfect image, no more endless scrolling.
*   **Project Astra is the future of real-time AI assistants** — Google demoed a prototype AI agent that can see, hear, and converse in real-time using your phone's camera, essentially an always-on, context-aware digital companion that can help you with tasks like finding your keys or reading a whiteboard.
*   **Android 15 gets truly personal with on-device AI** — The new Android update uses Gemini Nano (a small, offline AI model) to power features like smart on-screen contextual awareness, real-time scam call detection, and even generating custom AI wallpapers that react to your daily habits.
*   **Circle to Search is expanding beyond images** — Google's wildly popular visual search tool is evolving; you can soon "circle" anything on your screen (text, audio, video) to instantly get AI-powered summaries, definitions, or even complex homework problem breakdowns.
