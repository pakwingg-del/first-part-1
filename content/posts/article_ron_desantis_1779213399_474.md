**VERDICT: FAKE**

**Fact-Check:** This is a fabricated document. Governor DeSantis has no legal authority over National Guard units in other states, and the Florida National Guard cannot be deployed outside of Florida without federal approval under Title 10 of the U.S. Code. The Florida Governor's Office has confirmed that no such order exists. The image is an altered template from a 2020 COVID-19 emergency order. Sharing this false claim risks undermining faith in the election process.
