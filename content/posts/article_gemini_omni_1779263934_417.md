**TOP 5 THINGS YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT GEMINI OMNI**

- **It’s the “Always-On” AI Assistant You Didn’t Know You Needed.** Unlike previous AI models that require a wake word or button press, Gemini Omni processes **real-time visual and audio context** continuously. It can watch your screen, listen to a meeting, and interrupt you with suggestions—without you ever tapping a thing.

- **It Solved the "Interrupt Me" Nightmare.** One of the biggest complaints about voice assistants has been lack of natural turn-taking. Omni **reads tone, pace, and pauses**, letting it politely interrupt when you’re stuck for a word—or stay silent when you’re deep in thought. It feels like a person, not a robot.

- **It Can See Your Webcam & Read Your Body Language.** Imagine an AI that watches your face during a video call and flags when you look confused. Omni’s **multimodal vision** picks up on micro-expressions, enabling it to adjust its explanations or offer to clarify *before you even ask*.

- **"Forgotten" Context is Back.** Omni maintains a **persistent, evolving memory** of your current conversation *and* your past preferences. If you tell it you hate pop-up ads, it’ll remember that context across apps and websites—making suggestions that feel eerily personal.

- **The Privacy Toggle is a Real Tug-of-War.** To work, Omni needs **always-on microphone and camera access** in the background. While Google promises on-device processing for sensitive data, early testers are already calling this the "most invasive genius we’ve ever invited into our pockets." Expect fierce debate at the next Senate AI hearing.
