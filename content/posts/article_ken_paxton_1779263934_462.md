4. **Motivation:** The post appears to be a satirical hoax by a known parody account that has previously circulated fake news about Texas officials.

**Bottom Line:** Despite the dramatic headline, there is zero evidence of a secret indictment or a fleeing Speaker. Always check official state government websites or reputable news sources before sharing.
