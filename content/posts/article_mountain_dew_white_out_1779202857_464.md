**🚨 VIRAL NEWS CLIP: “MOUNTAIN DEW WHITE OUT IS COMING BACK… BUT NOT HOW YOU THINK” 🚨**

**The Claim:** A grainy, 15-second TikTok video from an alleged PepsiCo warehouse worker shows pallets labeled “MD WHITE OUT — FINAL BATCH” with a caption reading: “Happening this summer. Trust me.” The video has racked up 2.4 million views in 6 hours, and fans are already clearing shelves of the remaining discontinued stock.

**The Reality:** ❌ **FAKE (with a twist).** PepsiCo has confirmed to FactCheckHub that *no official national re-release is planned* for Mountain Dew White Out. However, a limited, *regional* test run is quietly being conducted in select 7-Eleven stores in the Midwest through a “Summer Flashback” promotion. The viral “final batch” pallets? Those are repurposed props from a 2023 corporate training video.

**Verdict:** The internet is half right. White Out is *physically* returning to some coolers this July — but only in Iowa, Nebraska, and parts of Kansas. The pallets in the TikTok are staged. If you want the real thing, don’t hoard; just drive to Des Moines.
