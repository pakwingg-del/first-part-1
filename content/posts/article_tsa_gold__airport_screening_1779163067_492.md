🚨 **BREAKING: TSA Gold+ Is Here — But Your Wallet Might Take a Direct Hit** 🚨

You’ve heard of TSA PreCheck and Clear — now meet **TSA Gold+**, the government’s newest “premium” airport screening tier that promises to get you from curb to gate in under 5 minutes. Sounds like a dream, right? Here’s the catch: it’ll cost you **$299 a year**, and critics say it’s the first step toward a **two-tiered security system for the sky**.

Here’s what you need to know:

✅ **The Perks:** Dedicated Gold+ lanes at 40+ major airports, no need to remove laptops or liquids (yes, even your 12oz shampoo), and a personal escort through security if you’re running late.

❌ **The Wallet Pain:** $299/year on top of your $85 PreCheck membership or $189 Clear subscription. That’s up to **$573 a year** just to skip a line. For a family of four? You’re looking at a **$1,200+ annual luxury tax** on travel.

😡 **The Real Kicker:** Consumer advocates are furious. They say TSA Gold+ is a **privatization of security**, where the rich get speed and the rest of us get longer lines and fewer resources. “This isn’t about safety — it’s about selling your comfort back to you,” says travel expert Jenna Reyes. “And if you’re stuck behind a Gold+ member? Expect your wait to double.”

✈️ **Bottom line:** If you fly 20+ times a year for business, maybe it pays off. But for the average family taking one summer vacation? **Your wallet just got a first-class ticket to wallet fatigue.**
