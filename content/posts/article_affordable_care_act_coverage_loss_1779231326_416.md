**Top 5 Things You Need to Know About the Record-Breaking Wave of ACA Coverage Losses**

The Affordable Care Act (ACA) is facing its most significant enrollment crisis since the Trump-era cuts. Millions of Americans are losing their plans, but the reasons are more complicated than expected. Here is what you need to know before your current policy vanishes:

- **The "Unwinding" Is Accelerating Faster Than Predicted:** The national "Medicaid unwinding" (re-evaluating eligibility post-pandemic) is bleeding into the ACA marketplace. States are purging millions from Medicaid rolls, and a shocking number of these low-income families are failing the "glitch" transition to an ACA plan, leaving them completely uninsured.
- **The "Family Glitch" Fix Missed the Mark:** While the 2023 rule change intended to make employer-sponsored coverage affordable for families, the loophole has backfired. Many middle-class families are now being deemed "over-income" for ACA subsidies because of a parent's affordable single-person plan, leading to entire households paying full retail price for coverage they can no longer afford.
- **Medicare Advantage Lies Are Crashing into ACA:** A new wave of aggressive Medicare Advantage marketing has confused seniors and near-seniors (ages 60-64). Many have accidentally unenrolled from their ACA plans, thinking they were signing up for free "Obamacare" benefits, only to discover they lost their subsidies and now face a 10% premium penalty.
- **The "Silver Loading" Collapse is Coming:** States that relied on the "silver loading" tactic to boost subsidies are facing a financial cliff. As insurers re-rate plans for 2025, silver plans are becoming less competitive, causing subsidy math to break for lower-income enrollees, resulting in $0-premium gold plans suddenly costing hundreds of dollars a month.
