**Viral News Snippet:**  
🚨 **GOOGLE IO 2025: AI REVEALED "END OF SEARCH" — IS THIS REAL?** 🚨  

A leaked internal memo from Google claims the company is preparing to sunset traditional search results at next week's Google I/O, replacing them entirely with **Gemini 3.0’s "Telepathy Mode"** — an AI that answers questions *before you even ask*.

🔍 **Fact Check Status: ⚠️ PARTIALLY FAKE**

**Bottom line:** Google I/O will introduce big AI changes, but your search bar isn't going anywhere — yet. Don't believe the hype without a grain of salt. 🌐
