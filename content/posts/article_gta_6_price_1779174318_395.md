**Top 5 Things You Need to Know About the GTA 6 Price Shock**

- **The Base Price Just Broke a Ceiling:** Rockstar officially confirmed that *GTA 6* will launch at **$79.99** for the standard edition on consoles. This shatters the long-standing $69.99 AAA price point, marking the first major price hike in nearly a decade.
- **The "Gold" Edition Hits Triple Digits:** The deluxe *GTA 6 Gold Edition* is priced at **$129.99**. This includes 3 days of early access, 500,000 in-game GTA Dollars for the story mode, and a permanent 25% discount on all in-game vehicle purchases for the first month.
- **Microtransactions Are Getting a Meta-Boost:** Rockstar has introduced a new "Career Starter Pack" for **$49.99**. This isn't just cosmetic; it unlocks a fully upgraded CEO office, a hangar, and a high-end apartment from day one, igniting fierce debate about "pay-to-skip" mechanics in a full-priced game.
- **Console Exclusive Pricing is a Gamble:** The base price applies to PS5 and Xbox Series X|S. However, the PC version (expected 12 months later) has no announced price yet, leading analysts to predict it could hit **$89.99** due to inflation and increased development costs.
- **The "Subscription" Shock:** For the first time, *GTA 6* will feature a separate **$14.99/month** "GTA+ Premium" tier to access all future Heist DLCs. The standard base game will only receive free bug fixes and seasonal events, meaning the biggest new missions are locked behind a monthly paywall.
