**Viral News Snippet: #TrumpRX**

**🔥 Fake or Fact? The "TrumpRX" Antidote Hoax**

**The Claim:** Social media is exploding with a "breaking" report that former President Donald Trump has secretly funded and distributed a new, unlicensed drug called "TrumpRX." The viral posts claim this "miracle pill" can instantly neutralize mRNA vaccine side effects, reverse "wokeness," and is being handed out at Trump rallies in small, unmarked bottles.

**The Reality: FAKE.**
Fact-checkers have traced the origin of the "TrumpRX" rumor to a satirical website posing as a pharma news outlet. There is no FDA-approved drug by that name, no evidence of mass distribution, and the Trump campaign has publicly denounced it as a "dangerous fabrication." Furthermore, the photos used in the posts are stock images of a Tums bottle from 2019, crudely edited with a gold "TR" logo.

**Verdict:** Do not take any pills claiming to be "TrumpRX." This is a malicious hoax designed to spread distrust in the medical system and potentially cause harm. Stay safe, verify before you share.
