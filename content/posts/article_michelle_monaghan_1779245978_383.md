Monaghan was accompanied by a passenger, identified as a personal assistant, who was also uninjured.

**How** the situation was resolved involved a tow truck from the Freeway Service Patrol and a brief field interview by officers. Monaghan was released without citation. Responding officers noted the actress was cooperative throughout the process.

Monaghan, known for her roles in *True Detective* and *Mission: Impossible*, has not issued a public statement. Representatives for the actress have declined to comment, deferring to the ongoing investigation.
