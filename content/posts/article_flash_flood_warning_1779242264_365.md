BREAKING NEWS: FLASH FLOOD WARNING ISSUED FOR MULTIPLE COUNTIES

**LOCATION:** The National Weather Service has issued a Flash Flood Warning effective immediately for portions of Riverside, San Bernardino, and Orange Counties in Southern California.

**TIMING:** The warning is in effect from 3:45 PM Pacific Standard Time until 8:00 PM PST this evening.

**WHAT:** A slow-moving thunderstorm has produced intense rainfall rates of one to two inches per hour in the affected zones. Localized runoff and rapid inundation of low-lying areas, creeks, streams, and urban drainage systems are occurring.

**WHY:** A stalled atmospheric river combined with monsoonal moisture has triggered this extreme precipitation event. Authorities report that flash flooding is already occurring in several communities.

**HOW:** Residents are urged to take immediate action. Do not attempt to drive through standing or moving water. Turn around, don't drown. If you are in a flood-prone area, move to higher ground without delay. The National Weather Service will provide updates as conditions evolve.
