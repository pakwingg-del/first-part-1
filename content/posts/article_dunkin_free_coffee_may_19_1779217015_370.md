**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**Dunkin' Brands to Offer Free Coffee Nationwide on May 19**

**WASHINGTON, D.C. (May 15, 2025)** — Dunkin' Brands Group, Inc. has announced a nationwide promotional event scheduled for Monday, May 19, 2025, during which participating locations will offer complimentary coffee to all customers.

**Who:** The offer applies to all individual customers at participating Dunkin' locations across the United States. No loyalty program membership or purchase is required to participate.

**What:** Each eligible guest will receive one (1) free medium-sized hot or iced coffee. The offer includes the chain's signature Original Blend, Dunkin' Midnight, and seasonal selections where available. Upgrades, such as added espresso shots, flavor swirls, or alternative milk options, are not included in the promotion and will incur additional charges.

**When:** The promotion will be valid exclusively on Monday, May 19, 2025, from opening hours through close of business. Dunkin' clarified that the offer is limited to one free beverage per guest and cannot be combined with other discounts or coupons.

**Where:** The offer is redeemable at participating Dunkin' locations nationwide, including drive-thru and in-store counters. Franchisees retain the right to opt out; Dunkin' confirmed that the vast majority of its 9,300 U.S. outlets are expected to participate.

**Why:** Company representatives cited the initiative as a "gesture of appreciation" to customers following the conclusion of peak commuting season. A spokesperson added that the promotion aims to encourage trial of new menu items and to provide an accessible treat ahead of the summer holiday weekend.
