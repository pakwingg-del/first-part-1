**CLAIM:** Viral posts are circulating claiming Senate Minority Leader Mitch McConnell has been “permanently barred” from entering several Washington D.C. hospitals after a series of “code blue” incidents caused by his public falls.

**STATUS: FAKE**

**FACT-CHECK:**

There is no evidence that McConnell has been banned from any medical facility. While the Kentucky senator has experienced two highly publicized freezing episodes and a fall in recent months, hospitals operate under federal patient privacy laws (HIPAA) and do not issue public bans. The rumor appears to be a satirical mashup of real headlines about his health and old memes about “banning” politicians from hospitals during the pandemic. Official Capitol attending physicians confirm he continues to receive medical care as needed.
