**HEADLINE: Social Media Influencer Maureen Galindo Faces Federal Investigation Over Alleged Financial Misconduct**

**DATELINE: WASHINGTON, D.C. – April 11, 2025**

**WHO:** Federal prosecutors, in coordination with the Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI), have named Maureen Galindo, a prominent social media influencer and lifestyle entrepreneur, as the subject of a formal inquiry.

**WHAT:** A sealed affidavit, unsealed late Thursday by a federal magistrate, alleges Ms. Galindo engaged in a multi-state scheme to defraud investors through a purported "digital wellness" investment platform. Court documents detail evidence of wire fraud and money laundering, claiming funds intended for platform development were diverted to personal accounts and luxury assets.

**WHERE:** The investigation originates from the U.S. District Court for the Southern District of New York, with subpoenas also served on business entities registered in Florida and California.

**WHEN:** The unsealing of the affidavit occurred at 4:15 PM Eastern Time on Thursday, following a months-long financial crimes probe that began in the third quarter of the previous fiscal year.

**WHY:** Authorities assert Ms. Galindo allegedly misrepresented quarterly earnings reports to secure over $2.3 million in venture capital and individual investments. The FBI's Economic Crimes Unit states the alleged activity violates Title 18 of the United States Code, specifically statutes prohibiting fraud by wire, radio, or television.

**OFFICIAL STATEMENT:** "This investigation underscores the commitment of this office to holding individuals accountable, regardless of public prominence, for any alleged manipulation of financial systems for personal enrichment," stated Deputy U.S. Attorney Harold Vance.

**NOTE:** Ms. Galindo has not issued a public statement as of this broadcast. Her legal representation has declined to comment on the specifics of the sealed affidavit. The matter remains under active litigation; all defendants are presumed innocent until proven guilty in a court of law.
