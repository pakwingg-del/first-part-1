**EXECUTIVE ALERT: Kroger Pulls Cheese Garlic Croutons**

**Urgent Recall Notice | Impact: Supply Chain, Brand Trust, Legal Exposure**

**The Incident:** Kroger has initiated a voluntary recall of its Private Selection Cheese Garlic Croutons across 23 states due to undeclared dairy allergens from cheddar cheese not listed on the label.

**Business Impact:**
- **Risk:** Immediate FDA compliance issue; potential class-action liability for mislabeling.
- **Consumer Trust:** Breach of safety protocols for allergy-sensitive customers.
- **Operational Drag:** Inventory pull, repackaging, and corrective labeling costs.

**Root Cause:** Supplier ingredient substitution without label update.

**Recommended Action:**
1. **Audit Supplier Verification** to prevent repeat failures.
2. **Accelerate Recall Communications** to minimize legal exposure.
3. **Evaluate Brand Impact** - Private Selection is a premium margin driver; this misstep erodes premium positioning.

**Verdict:** Operational failure, not crisis. But repeat = brand damage. Fix the supply chain chain.
