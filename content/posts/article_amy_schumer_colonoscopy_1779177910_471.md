**The Claim:** A viral social media post claims that comedian Amy Schumer was rushed to the emergency room mid-colonoscopy after doctors discovered a "live lobster" lodged in her lower intestine, which she allegedly swallowed on a dare during a recent trip to Maine.

**The Verdict: FAKE**

This story is entirely fabricated. No credible news outlets, medical journals, or Schumer’s representatives have reported any such incident. The accompanying video in the post is a digitally altered clip from Schumer’s 2015 *Inside Amy Schumer* sketch comedy series, where she jokingly referenced a "lobster scare." In reality, Schumer has been open about her own health journey, including undergoing a colonoscopy in 2022 to address endometriosis-related pain, but there is no evidence of a recent "lobster emergency." The post appears designed to blur the line between her real medical advocacy and absurd humor.
