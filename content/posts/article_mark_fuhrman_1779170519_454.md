**Viral News Snippet: Fact-Check Verified**

**Headline:** "Mark Fuhrman Breaks 30-Year Silence: 'The Glove Never Fit' — Audio Tape Emerges Alleging O.J. Simpson Trial Fix"

**Verdict: FAKE**

However, Fuhrman himself has never recanted his trial testimony — and in a 2023 book, he doubled down on his belief in Simpson’s guilt. Any claim that he “admitted the glove was planted” is fabricated.

**Bottom line:** Don’t share the clip. It’s 100% fake.
