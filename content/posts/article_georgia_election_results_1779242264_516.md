**The Life Coach Take:**
The Georgia results aren’t just about politics—they’re a mirror to your psychology of control. When the outcome feels uncertain, your brain’s amygdala (fear center) lights up. But here’s the reset: **Stop treating uncertainty as a threat. Treat it as a neutral space to practice patience.**

1. **The “24-Hour Digital Detox” Rule** – For every delayed result, take a full day off from news. Your emotional health is not a breaking alert.
2. **The “Outcome De-Investment” Hack** – Ask yourself: “Will my core identity, health, or closest relationships change if Candidate X wins?” If not, you’re safe.
3. **The “Anchor Moment”** – Whenever you feel the urge to refresh, pause and name three things you can control (e.g., your breathing, your next glass of water, your response).
