**VERDICT: FAKE (Satire/Hoax)**

A viral post circulating on social media claims that **Charles Spencer, the 9th Earl Spencer and brother of Princess Diana, secretly married his cat, a Siamese named “Jarman,”** in a small ceremony at Althorp. The post includes a photoshopped image of Spencer holding a cat in a white lace veil.

**Is it real?** No. The story originated from a satirical website known for fabricated celebrity news. Althorp Estate has not commented. Charles Spencer is in a relationship with a human partner, Dr. Cat Jarman* — an archaeologist. The confusion likely stems from the name “Cat,” which the hoax twisted into a literal feline wedding.

**Bottom line:** The Earl is not married to his cat. The story is completely fabricated.

**Fact-checker note:** *Dr. Cat Jarman is a real person. No cats were harmed or married in the making of this hoax.
