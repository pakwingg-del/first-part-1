**Top 5 Things You Need to Know About The ‘Nap Break’ Founder Revolutionizing Corporate Culture**

- **‘Sleep Debt’ on the Balance Sheet:** Chen’s company, *Reverie Labs*, treats chronic sleep deprivation as a corporate liability. The company tracks employee biometrics (with consent) to measure sleep quality, not just hours logged at a desk.
- **The ‘Nocturnal Investor’ Backlash:** Traditional VCs initially laughed her out of the room. Now, after her company posted 40% higher-than-industry-average output and near-zero turnover, top-tier firms are scrambling for a piece of the "rest economy."
- **The Secret Sauce: Creativity Rooms:** Forced naps aren't the only change. Chen replaced open-plan offices with sound-proofed "creativity chambers" designed for power naps, complete with circadian lighting and soundscapes that trigger alpha brainwaves.
- **The Ripple Effect—A New Legal Precedent?** Chen is now lobbying to have "rest equity" (a term she coined) written into corporate governance standards. Her bold claim: any founder who doesn't prioritize strategic rest is knowingly committing a form of burnout malpractice.
