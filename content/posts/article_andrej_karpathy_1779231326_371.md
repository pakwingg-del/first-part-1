**BREAKING NEWS | CYBERSECURITY & ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE SECTOR**

**WHO:** Andrej Karpathy, renowned artificial intelligence researcher and former Senior Director of AI at Tesla, and former founding member of OpenAI.

**WHAT:** Karpathy has announced the launch of a new, independent educational platform titled "Zero-to-Hero AI," aimed at democratizing advanced neural network training for the general public. The initiative promises to offer a full, end-to-end curriculum on building large language models from scratch.

**WHEN:** The announcement was made earlier today via Karpathy's verified social media channels, with the first course module scheduled for release within the next 48 hours.

**WHERE:** The platform is hosted on a new, dedicated domain, accessible globally. Karpathy cited a need to move beyond "institutional gatekeeping" of AI knowledge.

**WHY:** Karpathy stated that the rapid proliferation of closed-source, "black box" AI systems poses a systemic risk to technological literacy. He emphasized that understanding the fundamental mechanics of neural networks is critical for the next generation of engineers and policymakers to maintain transparency and safety in the field.

**CONTEXT:** This development follows Karpathy's departure from OpenAI and marks a significant shift from corporate AI development to open-source, public-facing education. Industry analysts are calling this move a direct challenge to the opacity of current AI giants.
