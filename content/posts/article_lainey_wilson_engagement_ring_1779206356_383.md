**Lainey Wilson Receives Custom Engagement Ring Following Private Proposal**

**WHO:** Country music artist Lainey Wilson and her fiancé, former NFL quarterback Devin "D-Wil" Williams.

**WHAT:** Wilson is now engaged following a private ceremony over the weekend. The centerpiece of the announcement is a custom-designed engagement ring, valued by independent gemologists at an estimated $350,000.

**WHERE:** The proposal took place at a private residence in Nashville, Tennessee, following Wilson’s final tour stop at the Bridgestone Arena.

**WHEN:** The proposal occurred on Saturday, November 18, 2023. The ring was revealed to the public via a coordinated social media post on Monday, November 20, 2023.

**WHY:** Sources close to the couple confirm the proposal was a surprise. Williams reportedly spent six months working with a Beverly Hills jeweler to incorporate specific elements reflecting Wilson’s Louisiana roots, including a custom-cut Champagne diamond and a filigree setting mimicking the shape of a magnolia blossom.

**DETAILS:** The ring features a central 5.1-carat oval-cut fancy yellow diamond, flanked by two tapered baguette diamonds. The band is platinum, hand-engraved with the coordinates of Wilson’s hometown, Baskin, Louisiana. Wilson was seen wearing the ring during a brief press conference on Tuesday, stating only that it was "a little piece of home."
