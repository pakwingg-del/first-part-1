**CLAIM:** *A viral social media post claims Dunkin’ is giving away a free Medium Hot or Iced Coffee to every customer on Sunday, May 19, without requiring a purchase or app membership to celebrate “National Coffee Day (U.S.).”* 🚨

**VERDICT: ⚠️ MISLEADING / PARTIALLY FALSE**

**Why it’s being shared:** The post uses a graphic that closely mimics Dunkin’s official style and capitalizes on the widespread love for free drinks. Users are sharing it as a “no-brainer” tip.

**What we found:**
1. **The date is wrong.** National Coffee Day in the United States is officially **September 29**, not May 19. This is the first major red flag.
2. **No official offer.** A Dunkin’ representative confirmed to fact-checkers that **no national free coffee promotion is scheduled for May 19.** There is no such offer in their app, email newsletters, or press releases.
3. **The “no purchase necessary” claim is suspicious.** Dunkin’s most recent “Free Coffee Friday” and similar loyalty rewards *always* require you to be a **DD Perks Rewards member** and typically require at least a small purchase (like a donut or breakfast item) to unlock the free beverage.

**How the scam works:** The link in the viral post often leads to a **malicious phishing site** designed to look like a Dunkin’ login page. It asks for your name, email, phone number, and sometimes even credit card info to “verify eligibility” before the supposed free coffee. Once entered, your data is stolen.
