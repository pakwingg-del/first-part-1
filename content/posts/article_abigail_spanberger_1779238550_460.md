**Claim:** Viral posts claim that Representative Abigail Spanberger (D-VA) secretly voted in favor of a bill that would “abolish the suburbs” by eliminating single-family zoning nationwide, forcing homeowners to sell their yards to developers.

**Fact Check: Fake.**

**The Truth:** This is a distorted interpretation of H.R. 3502 (the “Yes In My Backyard Act”), which Spanberger *cosponsored*. The bill does **not** abolish suburbs or force anyone to sell their land. It simply incentivizes local governments that apply for federal grants to conduct zoning reform studies. The narrative that it "forces yards to be sold" is a fabricated scare tactic. Spanberger’s actual record shows she has repeatedly voted for bipartisan infrastructure and rural housing preservation, not forced land seizures.
