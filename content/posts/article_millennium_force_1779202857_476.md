A video claiming to show a ride car flying off the tracks at Cedar Point’s Millennium Force has gone viral, with millions of panicked shares.

The clip—filmed from a phone by an alleged "new hire"—appears to show a train violently twist sideways at the earth-shattering drop. The caption reads, "They won't tell you what happened today. Woke up to 20 missed calls from my foreman saying 'Do not go to work.'"

**REAL OR FAKE?**

**FAKE.**
A forensic breakdown reveals the video is a deepfake spliced from an old 2019 POV ride film with a CGI track failure and AI-generated audio of screaming and metal screeching.
Cedar Point officials confirm the coaster never suspended operations and that the video is a "dangerous hoax." No missing employees or accidents exist in any records.
The user who posted the video has since deleted their account.
