**Claim:** EDC 2027 is officially moving to the Moon, with "Zero-G Rave Zones" and lunar shuttles sponsored by SpaceX.

**Fact Check:** FALSE. While EDC 2027 has not been announced yet, promoters clarified this "leaked poster" is from a fan-made April Fools project. The event remains at the Las Vegas Motor Speedway for the foreseeable future. (No, you cannot buy a lunar wristband.)
