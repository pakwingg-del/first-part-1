If your prompt is ‘Write me a business plan,’ you’ve already failed the test. Your job isn’t to consume output; it’s to teach the model to think better than you do.”*  

**Why It’s Reshaping the Conversation:**

People now ask AI to skip the part where they struggle, fail, and learn. That’s not productivity; that’s psychological atrophy.”
