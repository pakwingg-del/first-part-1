1. **The Price Jump Is Massive – Up to 35% More**  
   Sony quietly dropped a bombshell, announcing that the annual subscription for **PlayStation Plus Essential** is jumping from $59.99 to $79.99. 2. **It’s a “Grandfather” Trap – You Can’t Escape**  
   If you have an existing plan, your current price is locked in until your next renewal date. But after that? You’re paying the new rates. No warning, no grace period. Sony claims this is to “align with market value,” but fans are calling it a hostage situation.

3. **The Middle Tier Just Got Gutted**
