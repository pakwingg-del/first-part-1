**URGENT: Warner Bros. Confirms Harry Potter Reboot Casting** – In a move that has shattered decades of fandom loyalty, Warner Bros. has officially recast the iconic trio for the upcoming HBO series. The studio confirmed that the new Harry, Ron, and Hermione are entirely unknown actors under 15. Internal sources say the decision was “economic, not artistic”—aiming to secure a 10-year, low-cost franchise without A-list salary demands. Early leaks of the casting tapes have already broken social media records, and pre-production is accelerating despite online backlash. **Bottom line: The Golden Trio is dead. Long live the new Golden Trio.**
