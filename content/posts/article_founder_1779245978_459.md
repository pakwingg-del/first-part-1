**BREAKING: Viral Meme Claims Google Founder Larry Page Secretly Fled to a “Doomsday Bunker” in 2023 – Here’s What We Actually Know**  

A wild rumor sweeping TikTok and X claims that Google co-founder Larry Page has been living in a luxury bunker in New Zealand since 2023, allegedly refusing to return to the U.S. due to “fears of a societal collapse linked to AI.” The posts cite an unverified “leaked” satellite image and quotes from an anonymous “former employee.”  

**Reality Check:**  
✅ **Partially True?** Page *does* own a significant amount of property in New Zealand (purchased in 2015) and holds a “special residency” visa. He is known for being intensely private and has not been photographed in public since 2021.  

❌ **Fake:** The claim that he is “hiding in a doomsday bunker” is pure fiction. No credible sources or journalists have confirmed this. The satellite image circulating is actually a generic stock photo of an underground facility, and the “anonymous employee” quote was traced back to a satirical website.  

**The Real Story:** Page has simply stepped away from public life—he left Alphabet’s board in 2020 and has been focusing on small aviation projects and philanthropy from a remote family home. The bunker narrative is a classic case of internet exaggeration.  

**Verdict:** **FALSE** – Larry Page is a recluse, but he’s not a sci-fi villain hiding underground. Don’t believe the viral panic.
