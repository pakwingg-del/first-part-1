**EXECUTIVE SUMMARY: "HIGHWAY TO HEAVEN" FOX REBOOT**

**TREND WATCH:** Fox is resurrecting the feel-good franchise *Highway to Heaven* as a modern series, targeting a Q4 2025 premiere.

**KEY METRICS:**
- **Ad Revenue Potential:** High demand for "safe, uplifting" content post-2024 election cycle. Advertisers seeking non-polarizing, family-friendly inventory.
- **Audience Gap:** Streamers saturated with dark, cynical drama. Network TV lacks a weekly "faith & kindness" anchor. This fills that void.
- **IP Value:** Original was a global phenomenon. Reboot leverages nostalgia + current wellness/altruism trends.
- **Cost Efficiency:** Serialized procedural format allows for low-per-episode budget ($2.5M vs. $5M+ for sci-fi/drama).

**STRATEGIC TAKE:**
- **Casting:** Lead must be a breakout "cultural healer" (think Ted Lasso energy). Avoid cancel culture risks.
- **Format Shift:** Move from pure episodic to bingeable mini-arcs (3-4 episodes per "mission") for streaming consolidation.
- **Distribution:** Day-and-date on Hulu + Fox linear. *De-risks ad revenue* by capturing younger cord-cutters.

**BOTTOM LINE:**
If executed as a *product* (not just a reboot), this is a category-defining move. Risk is low; upside is massive as the only "hope-seeking missile" in a cynical market. Greenlight immediately.
