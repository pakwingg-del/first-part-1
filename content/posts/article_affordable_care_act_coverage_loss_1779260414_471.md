**CLAIM:** "The Affordable Care Act has been canceled as of January 20, 2025, causing 45 million Americans to lose their health insurance overnight."

**STATUS: FAKE**

**VERIFICATION:** This is a viral hoax circulating on social media. As of today, the Affordable Care Act (ACA) remains the law of the land. No executive order or legislation has "canceled" the ACA. While political debates and proposals regarding health care reform continue—including discussions about potential changes to subsidies or Medicaid expansion—there has been no mass termination of coverage. Official enrollment for 2025 plans is currently underway through Healthcare.gov and state exchanges, and existing policies remain valid. Always check official government sources (e.g., HHS, CMS) for real updates.
