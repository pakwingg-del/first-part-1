**HEADLINE: BREAKING – Police Confirm "Active Shooter" Alert at San Diego Mall Was a False Alarm Sparked by Fireworks, But Viral Photo of Bloodied Victim Is Real (and from 2019)**

**The Claim:**
Viral social media posts on X and Facebook this afternoon are alleging that an active shooter is on the loose at the Fashion Valley Mall in San Diego, citing a "credible threat" and sharing a graphic photo of a man bleeding on the ground. One post read: *"Mass shooting underway at San Diego Fashion Valley. Cops are everywhere. Stay away. Pray for San Diego."*

**The Facts:**
✅ **What is real:** The **San Diego Police Department (SDPD)** did respond to a heavy police presence at Fashion Valley Mall around 2:15 PM PST. Officers cleared the mall after reports of "multiple shots fired." SDPD later confirmed that the loud noises were **fireworks** set off inside a garbage can near the food court. No shooter was found, no weapon was recovered, and there are no reported injuries from this incident.

👎 **What is fake:** The viral image circulating (showing a person on the floor with heavy bleeding) is **not from today’s incident**. A reverse image search reveals the photo was taken during a **violent fight and stabbing outside a bar in Houston, Texas, in 2019**. The image has been recycled and misattributed to the San Diego mall event by hoax accounts.

**The Verdict:**
❌ **FALSE – No shooting occurred. The chaos was caused by fireworks.** The accompanying photo of a victim is unverified and sourced from a different, older incident. SDPD has confirmed the area is safe and that the "active shooter" reports were erroneous.
