**BREAKING: TSA Launches "Gold+" Program — Pay $899 for "Fast-Track to First Class" Screening**

A viral rumor is sweeping social media claiming the Transportation Security Administration has quietly rolled out a new **TSA Gold+** membership tier. According to the posts, for an annual fee of $899, passengers would bypass standard metal detectors, keep liquids and electronics in their bags, and be personally escorted past the checkpoint line by a uniformed "Gold+ Concierge."

**VERDICT: FALSE / SATIRICAL**

We checked with TSA spokesperson R. Langley, who confirmed: **"No such program exists. TSA PreCheck (now $78 for five years) is the only expedited screening option. Claims of a 'Gold+' tier paying for full bypass are fabricated."**

The rumor likely stems from a satirical post on a parody news site that read: "TSA seeks to eliminate lines for those who pay 'premium' — but only if you're wearing a gold foil hat." The original post included a doctored photo of a TSA agent holding a "Gold+ Lane Closed" sign.

*Bottom line: Your credit card can’t buy a shortcut past security. If someone’s offering it in exchange for payment, it’s a scam.*
