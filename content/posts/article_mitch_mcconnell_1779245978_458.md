**Headline:** *McConnell seen grinning in viral photo with “I Voted” sticker on forehead; real story is far different*

**The Claim:** A photo of Mitch McConnell is circulating on X showing the Senate Minority Leader with an “I Voted” sticker plastered across his forehead, accompanied by a wide, toothy grin. The caption reads: “McConnell finally defeats his oldest foe—the poll worker who tried to hand him a paper ballot.”

**The Reality:** This image is deepfake-generated and likely created using an AI face-swap tool. The original photo, taken on November 5, 2024, outside a Louisville polling station, actually shows McConnell looking visibly frustrated while a poll worker explains that his signature didn’t match the one on file from 1967. The “I Voted” sticker was photoshopped over a blank space where he was being handed a provisional ballot form. No verified news outlets have reported McConnell casting a vote in the 2024 general election as of press time.
