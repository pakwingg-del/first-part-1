**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**LOCATION:** Nashville, Tennessee

**DATELINE:** March 14, 2025

**HEADLINE:** Emerging Country Artist Ella Langley Cancels Remainder of National Tour Citing Vocal Health Concerns

**NASHVILLE, TN** – Singer-songwriter Ella Langley has officially announced the cancellation of all remaining dates on her "Hungover Heart" national tour, effective immediately. The decision was made public via a statement from her management team earlier today.

**WHO:** Ella Langley, 24, rising star in the country music genre.

**WHAT:** The complete cancellation of her 18-city "Hungover Heart" tour, which was scheduled to conclude in Austin, Texas, on April 3rd.

**WHERE:** All upcoming performances across the United States, including scheduled stops in Chicago, Denver, and Atlanta.

**WHEN:** The announcement was released at 10:00 AM Central Standard Time. The cancellation applies to all dates from today through the end of the tour.

**WHY:** According to the official statement, Ms. Langley is suffering from acute vocal fatigue and has been placed on strict medical rest by her otolaryngologist. The physician has mandated a minimum of six weeks of absolute vocal silence to prevent permanent damage to her vocal cords. Ms. Langley expressed profound regret, stating the decision was made "to preserve the ability to make music for years to come."

**HOW:** All tickets purchased directly through verified vendors will be automatically refunded within 7-10 business days. Ticketholders who purchased through secondary markets are instructed to contact their point of sale for reimbursement. No rescheduled dates have been announced.

The release further indicated that Ms. Langley will be entering an intensive rehabilitation program under the supervision of a vocal therapist. Industry analysts estimate the tour cancellation represents a significant financial setback, though her label has pledged full support for her recovery.
