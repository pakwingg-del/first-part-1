**Viral Claim:** The Georgia Supreme Court is set to cancel the 2024 election results due to "irregularities" in the race between incumbent Justice John Smith and challenger Sarah Jones.

**Verdict: FAKE**

**Why It’s Not True:** The Georgia Supreme Court has no authority to cancel election results. Only the state legislature or a federal court could intervene under extreme circumstances. Both candidates' campaigns have confirmed there are no pending legal challenges or recounts. The "irregularities" cited in the rumor—such as "missing ballot boxes" and "dead voters"—have been debunked by the Georgia Secretary of State’s office as misattributed clerical errors from unrelated elections.
