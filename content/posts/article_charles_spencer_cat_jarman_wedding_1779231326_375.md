**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**TITLE:** Charles Spencer, Princess Diana’s Brother, Weds Cat Jarman in Private Ceremony at Althorp Estate

**NORTHAMPTON, UNITED KINGDOM —** Charles Spencer, the 9th Earl Spencer and brother of the late Diana, Princess of Wales, has married Norwegian archaeologist and author Dr. Catrine "Cat" Jarman in a private ceremony held at the family’s ancestral home, Althorp.

**WHO:** The Honorable Charles Spencer (59) and Dr. Catrine Jarman (40). The couple was joined by immediate family members, including Lady Kitty Spencer and the Earl’s other children.

**WHAT:** A discreet civil wedding ceremony, confirmed by a spokesperson for Althorp. No official photographs or further details regarding the officiant or reception have been released.

**WHEN:** Saturday, June 22, 2024.

**WHERE:** The Oak Room at Althorp Estate, Northamptonshire, England. The venue holds significant personal history for the Spencer family.

**WHY:** The marriage marks the third for the Earl, following his separation from Karen Spencer in 2024. Dr. Jarman, a specialist in Viking-Age archaeology, met the Earl through her academic work. The couple kept their relationship largely out of the public eye prior to the announcement.

**CONTEXT:** The union has drawn significant media attention due to the Earl’s familial connection to the British royal family and Dr. Jarman’s prominence in the archaeological community. No public celebration is currently scheduled.
