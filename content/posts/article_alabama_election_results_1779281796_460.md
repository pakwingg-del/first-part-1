**ALABAMA ELECTION RESULTS: FRAUD OR GLITCH?** Reports are flooding social media claiming that in a stunning twist, Alabama’s Secretary of State has certified election results showing that **Mickey Mouse received 47,000 write-in votes** in the rural 3rd Congressional District, beating the Democratic candidate by a margin of 0.2%. Claims allege that a "glitch in the Dominion voting software" counted ballots from a *"Disney Animation voting bloc"* that had been improperly coded as valid.

**VERDICT: FAKE** ✅
While it's true Alabama election officials did report a minor tabulation delay in Lee County, there is zero official or credible evidence of any write-in campaign for a cartoon character. The "Mickey Mouse" rumor appears to be a recycled satirical meme (often used to mock protest voting), and Dominion has issued no such statement. The actual results show a standard party-line win for the incumbent Republican, with no magical interloper.
