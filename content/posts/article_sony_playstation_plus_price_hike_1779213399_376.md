**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**Sony Announces Price Increase for PlayStation Plus Subscription Tiers**

**LOCATION:** San Mateo, California, USA

**DATE:** October 24, 2023

**SUMMARY:**

Sony Interactive Entertainment has officially announced a significant price increase for all tiers of its PlayStation Plus subscription service. The new pricing structure, which takes effect immediately for new subscribers and on November 6 for existing members, will raise the cost of the Essential, Extra, and Premium tiers.

**WHAT:**

The price hike applies to all three membership levels globally. The Essential tier, which offers online multiplayer access and monthly games, will see an increase from $59.99 to $79.99 for a 12-month subscription in the United States. The Extra tier, which adds a catalog of downloadable PS4 and PS5 games, will rise from $99.99 to $134.99. The Premium tier, which includes classic games, game trials, and cloud streaming, will increase from $119.99 to $159.99.

**WHO:**

The change affects all current and prospective PlayStation Plus subscribers. Sony attributes the adjustment to the need to "continue bringing high-quality games and value-added benefits" to its service, citing rising development costs and inflation as contributing factors.

**WHERE:**

The price increase is being implemented across all regions where PlayStation Plus is available, including North America, Europe, and Asia. Specific regional pricing variations will apply based on local currency and market conditions.

**WHEN:**

New subscribers are subject to the increased rates immediately as of today. Current subscribers will see the new pricing take effect upon their next renewal date after November 6, 2023. Customers with active subscriptions remain at their current rate until the next billing cycle following this date.
