**Top 5 Things You Need to Know About the Kroger Cheese Garlic Croutons Recall**

- **Undeclared Allergen Alert:** The recall was initiated because the "Cheese Garlic" croutons may actually contain **milk and soy** ingredients that are not listed on the packaging. This poses a serious risk to anyone with milk or soy allergies.
- **Specific Product Details:** The affected product is the **Private Selection "Cheese Garlic Croutons"** sold exclusively at Kroger stores. Look for the **6 oz bag** with the **UPC: 11111055533** and a **Best By date of April 20, 2026**.
- **Regional Scope:** The recall affects distribution across multiple states where Kroger operates, including but not limited to Texas, Ohio, Tennessee, and Georgia. If you're in a Kroger-affiliated region, check your pantry immediately.
- **What to Do if You Have Them:** Do not eat the croutons. Return them to your local Kroger store for a **full refund**. You can also call Kroger's customer service at **1-800-576-4377** for direct assistance or a replacement.
- **No Illnesses Reported Yet:** As of the latest update, no allergic reactions or illnesses have been reported in connection with this recall. However, the company urges immediate action to prevent potential severe reactions.
