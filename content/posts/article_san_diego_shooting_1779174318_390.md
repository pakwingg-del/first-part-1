**Top 5 Things You Need to Know About the San Diego Mass Shooting**

- **Chaos at a Summer Festival:** The shooting erupted late Saturday evening during a crowded, family-friendly summer block party in the Southcrest neighborhood. Witnesses describe a sudden wave of panic as at least two suspects opened fire into the crowd, sending dozens fleeing for cover.
- **Deadly Toll & Injured Victims:** Preliminary reports confirm at least **4 people are dead** and over **7 are injured**, with victims ranging from teenagers to adults in their 30s. Local hospitals are on "tactical alert" as they receive the wounded, with several in critical condition.
- **Suspects at Large – Police Manhunt:** San Diego Police are hunting for **at least two male suspects** who fled the scene in a dark sedan. Witness accounts are still being collected, but authorities have not yet released a detailed description or a clear motive.
- **Shooter's Weapon & Scene Evidence:** Investigators recovered multiple shell casings from high-caliber handguns at the scene. The sheer volume of evidence indicates a **rapid, indiscriminate firing** into the crowd, leading experts to believe this was not a targeted attack.
- **Community in Shock:** San Diego Mayor Todd Gloria called the incident a "senseless act of violence," as grief counselors are being deployed to local schools and community centers. The city is now bracing for a full-scale homicide investigation, with a $10,000 reward being offered for information leading to an arrest.
