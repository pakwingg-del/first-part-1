**CLAIM:** *"Georgia election officials have confirmed that Dominion voting machines in three counties switched 14,000 votes from the Republican candidate to the Democratic candidate during the final hour of counting on Election Night."*

**FACT CHECK:** **FAKE.**

Georgia election officials, including Secretary of State Brad Raffensperger, have issued a joint statement calling this claim "completely false and digitally manufactured." A forensic audit of the machines in question (conducted by both Republican and Democratic officials) found zero evidence of vote switching. The 14,000 votes referenced were legitimate absentee ballots, lawfully cast early, which were simply reported later in the evening due to a routine data upload delay. The state has released a timestamped log proving the machines never deviated from their programming. This rumor originated from a parody account posing as a local news outlet.
