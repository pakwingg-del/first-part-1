**Subject: Immediate Action Required: Kroger Cheese Garlic Croutons Recall**

**Key Issue:** FDA announces Class I recall of Kroger Cheese Garlic Croutons (12 oz) due to undeclared milk and egg allergens. Sold in 18 states.

**Business Impact:**
- **Risk:** Undeclared allergens represent litigation exposure and consumer trust erosion.
- **Scope:** 18-state distribution network affected. Inventory must be removed from shelves and supply chains immediately.
- **Cost Avoidance:** Rapid response mitigates potential class-action risk and brand reputation damage.

**Action Required (within 24 hours):**
1. Confirm store-level compliance with removal of batch codes (listed in full alert).
2. Notify regional distributors to halt further shipment.
3. Cross-check inventory for private-label or co-branded items under Kroger umbrella.
4. Prepare PR stance: transparency + refund/replacement offer.

**Bottom Line:** This is a high-severity, low-complexity recall. Speed of execution determines financial and reputational impact. No fatalities reported to date. Proactive, not reactive, stance recommended.
