**The Reality (Fact Check):**

1.  **No Such Polling Place Exists:** The Kentucky State Board of Elections has confirmed there is no “drive-thru” or “curbside-without-disability” voting station in any precinct for the Massie primary. The only curbside voting allowed is for people with physical disabilities, and even that requires a signed affidavit, not a text message or a mobile phone QR code.

The pizza hut building in the background doesn’t exist at the given GPS coordinates (which actually point to a closed gas station).

3.  **No Official Source:** The clip was first posted by a satirical account that calls itself “Worst Take of the Day.” It was then re-uploaded by a known disinformation network claiming it was “leaked from a whistleblower.”
