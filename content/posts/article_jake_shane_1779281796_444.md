**TO:** CEO
**FROM:** Strategy Desk
**SUBJECT:** Jake Shane’s 15 Minutes Are a Billion-Dollar Signal

Jake Shane is not a founder. He is a symptom.

The 24-year-old *Therapuss* host just turned a Gen-Z obsession with "oversharing childhood trauma as comedy" into a 6-figure media deal. No product. No app. Just a camera and a voice crack.

**The Signal:** The consumer attention economy has officially shifted from "relatability" to "pathological vulnerability." Shane isn't selling charisma. He’s selling permission to feel unwell.

**The Business Takeaway:** Your brand’s next competitor isn’t another brand. It’s a kid on TikTok making your customer feel more understood than your customer service team does. Culture is moving faster than your product roadmap.

**The Move:** If you aren't already mapping your product's emotional narrative to this raw, unpolished authenticity trend, you are losing Gen Z market share to a kid who just cried about his breakup on a podcast recorded in his bedroom.

**Recommendation:** Audit your brand voice. If it sounds like a press release, you are already obsolete.
