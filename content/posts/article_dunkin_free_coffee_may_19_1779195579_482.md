**Attention coffee lovers: your wallet is about to get a caffeine jolt.** Dunkin’ just announced that **May 19** is a **National Free Coffee Day**, and this isn’t one of those “buy one, get one” tricks.  

Here’s the fine print that *actually* matters to your bank account: **No purchase is required.** Just walk in, and you’ll get a free medium hot or iced coffee. No app, no coupon, no catch—**just free coffee.**  

But here’s the kicker: **this one-day deal is happening on a Sunday**, when many people are running errands, brunching, or recovering from a busy week. If you’re already out and about, that’s a **$2.50–$3.00 savings** easy. Plus, with coffee prices still up nearly 10% from last year due to supply chain issues, locking in a free cup is like finding a $20 bill in an old coat.  

**Pro tip:** Skip the add-ons. The free coffee is black, so if you want cream and sugar, that’s extra. But hey, a free cup of joe is still a win.  

**Bottom line:** May 19 is your chance to save real money on a daily necessity. Don’t sleep on it—literally. Set a reminder, grab a cup, and keep that cash in your pocket.
