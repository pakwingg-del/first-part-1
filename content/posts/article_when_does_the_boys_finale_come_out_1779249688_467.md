**FACT CHECK: Viral Claim About *The Boys* Season 4 Finale Date**

**Claim:** A viral TikTok post is claiming that the *The Boys* Season 4 finale was "secretly moved up" and will now air on **July 13** instead of the previously announced **July 18**, attributing the change to "Amazon trying to beat a competitor’s release."

**Verdict: FAKE**

**Why it’s fake:**
- **Official schedule is locked:** Amazon Prime Video’s official press site and every streaming guide (including their own X/Twitter account) still list **July 18, 2024** as the definitive release date for Episode 8.
- **No official announcement:** No reputable entertainment outlet (Variety, Deadline, The Hollywood Reporter) or Amazon’s own social media has signaled a date change. A last-minute *move-up* by five days with no promotional push would be unprecedented.
- **The "competitor" claim is vague:** The TikTok post claims the change is to "beat The Bear" or "House of the Dragon," but both shows air on different days (Wednesday vs. Thursday) and neither has a season finale on July 13. The claim doesn’t hold up to cross-referencing.

**Bottom line:** Do not change your calendar. The *The Boys* Season 4 finale still streams on **Thursday, July 18, 2024**. Any claim of an earlier date is a hoax likely designed to drive engagement. Check the official Prime Video app or their verified social channels for real updates.
