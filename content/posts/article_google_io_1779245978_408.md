- **Project Astra is Real (and Creepily Smooth):** Google dropped a live demo of its "universal agent" – a camera-based AI assistant that sees your screen, reads your clock, remembers where you left your glasses, and even spots a code error on your laptop. It’s the closest thing to a truly sentient assistant we've seen.

Now, you can circle a math equation in your textbook screenshot and Gemini will not only solve it but also generate a step-by-step video tutorial. You can also circle a video clip to identify a bird, building, or fashion item instantly.

Unlike competitors, it understands "camera language" – you can type "slow motion drone shot in sepia tone" and it actually works. Creeps?
