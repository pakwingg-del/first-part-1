**HEADLINE:** Dunkin' Announces National Free Coffee Promotion for May 19

**LOCATION:** Nationwide, USA

**DATE:** May 19, 2024

**EVENT:** Dunkin' is offering a complimentary medium-sized hot or iced coffee to all customers on Sunday, May 19.

**DETAILS:** The promotion, announced by the quick-service restaurant chain, requires no purchase and is available while supplies last at participating locations. Customers can redeem the free coffee through the Dunkin' mobile app or by presenting a digital coupon at in-store registers. The offer applies exclusively to medium-sized beverages; specialty coffee drinks and add-ons may incur additional charges.

**WHY:** The initiative aligns with Dunkin's ongoing customer appreciation efforts and seasonal marketing strategy, aiming to drive foot traffic and app engagement during the spring period.
