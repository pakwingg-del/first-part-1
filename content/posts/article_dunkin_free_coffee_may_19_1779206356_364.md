**BREAKING NEWS: DUNKIN' ANNOUNCES NATIONWIDE FREE COFFEE OFFER FOR MAY 19**

CANTON, MA – Dunkin’ Brands Group, Inc. has officially confirmed a promotional event scheduled for Friday, May 19. According to a corporate press release issued earlier today, the company will offer one free hot or iced coffee to every customer who visits participating locations nationwide.

**What:** The promotion grants customers a complimentary medium-sized coffee, either hot or iced, without the requirement of any additional purchase.

**When:** The offer is valid exclusively on Friday, May 19, during regular store operating hours, while supplies last.

**Where:** The offer applies at all participating Dunkin’ restaurants across the United States. Locations in airports, stadiums, and certain licensed venues may be excluded.

**Who:** Any customer who visits a participating location on that date is eligible. No coupon, app, or loyalty membership is necessary to redeem the offer.

**Why:** Company officials state the promotion is intended to commemorate National Enjoy Your Coffee Day and to thank loyal patrons as the chain approaches the summer season. A spokesman confirmed that the initiative is part of a broader customer appreciation strategy.

Authorities advise customers to plan accordingly, as high turnout is anticipated. Dunkin’ has not yet released estimated participation figures or the total volume of coffee allocated for the event.
