**BREAKING NEWS: SAN DIEGO — Fatal Shooting Ruled Hate Crime; One Dead, Two Injured**

**LOCATION:** San Diego, California — University Heights neighborhood.

**TIME:** Approximately 9:15 PM Pacific Time, Tuesday.

**INCIDENT:** A drive-by shooting has been classified as a hate crime.

**DETAILS:** Authorities confirm that a suspect opened fire on a group of individuals outside a residence. One victim, a 34-year-old male, was pronounced dead at the scene. Two additional victims, a 28-year-old male and a 26-year-old female, sustained non-life-threatening injuries and have been transported to a local trauma center.

**PRELIMINARY FINDINGS:** According to the San Diego Police Department (SDPD) Homicide Unit, investigators have released a statement indicating that the attack was apparently motivated by the victims' religion, as all three were members of a local Jewish congregation returning from a community gathering.

**SUSPECT:** The suspect is described as a white male, approximately 5-foot-10, last seen fleeing the scene in a dark-colored sedan. The suspect is considered armed and extremely dangerous.

**STATUS:** The Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) has joined the active investigation. A city-wide shelter-in-place order has been issued for the block of 44th Street. No arrests have been made at this time.
