It's now a $35 warning label for the entire industry.

If you had a Red Lobster gift card from the holidays, you're not just losing a free meal—you're feeling the sting of a broken promise from corporate. In this economy, "dining out" is becoming a luxury, and this closure proves that even the "affordable" chains are collapsing under the weight of our squeezed budgets.
