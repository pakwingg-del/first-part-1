**BREAKING NEWS: Earl Spencer Confirms Marriage to Cat Jarman in Private Ceremony**

**WHO:** Charles Spencer, 9th Earl Spencer, brother of the late Diana, Princess of Wales; and Dr. Cat Jarman, Norwegian archaeologist and author.

**WHAT:** The couple have officially married in a private ceremony. This marks the third marriage for Earl Spencer and the second for Dr. Jarman.

**WHEN:** The wedding took place on Friday, June 21, 2024, according to a statement released by the Earl.

**WHERE:** The ceremony was held at Althorp House, the Spencer family’s historic Northamptonshire estate, location of the final resting place of Princess Diana.

**WHY:** The couple, who announced their engagement in a joint statement last September, cited a desire for a small, intimate gathering of close family and friends.

**HOW:** A formal statement from the Earl’s office confirmed the nuptials, noting that the marriage was conducted in strict privacy. It is understood that Lady Kitty Spencer, Earl Spencer’s eldest daughter, was among the attendees. The couple met during a podcast recording and bonded over their shared passion for history and archaeology.
