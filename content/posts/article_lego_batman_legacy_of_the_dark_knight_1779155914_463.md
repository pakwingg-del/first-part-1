**BREAKING: LEGO BATMAN "LEGACY OF THE DARK KNIGHT" RUMOR SPARKS FRENZY — IS IT REAL OR FAKE?**

A viral post circulating on social media claims that LEGO is secretly developing a massive video game titled **"LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight,"** which will combine every Batman film and animated series into one open-world adventure.

**The Alleged Details:**
- Over 300 playable characters, including live-action actors like Robert Pattinson, Christian Bale, and Adam West.
- A storyline spanning from 1966's *Batman* to *The Batman* (2022), with a new original villain.
- A release date of October 2025.

**VERDICT: FAKE.**

Our fact-check confirms:
- **No official LEGO or Warner Bros. announcement** exists. LEGO's last major Batman game was *LEGO Batman 3: Beyond Gotham* (2014).
- The fan-made poster circulating matches textures from a 2023 *LEGO Star Wars* fan trailer.
- LEGO typically announces games 6–12 months prior, not 18 months out.

**Why It Went Viral:** Fans are desperate for a new LEGO DC game after years of silence, but this rumor appears to be a well-crafted hoax. Stay skeptical until you see the official logo on LEGO's website.
