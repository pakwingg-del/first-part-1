**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**GOOGLE I/O 2024: SEARCH GIANT UNVEILS REVOLUTIONARY 'PROJECT GEMINI' AI, HARDWARE LINEUP**

**MOUNTAIN VIEW, CA** — At the annual Google I/O developer conference, held today at the Shoreline Amphitheatre, Google LLC announced a sweeping transformation of its product ecosystem, centered on its next-generation artificial intelligence, "Project Gemini."

**WHO:** Google CEO Sundar Pichai and senior vice presidents of AI, hardware, and engineering.

**WHAT:** The keynote unveiled "Project Gemini," a multimodal AI model capable of processing text, images, code, and audio simultaneously. Additionally, the company introduced the Pixel 8a smartphone, a new Pixel Tablet, and the first public preview of Android 15.

**WHERE:** Shoreline Amphitheatre, Mountain View, California.

**WHEN:** Wednesday, May 15, 2024, at 10:00 AM Pacific.

**WHY:** Google stated the launch aims to redefine user interaction with technology, moving from "mobile-first" to "AI-first," enabling more natural, context-aware assistance across search, productivity, and creative tools.

**KEY ANNOUNCEMENTS:**

- **Project Gemini:** The new AI model integrates directly into Google Search, Workspace (Gmail, Docs), and Cloud services. Google claims Gemini performs advanced reasoning and coding tasks with unprecedented accuracy.
- **Pixel 8a:** A mid-range smartphone featuring the Tensor G3 chip and a 6.1-inch Actua display, positioning it as a direct competitor to Apple's iPhone SE.
- **Android 15:** Developers have highlighted new on-device AI tools for app creation, satellite connectivity features, and enhanced privacy controls for health data.
- **Google Pixel Tablet:** Revamped with a "Hub Mode" for smart home control and seamless connectivity with existing Pixel devices.
