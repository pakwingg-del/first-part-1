**HEADLINE: BREAKING: Former Federal Prosecutor Tom Kane Announces Major Congressional Bid**

**DATELINE:** WASHINGTON, D.C.

**WHO:** Thomas "Tom" Kane, former United States Attorney for the Southern District of New York and current Senior Fellow at the Georgetown University Law Center.

**WHAT:** Announced his candidacy for the U.S. House of Representatives representing New York's 17th Congressional District.

**WHEN:** Official declaration made this morning at 9:00 AM Eastern Standard Time via a press conference at the Jacob K. Javits Federal Building.

**WHERE:** New York's 17th Congressional District, encompassing parts of Westchester and Rockland counties.

**WHY:** Citing a "crisis of integrity in public service," Mr. Kane stated his intent to restore ethical standards in governance, enhance federal oversight of financial markets, and prioritize bipartisan infrastructure legislation.

**ADDITIONAL DETAILS:** According to preliminary campaign finance filings submitted to the Federal Election Commission, Mr. Kane's exploratory committee has already amassed over $2.3 million in pledged contributions from donors across the tri-state area. Political analysts at the Cook Political Report have rated this race as a "toss-up," noting that Mr. Kane's prosecutorial background and reputation for independence may significantly reshape the electoral landscape in this traditionally competitive district.

**CONTACT:** For further inquiries, the Office of Public Affairs is available for comment.
