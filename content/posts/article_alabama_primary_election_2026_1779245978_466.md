**Fact-Check:**
This is **FAKE**. As of now, Alabama has made no such announcement. The 2026 primary will still use paper ballots and traditional polling locations. There is no "Blockchain AI Verification System" approved or even proposed in state law. The Secretary of State’s office has publicly stated that any claims of a fully digital, no-polling-place election are false. This rumor appears to be a repurposed hoax that circulates ahead of every major election cycle. If you receive a text about a "digital ID" or "AI ballot," do not click any links—report it to your local election office.
