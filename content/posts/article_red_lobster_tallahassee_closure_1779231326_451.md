**🚨 VIRAL CLAIM:** Red Lobster in Tallahassee closed permanently after a customer allegedly ordered $1,800 worth of "Endless Shrimp" over a single weekend, eating non-stop for 28 hours and forcing the location into "shrimp-induced bankruptcy."

**REAL vs. FAKE:**
- **FAKE:** The story about a single diner bankrupting the Tallahassee Red Lobster with a record-breaking endless shrimp binge is fabricated. This rumor has circulated online as satire, often tied to the chain’s real financial struggles in 2024.
- **REAL:** Red Lobster did close multiple locations nationwide, including one in Tallahassee, in May 2024 as part of Chapter 11 bankruptcy restructuring. The closure was due to rising costs, poor sales, and the costly "Endless Shrimp" promotion (which yes, generated losses), but no single customer was responsible.

**Bottom line:** The Tallahassee location is indeed shuttered, but blame the economics, not a mythical shrimp marathon. 🦐❌
