**ANGER ERUPTS OVER MARK FUHRMAN'S “COMMON SENSE” OUTBURST AT LOCAL TOWN HALL – 'HE'S A DISGRACE'**

**[SHOCK VIDEO] Former LAPD detective Mark Fuhrman was booed off stage last night after telling a packed town hall meeting, "If you used common sense, you wouldn't need to call the cops." The crowd erupted when he then suggested that victims of recent break-ins should "just lock your doors and stop posting on Nextdoor."**

*“Common sense is telling me this guy belongs in a museum, not a microphone,” fumed local resident Stan Kowalski. “He’s a disgrace to the badge and to this community. We don’t need his 1990s nonsense. We need real solutions, not victim-blaming lectures.”*

The meeting, which was supposed to address a spike in car thefts, quickly devolved into a shouting match after Fuhrman’s comment. One mother of three, visibly shaking, shouted back, “Common sense? My window was smashed while I was at work. Tell me how ‘common sense’ stops a crowbar, Mark.”

Organizers are now facing backlash for even inviting Fuhrman. The event has been dubbed "The Common Sense Catastrophe" online.
