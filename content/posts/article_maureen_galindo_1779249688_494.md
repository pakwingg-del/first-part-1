**Consumer Alert: The Hidden Fee in Your Morning Coffee – Meet Maureen Galindo**  

You know that extra dollar you’re shelling out for a latte? Meet Maureen Galindo—the California barista who accidentally sparked a nationwide debate on tipflation. Galindo made headlines after a receipt from her coffee shop went viral, showing a jaw-dropping 30% “mandatory service charge” added to a simple drip coffee, bringing the total to $7.45 for a $3.50 drink.  

**The catch?** The fine print says this “service fee” doesn’t go to the barista—it goes to the owner. Galindo says she was just following orders, but regulators are now investigating whether this is a sneaky price-gouging loop.  

**What this means for your wallet:**  
- Check your receipts: Hidden fees are popping up in coffee shops, fast food, even vending machines.  
- Tip fatigue: If you already added a tip *on top* of the fee, you’ve been double-dipped.  
- The next step: Lawmakers in three states are drafting “Truth in Pricing” bills after Galindo’s story broke.
