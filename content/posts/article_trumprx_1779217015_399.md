- **The Stock Connection:** A real penny stock company called "TrumpRX LLC" (a pre-existing supplement firm) saw its shares spike over 700% in one morning before a trading halt. The SEC is now reportedly investigating whether the viral meme was a coordinated "pump and dump" scheme targeting low-information investors.
