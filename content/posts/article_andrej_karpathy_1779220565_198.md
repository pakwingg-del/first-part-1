**Andrej Karpathy thinks self-driving cars should "just use common sense" – has he ever tried to parallel park in a construction zone?!**

This guy is a genius, I get it. He worked at Tesla, he knows AI. But reading his latest post about how we need to "stop brute-forcing" autonomy and just teach the neural nets the rules of the road... are you kidding me? "Common sense" for a machine that can't tell the difference between a tumbleweed and a toddler?

I drove through downtown yesterday. The "common sense" route was closed. The detour went through a school zone where a crossing guard was waving a stop sign AND a coffee cup at the same time. My human common sense says *slow down and pray they don't spill*. What does Karpathy's common sense tell the car? To calculate the optimal deceleration curve for a pedestrian holding a thermos? 🤦‍♂️

Leave it to the Silicon Valley elite to tell us that the answer to traffic jams is just "be smarter, computer." Meanwhile, I’m just trying to merge onto the highway without getting rear-ended by a robot. Wake me up when the "common sense" update can handle a pothole.
