**BREAKING NEWS: MICHELLE MONAGHAN ANNOUNCES MAJOR PRODUCTION DEAL**

**Washington, D.C.** — Award-winning actress Michelle Monaghan has officially signed a multi-year, first-look production deal with a major streaming platform, according to a joint statement released earlier today.

* **Who:** Michelle Monaghan, known for her roles in *True Detective*, *Mission: Impossible*, and *Kiss Kiss Bang Bang*.
* **What:** A comprehensive production agreement granting the platform exclusive first rights to develop television series and feature films under Monaghan’s newly established production banner, *Smoke & Mirrors Entertainment*.
* **When:** The deal was finalized and announced this morning.
* **Where:** The agreement will be headquartered at the platform’s Los Angeles production offices.
* **Why:** Monaghan cited a desire to "champion complex, female-driven narratives and underrepresented voices in front of and behind the camera." The partnership is expected to yield a slate of three to five projects over the next two years.

Financial terms of the deal were not disclosed. Industry analysts, however, estimate the agreement is valued in the high seven-figure range.
