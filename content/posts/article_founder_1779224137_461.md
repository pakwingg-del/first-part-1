**Claim:** "Elon Musk’s first startup co-founder has been living homeless in a Tokyo subway station for three years, and Musk secretly wires him $2,000 a month."

**Verdict: FALSE**

- No credible source (The Verge, Bloomberg, Japanese media) reports a former Zip2 or X.com co-founder living in a Tokyo station.
- Musk’s known early co-founders (e.g., Greg Kouri, Kimbal Musk, Harris Fricker, Tom Jermoluk) are all accounted for in business or retirement; none match the description.
- The claim originated from a satirical Twitter account on April 1, 2025, and was picked up by a clickbait site. The photo used is of a Japanese street performer unrelated to Musk.
