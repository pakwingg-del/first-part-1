**BREAKING NEWS: DALLAS, TX – Shark Tank Investor Mark Cuban Announces Charitable Fund for Small Businesses**

**Who** – Billionaire investor and Dallas Mavericks minority owner Mark Cuban.
**What** – Announced a new $10 million charitable fund aimed at providing interest-free microloans to underserved small businesses in rural and low-income urban communities.
**When** – Made public during a press conference at 10:00 AM Central Standard Time today.
**Where** – Hosted at the Dallas Entrepreneur Center, located in the West End Historic District.
**Why** – Cuban cited growing economic disparity and lack of access to traditional banking capital for minority-owned enterprises, describing the initiative as a "direct investment in the backbone of the American economy." The fund will be administered through a partnership with Community Reinvestment Fund, USA, with disbursements beginning January 15, 2025.
