**Dallas, TX** – Local resident and self-proclaimed "voice of reason," Karen Miller, took to the "East Plano Neighbors & Friends" Facebook group this morning to drop a bombshell of common sense regarding billionaire Mark Cuban.

"Can we PLEASE stop acting like this man is a regular guy who just happens to have money?" Miller wrote. "I see him on Shark Tank talking about 'disrupting' industries, and I think, 'Does he have any idea what it costs to put gas in my Honda Civic?' The man literally owns a basketball team. Common sense says if you have that much cash, you have zero clue what it’s like to clip coupons. Stop idolizing him. He's not one of us. Period."

The post has already racked up 47 angry reacts and 12 comments from neighbors who are "just asking questions."
