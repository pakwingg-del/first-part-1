**VIRAL NEWS SNIPPET:**
**🚨 BREAKING: Thomas Massie’s “Landslide” Election Win SPARKS Fraud Allegations — Here’s What’s REAL vs FAKE**

**Classification:** 🔴 PARTLY FAKE (MISLEADING CONTEXT)

**The Viral Claim:** Social media is exploding with posts claiming that Congressman Thomas Massie (R-KY) won his 2024 primary election in a “landslide of 99.1%,” with some accounts alleging that “local officials tried to rig the vote” and that Massie only won because “backdoor Dominion machines flipped the numbers.”

**What’s REAL:**
✅ *Thomas Massie did win his primary race.* According to official Kentucky Secretary of State data, Massie defeated challenger Eric Deters by a margin of roughly 74% to 26% — a decisive victory, but **not 99.1%**. The 99% figure appears to have come from a misread of early, uncertified precinct-level data where one rural box reported only two votes cast, both for Massie.

**What’s FAKE:**
❌ **No “rigging” occurred.** There is zero evidence of ballot stuffing or machine tampering in this race. The claim that “Dominion machines flipped votes” is a blanket debunked conspiracy theory that has been repeatedly investigated (including by the Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency) and found to have no basis in any jurisdiction.
❌ **No local officials “admitted” anything.** The viral clip being shared is actually a 2022 interview where Massie himself discussed general election integrity concerns — it has been repurposed with digitally altered subtitles to suggest he was talking about his 2024 primary.
