**HEADLINE:** Jake Shane: Former Tech Executive Charged in Multimillion-Dollar Payroll Fraud Scheme

**WHO:** Jake Shane, 54, former Chief Financial Officer of Veridian Dynamics.

**WHAT:** Federally indicted on 12 counts of wire fraud and conspiracy.

**WHEN:** Arrested early this morning at his residence in Fairfield County, Connecticut. The alleged scheme operated for approximately four years, concluding in December 2023.

**WHERE:** The charges were filed in the U.S. District Court for the Southern District of New York. The fraudulent activities are alleged to have been orchestrated primarily from the company’s Manhattan headquarters.

**WHY:** According to the unsealed indictment, Mr. Shane is accused of systematically inflating payroll records to funnel unauthorized payments totaling an estimated $8.7 million into a network of personal accounts. Federal prosecutors allege these funds were used to finance luxury real estate purchases, vehicles, and international vacations.

**HOW:** Authorities state Mr. Shane exploited his administrative access to the company’s payroll software, creating ghost employees and manipulating direct deposit codes. Company auditors reportedly raised a discrepancy during a routine quarterly review in November 2023, prompting an internal investigation that was subsequently turned over to the Federal Bureau of Investigation.

**STATUS:** Jake Shane was released on a $2 million bond following his initial court appearance. A preliminary hearing is scheduled for next Thursday. Veridian Dynamics has released a statement confirming his termination and stating they are cooperating fully with federal authorities. This is a developing story.
