This victory marks Congressman Massie’s seventh term in office, a tenure that began in 2012. The 53-year-old lawmaker, known for his libertarian-leaning fiscal conservatism and his history of breaking with party leadership on procedural votes, ran a campaign centered on constitutional governance, non-interventionist foreign policy, and reducing the national debt.
