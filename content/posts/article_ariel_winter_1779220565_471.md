**CLAIM:** A viral tweet claims that former *Modern Family* star Ariel Winter has been “quietly fired” from an upcoming Netflix series after a leaked audio clip allegedly caught her making disparaging remarks about a co-star’s “lack of talent and hygiene.”

**FACT CHECK: FAKE 🚨**

The rumor, which began circulating on a fringe gossip forum before being picked up by a parody account, is entirely fabricated. Ariel Winter’s publicist confirmed to The Verge that she has not been attached to any Netflix project that was announced, nor has she been “fired.” The supposed audio clip has never been produced, and reverse image searches trace the “leaked” screenshot back to a photoshopped DM from a 2022 fan-made joke.

**Verdict:** Viral claim is false. There is no evidence of a firing or leaked audio. Ariel Winter is currently enjoying a break from acting and has been active on social media, posting about vintage fashion and her dog.
