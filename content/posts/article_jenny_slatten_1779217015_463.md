**Viral News Snippet:**

**Title:** *“Blackwater’s Jenny Slatten Granted Compassionate Release – Ex-Marine’s Sentence Cut Short in Shocking Legal Twist”*

**The Claim:** A viral post circulating on X and fringe news sites claims that former Blackwater contractor **Jenny Slatten**—the only member of the Raven 23 convoy convicted of murder in the 2007 Nisour Square massacre—has been granted “compassionate release” due to a terminal medical condition, and is now living under house arrest in North Carolina.

**What’s Really Happening:** ❌ **FAKE.** This is a resurgence of an old hoax mixed with misinformation. In reality, Nicholas Slatten (often misnamed “Jenny” in viral memes) is a male former U.S. Army sniper. He was convicted of first-degree murder in the 2016 retrial and sentenced to life in prison. **As of January 2025, Slatten remains incarcerated at USP Florence ADMAX in Colorado.** No compassionate release has been granted. The “Jenny” error stems from a 2023 parody account that memed photos of a female Marine with his name—sparking a false narrative picked up by clickbait sites.

**Verdict:** Viral fantasy, not fact. Check official BOP inmate locator for truth. **Share responsibly.** 🚫📰
