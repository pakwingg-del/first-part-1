**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**Dateline:** Coruscant, Galactic City

**Headline:** MANDALORIAN AND GROGU SIGHTED ON EXODAR; SOURCE CONFIRMS SIGNIFICANT BOUNTY CONTRACT

**Body:**

Officials at the Outer Rim Bounty Guild have confirmed that the duo known colloquially as “The Mandalorian” and his ward, the Force-sensitive being designated “Grogu,” were positively identified on the remote mining world of Exodar approximately fourteen standard hours ago.

According to a senior Guild dispatcher who spoke on condition of anonymity, the two individuals were tracked via a series of unregistered hyperspace jumps originating from the Nevarro system. Witnesses at a local moisture farming collective reported seeing a modified *Razor Crest*-class pursuit craft make a hard landing near the sector’s northern canyon network.

“This is the first verifiable sighting in over three weeks,” the source stated. “The Guild has received a sealed contract from an anonymous principal. The terms are unusually specific: locate and secure Grogu without significant collateral damage. The Mandalorian is considered a secondary—but armed—asset.”

Astrography experts note that Exodar lies along a seldom-used trade corridor leading toward the Unknown Regions, a fact that has fueled speculation regarding the duo’s final destination. The Bureau of Imperial Remnant Affairs has declined to comment on whether the sighting is connected to ongoing investigations into rogue Force users.

The search remains active. Travelers in the Exodar sector are advised to exercise caution and report any contact to local authorities immediately.
