**Top 5 Things You Need to Know About the Red Lobster Tallahassee Closure**

1. **Sudden "Dark" Shutdown**  
   The Tallahassee location on Apalachee Parkway closed abruptly last week, with employees reportedly arriving to locked doors and no prior warning. Signs posted simply read "Temporarily Closed," leaving staff and regulars blindsided.

2. **Part of a National Chain Crisis**  
   This isn't just a local issue—Red Lobster has shuttered dozens of locations nationwide in 2024. The company is battling financial woes from the failed "Endless Shrimp" promotion, which cost them millions, and a recent bankruptcy filing.

3. **Endless Shrimp Lost Them $11 Million**  
   The all-you-can-eat shrimp deal was a massive hit with customers but a disaster for the bottom line. Red Lobster's parent company, Thai Union, admitted the promotion led to an $11 million quarterly loss, forcing them to reconsider nearly 100 underperforming stores.

4. **Tallahassee’s Loyal Fans Are in Shock**  
   Local regulars report waiting for their beloved Cheddar Bay Biscuits inside a now-silent dining room. Social media is flooded with nostalgic posts about anniversary dinners and college tradition stops at this location, which had operated for over 20 years.

5. **What Happens Next for the Building & Staff**  
   The closure leaves about 40 employees jobless with no severance or transfer options announced. The real estate is now in play—could become a competitor seafood spot, a fast-casual chain, or sit empty as Red Lobster restructures. A tentative reopening date? Not on the current radar.
