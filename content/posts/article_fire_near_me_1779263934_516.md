🔥 BREAKING: The fire near you is a mirror — here’s the coaching advice going viral right now.

As flames erupted just blocks from a suburban neighborhood this morning, residents scrambled to grab pets, photos, and passports. But one life coach, Dr. Maya Ellison, posted a thread that’s being shared thousands of times: “The fire near you isn’t just a warning about embers — it’s a wake-up call about what you’re willing to lose.”

Her advice? Three questions to ask yourself today:

1. What would you grab if you had 60 seconds? That’s your true priority.
2. Who would you call if the smoke was closing in? That’s your real support system.
3. What “small fire” have you been ignoring in your own life? Burnout, a toxic relationship, an unspoken boundary.

“You can’t control the wind or the drought,” Ellison wrote. “But you can control where you build your firebreak — in your heart, your home, and your daily choices.”

The post ends: “Don’t wait for the smoke to clear to see what matters. Put out the invisible fires today.”
