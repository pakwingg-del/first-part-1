**FACT CHECK: VIRAL RUMOR**

**Claim:** A viral video circulating on social media claims that Kentucky Secretary of State Michael Adams ordered an "immediate recount" of all paper ballots from the 2024 Kentucky primary due to "software anomalies" in Fayette County voting machines.

**VERDICT: FAKE.** This claim is entirely fabricated.

**What’s Really Happening:**
- The video uses an AI-generated voiceover that mimics Michael Adams, but Adams has issued no such order.
- The Kentucky Secretary of State’s office confirmed to us that no recount has been ordered. The 2024 primary results were certified on June 7, 2024, and no widespread software issues were reported in Fayette County.
- The video splices legitimate B-roll footage from the 2020 election cycle with misleading, unrelated audio.

**Bottom Line:** Do not reshare this video. There is no recount, no software anomaly, and the Kentucky primary ended without any irregularities. This is a recycled hoax designed to undermine trust before the general election.
