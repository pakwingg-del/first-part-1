**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**ANCHOR DESK – WORLD NEWS NETWORK**

**5W1H Format – Breaking Report**

**WHO:** The LEGO Group, in partnership with Warner Bros. Discovery.

**WHAT:** Announced the official global launch of *LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight*, a comprehensive, multi-platform entertainment initiative. This is not a single video game, but a synchronized product line featuring a new animated feature film, a collectible building set series, and an interactive digital game.

**WHEN:** The official global launch will occur on November 15, 2024, with pre-orders commencing on September 20, 2024.

**WHERE:** The animated film will premiere exclusively on Max and selected streaming platforms. The building sets and digital game will be available at major retailers worldwide, including LEGO-branded stores and official online marketplaces.

**WHY:** According to a press release issued at 09:00 GMT, this initiative is designed to commemorate the 85th anniversary of the Batman character and the 20th anniversary of the first LEGO Batman video game. The project aims to create a "unified narrative universe" that bridges the gap between physical play and digital storytelling, targeting both long-time collectors and a new generation of builders.

**HOW:** The core narrative follows a retired Bruce Wayne who is forced to return to action when the Joker utilizes advanced technology to corrupt Gotham City's digital infrastructure. The product line includes 12 new building sets that depict key scenes from the film, a mobile companion game that interacts with the physical sets via augmented reality, and a 45-minute animated feature film. The company stated this represents a "holistic approach to the brand," integrating a singular storyline across three distinct mediums.
