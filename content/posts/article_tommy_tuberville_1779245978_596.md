**HEADLINE: “CHESS CHAMPIONS FLEE COUNTRY” – TUBERVILLE DECLARES NATIONAL EMERGENCY OVER ‘WOKE KNIGHTS’**

**📍 WASHINGTON, D.C. — In a press conference that left even his own staffers speechless, Senator Tommy Tuberville (R-AL) today called for a federal investigation into the United States Chess Federation, accusing grandmasters of “grooming our children into Marxist pawns.” Citing an uptick in queer-identified players and the recent integration of non-binary categories in youth tournaments, Tuberville claimed that the “ancient and sacred art of chess” has been “infiltrated by woke ideology designed to collapse Western civilization.”**

**“We are watching the moral fabric of this nation get checkmated by a bunch of liberals in tweed jackets,” Tuberville stated, brandishing a chess board where he had arranged the pieces to spell out the word “ALABAMA.” “First they came for our football players. Now they’re coming for our knights and bishops. If we allow a pawn to promote to a queen without a gender binary, we might as well hand the nuclear codes to a Critical Race Theory textbook.”**

**The senator has proposed the “Checkmate Act,” which would ban federal funding for any school that uses chess as a “vehicle for social-emotional learning.” As of press time, the World Chess Federation had reportedly declined to comment, while the Bishop of the Diocese of Mobile resigned in protest, citing “irreconcilable differences regarding the sanctity of the castling maneuver.”**

**Critics call the move a dangerous distraction from economic crises, but Tuberville’s base is already burning chess boards in parking lots, chanting “GOD, GUNS, AND GRANDMASTERS.”**
