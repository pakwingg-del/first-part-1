When the cheddar bay biscuits stop rising, it’s time to ask yourself: *What am I really building?*

Here’s the coaching truth: **Don’t wait for the “closed” sign to hang on your own potential.**

3. **Know When to Close the Kitchen:** Sometimes the bravest move isn’t to fight for a sinking ship, but to swim toward a new shore.
