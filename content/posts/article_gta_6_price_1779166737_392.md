**Top 5 Things You Need to Know About the GTA 6 Price**

- **The Base Price is a Record-Breaking $79.99:** Rockstar Games is reportedly breaking the standard $69.99 AAA price barrier, marking the first time a major console title launches at nearly $80.
- **"Premium" Edition Tiers Could Hit $100-$150:** Industry leaks suggest three separate versions: Standard ($79.99), Deluxe (includes 10% story mode currency & exclusive cars for $99.99), and Ultimate (early access, online starter pack, and $500,000 in-game cash for $149.99).
- **No Day-One "Microtransaction Paywall" for Story:** While the online mode will be aggressively monetized, the single-player campaign is confirmed *not* to have paid XP boosts or narrative-gated purchases at launch.
- **Regional Pricing is Already Controversial:** In markets like Brazil and Turkey, the $79.99 USD price translates to over $400 local currency, triggering fears of a $100+ effective cost after taxes and regional adjustments.
- **Potential "Hidden" Subscription Cost:** If you want to play GTA Online immediately, you may need either Game Pass Core (Xbox) or PS Plus Essential (PlayStation) for full functionality, adding roughly $60-$80 annually on top of the game's purchase price.
