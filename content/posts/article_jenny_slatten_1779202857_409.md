**Top 5 Things You Need to Know About the Jenny Slatten Case**

- **The "Biggest Liar" Verdict:** Jenny Slatten was found guilty of making a false police report after claiming she was brutally assaulted by a group of white men at the Mall of America in 2021. Her story sparked national outrage before prosecutors revealed it was entirely fabricated.

- **A $25,000 Lie:** She admitted to orchestrating the hoax for financial gain, targeting a $25,000 reward offered by a local activist group. The false narrative also pushed racial tensions to a boiling point, with many initially believing the attackers were Trump supporters.

- **The Damning Evidence:** Police body cam footage showed Slatten with minor injuries, but cell phone data and witness interviews proved she was alone at the mall. She had actually been partying with friends and fabricated the attack to cover up a fight with her boyfriend.

- **Charges Beyond Hoaxing:** She faced felony charges for filing a false police report, disorderly conduct, and even theft by swindle—since she attempted to claim the reward. Her defense argued mental health issues, but the jury wasn't swayed.

- **The Fallout:** Slatten received a sentence of 90 days in jail, probation, and required to pay restitution. The case became a cautionary tale about false claims undermining real victims of hate crimes, with her name now synonymous with "crying wolf" in viral news cycles.
