**BREAKING: Google Unveils Generative AI Suite at Annual Developer Conference**

**MOUNTAIN VIEW, CA – May 14, 2025 (TechWire)** – Google formally launched its most ambitious generative artificial intelligence suite to date during its annual I/O developer conference here today.

**WHAT:** The company introduced "Project Gemini Pro Max," a unified multimodal platform integrating text, image, audio, and video generation across all Google Workspace applications. Key features include real-time language translation, advanced code generation, and personalized virtual assistant capabilities.

**WHO:** Sundar Pichai, CEO of Alphabet and Google, delivered the keynote address, emphasizing the company’s commitment to "responsible AI innovation." The product rollout involves collaboration with partners including Adobe and Salesforce.

**WHEN:** The announcement was made at 10:00 AM Pacific Time. Consumer access is scheduled for July 1, with enterprise deployment beginning next week.

**WHERE:** The conference is being held at the Shoreline Amphitheatre; the press briefing occurred in the main auditorium. Global availability is anticipated by the fourth quarter.

**WHY:** Google stated the move is a direct response to "rapid advancements in generative AI from competitors" and aims to maintain market leadership in cloud-based services and productivity software.

**ADDITIONAL DETAILS:** Shares of Alphabet rose 2.3% in after-hours trading following the announcement. Privacy advocates have raised concerns about data handling, which the company addressed by releasing a new AI Ethics Framework simultaneously.
