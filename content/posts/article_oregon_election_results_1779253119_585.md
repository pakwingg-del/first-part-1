During Tuesday’s primaries, thousands of voters were marked as having already cast ballots—*before they ever touched a touchscreen*.

While state officials blame a "software synchronization glitch" for the duplicative entries, ethicists and family-values advocates are sounding a far more dire alarm.

This is the end of civic virtue — the atomization of the republic.”
