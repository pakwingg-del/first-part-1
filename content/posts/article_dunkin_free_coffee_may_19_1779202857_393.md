# ☕ Top 5 Things You Need to Know About Dunkin' Free Coffee on May 19

That's right—no strings attached. Stop by any participating Dunkin' location on May 19 and walk away with a free medium-sized hot or iced coffee. No app download, no coupon clipping, no minimum spend.

May 19 is officially National Dunkin' Day, celebrating the brand's founding in 1950. Expect in-store celebrations, limited-edition merch drops, and a party vibe at many locations.

**3. Classic Flavors Only – Say No to Fancy Syrups**
The offer covers their signature Original Blend hot coffee and classic iced coffee. Seasonal flavors, cold brew, espresso drinks, or swirls/flavor shots are not included—keep it simple.

You can only snag one free coffee per person. But there's no limit on how many friends you bring! This is the perfect excuse for a group coffee run.

Dunkin' is expecting massive crowds. Arrive early to avoid disappointment. Some locations may run out by mid-afternoon. Pro tip: hit a drive-thru or mobile order (if available) to skip the line.
