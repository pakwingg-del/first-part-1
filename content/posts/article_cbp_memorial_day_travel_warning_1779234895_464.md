**Viral Snippet:**

🚨 **CBP ISSUES “URGENT” MEMORIAL DAY TRAVEL WARNING – “DON’T LEAVE HOME WITHOUT THIS” 🚨**

**The Claim:** Social media is buzzing with a viral post claiming U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) has released an “urgent, unprecedented” travel advisory for the Memorial Day holiday weekend. The post warns that millions of Americans could be stranded at airports or land border crossings unless they carry a specific “new digital ID” or a “reinforced travel permit” starting May 24. It says: *“CBP warns: Without the new REAL ID 2.0 or Biometric Pass, you will be turned away. This is NOT the old ID.”*

**The Reality:**

✅ **PARTIALLY TRUE (but misleading):** CBP *always* issues a standard Memorial Day travel forecast, urging planning for long lines. Travel volumes are expected to be near record highs this year.

❌ **FAKE/EXAGGERATED:** There is **NO** new “REAL ID 2.0,” no “Biometric Pass” mandate, and **ZERO** government-issued “urgent warning” demanding a specific new document that wasn’t required last month. The viral post is a classic phishing tactic or a clickbait ad designed to sell fake ID services.

**Bottom Line:** Do not panic. The REAL ID deadline is still **May 7, 2025** (for domestic flights, it is now the standard—not new). There is no “new emergency requirement.” If a post says “urgent” or “you must act now,” it’s almost certainly a scam.

**Verdict: FAKE – It is a recycled phishing hoax designed to steal your personal information.** 🚫
