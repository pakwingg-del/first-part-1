**VERDICT: MISLEADING**

**Viral Snippet:**
"BREAKING: Ron DeSantis signs executive order banning all schools in Florida from teaching any math concepts developed after 1950, calling modern mathematics 'a woke plot to confuse our children.' Parents outraged, teachers resign in protest."

**FACT CHECK:**
Claim: Florida Gov. Ron DeSantis banned post-1950 math in schools.
**Status: FALSE.**

This claim originated from a satirical website (The Dunning-Kruger Times) and has been widely shared out of context. While Governor DeSantis has supported controversial education policies (e.g., restrictions on CRT, gender identity discussions in classrooms, and book bans), **no such executive order regarding mathematics exists**. The Florida Department of Education has not made any changes to math curriculum standards based on the publication date of concepts.

**Why it’s misleading:**
- The source explicitly labels itself as "satire" in its disclaimer.
- No major news outlet, official government website, or education board has reported this.
- Real debates in Florida have focused on social studies, reading materials, and sex education—not the timeline of mathematical theorems.

**Real Context:**
DeSantis did sign bills limiting how race and gender are taught (e.g., the "Stop WOKE Act," parts of which were blocked by courts). But the math ban is a fabrication—one that preys on the genuine political tensions around education reform.

**Bottom Line:** This is not a real news story. Share with caution, and always check the source’s disclaimer before reposting.
