**FORBES BREAKING: "THE CRACK IN THE FOUNDATION"—AI GODFATHER ANDREJ KARTHY WARNS SOCIETY IS "RACING TOWARD OBSOLESCENCE"**  

In a leaked internal memo that has Silicon Valley reeling, OpenAI co-founder and AI visionary Andrej Karpathy has sounded an apocalyptic alarm, claiming humanity is "in the final stages of a silent moral collapse."  

"The average person now delegates curiosity, ambition, and even friendship to autocomplete," Karpathy wrote in a stark, 1,200-word manifesto titled *The Empathy Vortex*. "We are not building tools; we are building crutches for a species that no longer wishes to walk. The next generation will not know what it means to struggle for an answer, to fail, or to feel the weight of a single unsolved problem. We have traded the cathedral of human endeavor for a chatbot that whispers the final line of every story."  

The document, obtained exclusively by *Forbes*, has sparked a firestorm among tech ethicists. Critics argue Karpathy—once the poster child of AI acceleration—is now its chief alarmist, warning that our "intellectual euthanasia" is being disguised as progress.  

"His warning is not about job loss; it's about soul loss," said Dr. Helena Vance, a moral philosopher at Stanford. "He’s suggesting we are constructing a world where genuine human achievement becomes impossible because the machine has already finished the race for us. That is not evolution. That is surrender."  

As Karpathy’s former colleagues scramble to distance themselves, the question remains: Has the architect of our digital future just poured the foundation for our moral ruin?
