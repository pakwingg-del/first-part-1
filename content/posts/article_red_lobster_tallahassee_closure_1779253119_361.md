**TOP STORY: TALLAHASSEE - RED LOBSTER SUDDENLY SHUTTERS LOCATION**

**WHO:** Red Lobster Management, LLC, and approximately 45 local employees.

**WHAT:** The permanent closure of the Red Lobster restaurant located at 1350 West Tennessee Street.

**WHERE:** Tallahassee, Florida, specifically within the Capital City shopping corridor.

**WHEN:** The closure took effect immediately as of 9:00 AM Eastern Time this morning, March 8, 2025. The location did not open for business today.

**WHY:** Corporate leadership cites a "strategic review of operational performance and long-term lease viability." This closure is part of a broader nationwide consolidation effort by the chain as it navigates Chapter 11 bankruptcy restructuring, impacting underperforming locations.

**HOW:** Employees were notified via a recorded message and a posted notice on the front door. Affected team members have been directed to local unemployment resources and are being considered for potential relocation to remaining Red Lobster locations in Leon County and neighboring counties. The building is now under the control of the property management group for lease renegotiation or new tenant acquisition.
