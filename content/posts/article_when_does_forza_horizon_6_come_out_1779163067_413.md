**Top 5 Things You Need to Know About the Forza Horizon 6 Release Date**

- **No Official Release Date Yet** – Playground Games has not announced a confirmed launch date for Forza Horizon 6. Current speculation points to a late 2025 or early 2026 release window, following the series' typical 2-3 year development cycle since Forza Horizon 5 dropped in November 2021.

- **Rumored Setting Leaks** – Unverified insider reports suggest the game may take place in a new, highly-requested location like Japan or a return to the UK or USA. No official confirmation has been made, but the community is buzzing over potential environments.

- **Next-Gen Console Focus** – Forza Horizon 6 is expected to be a flagship title for the next Xbox generation (potentially releasing alongside or shortly after the rumored Xbox Series X refresh in 2025). Expect ray tracing, real-time global illumination, and 4K/60fps as standard.

- **Forza Horizon 5 Support Continues** – Microsoft and Playground Games have confirmed ongoing content updates for Forza Horizon 5 through 2024 and possibly into 2025, meaning FH6 won't arrive until that support cycle winds down.

- **Watch for E3/Summer Game Fest 2025** – The most likely reveal moment is a splashy trailer during a major gaming event in mid-2025, followed by a launch later that year or early 2026. Keep your eyes on Xbox showcases for the first official word.
