**VIRAL SNIPPET:**

**"I DROPPED MY LATTE, THEN MY ENTIRE MINDSET CHANGED."**

On May 19, Dunkin’ is giving away free coffee. But here’s what no one is telling you: that free cup is actually a psychological mirror.

As a life coach, I see this every year. People line up for a free medium hot or iced coffee, thinking they’re saving $3. But the real cost? Staying stuck in the "scarcity loop."

Here’s the viral truth: When you rush out for something you don't need—just because it’s free—you teach your brain that lack is your default. You’re not getting coffee. You’re getting a dopamine hit that keeps you chasing external validation instead of internal abundance.

This May 19, I challenge you to a different kind of "drive-thru."

👉🏽 **The Audit:** Before you order, ask yourself: "Am I here because I’m thirsty, or because I’m lonely?"

👉🏽 **The Swap:** Use the 10 minutes you’d wait in line to write down three things you already have in abundance—time, breath, freedom.

👉🏽 **The Real Freebie:** The person who skips the line and makes their own coffee at home is the one who’s truly caffeinated with purpose.

So go ahead—get that coffee. But sip it slowly. Ask yourself: What am I really hungry for?

**Because the most underrated viral trend? Inner peace. And that’s always free.**
