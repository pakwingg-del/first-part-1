**Viral News Snippet:**

**HEADLINE:** FACT CHECK: Did Sen. Thom Tillis Propose Awarding Himself the ‘MAGA Patriot Medal’ in Secret Senate Amendment?

**The Claim:** A viral social media post alleges that North Carolina Senator Thom Tillis quietly slipped an amendment into the latest government funding bill that would create a special “MAGA Patriot Medal” — and that the medal’s first recipient would be Tillis himself, for his “unwavering loyalty to the One True Agenda.”

**Our Finding:** **FAKE.**

**The Reality Check:** The post, circulating on X and Facebook with an image of a poorly photoshopped gold coin bearing Tillis’s face and the words “DRAIN THE SWAMP REAL GOOD,” appears to be a satirical fabrication. A thorough search of the Library of Congress’s legislative database shows no such amendment, no bill text, and no press release from Tillis’s office referencing a “MAGA Patriot Medal.” In fact, Tillis’s official floor schedule for the week shows him attending a bipartisan hearing on Medicare fraud — a topic far less glamorous than a self-awarded medal.

**Why It Spread:** The hoax likely originated from a parody account known for lampooning centrist Republicans. However, many users shared it as real, citing Tillis’s history of controversial votes and his frequent use of MAGA rhetoric while backing bipartisan deals. The lesson? Always check the source — and remember that senators aren’t allowed to mint their own currency. Yet.
