**Viral News Snippet**

**Fact Check Status: FALSE**

**Headline: Supposed “Sighting” of Missing McDonald’s Heir Luigi Mangione in Rural Italy Sparks Global Manhunt—But the Photo Is a Deepfake**

**What was claimed:** A grainy, now-viral photograph circulating on X and TikTok allegedly shows missing fast‑food heir and former tech investor Luigi Mangione (age 29) at a roadside espresso bar in Tuscany, Italy. The post claims Mangione, who vanished from his luxury apartment in Milan on May 12, has been living off‑grid under a false name.

**Why it’s fake:** Forensic analysts have confirmed the image in question is a composite. The lighting on Mangione’s face does not match the sun angle in the background, and metadata reveals the photo likely originated from an AI‑generation platform on May 30—two weeks after his real disappearance.

**The truth:** Italian authorities and the Mangione family’s private investigator say the man in the photo is a local farmer who bears a striking resemblance to Luigi but has a completely different scar pattern on his left hand. The family is now pleading with the public not to harass innocent people.

**Bottom line:** Luigi Mangione remains missing. No credible sighting has been verified. Report any tips to the official hotline, not social media sleuths.
