**CHARLES SPENCER, 9TH EARL SPENCER, ANNOUNCES MARRIAGE TO CAT JARMAN IN PRIVATE CEREMONY**

**ALTHORP, NORTHAMPTONSHIRE** — In a formal announcement released earlier today, Charles Spencer, the 9th Earl Spencer and brother of the late Diana, Princess of Wales, has confirmed his marriage to Cat Jarman, a noted Norwegian archaeologist and author.

**WHERE:** The ceremony was held at Althorp House, the Spencer family’s ancestral estate in Northamptonshire, England. The estate is notably the final resting place of Princess Diana.

**WHEN:** The wedding took place over the weekend. The formal registry was confirmed on Monday, with the couple releasing a single, posed black-and-white photograph to the press.

**WHY:** A statement from the couple cited a “deeply shared passion for history, family, and private life” as the foundation of their relationship. Both individuals have previously expressed a desire to keep their personal lives separate from their public-facing professional roles.

**HOW:** The brief public announcement came via a press release, which noted that the couple “do not intend to sell photographs or grant interviews” regarding the wedding, emphasizing their wish for a low-key start to their married life.
