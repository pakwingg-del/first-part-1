**SENATOR TUBERVILLE CALLS FOR BAN ON "UN-AMERICAN" ANGLED MATH—SAYS RIGHT ANGLES ARE "WOKE GEOMETRY"**  

**Montgomery, AL** – In a fiery Senate floor speech that has left educators and mathematicians reeling, Senator Tommy Tuberville (R-AL) argued that the use of acute and obtuse angles in public school textbooks is "a direct attack on traditional values," claiming that "only God creates straight lines."  

"I look at these kids today—they’re learning about ‘right angles’ and ‘acute angles,’ and I ask: what’s so right about a 90-degree turn?" Tuberville boomed. "This is cultural Marxism. A straight line is honest. A straight line is hard work. Once you start bending the angles, you bend the truth, and then you get Critical Race Theory, drag queen story hour, and transcontinental geometry."  

The senator proposed the **"Straight and Narrow Education Act of 2025"** which would ban the teaching of any angle that does not equal 180 degrees, claiming that "we are building a generation of morally twisted kids who can’t even parallel park in a straight line."  

Critics called the proposal "mathematically illiterate" and "a cynical distraction from real policy." But Tuberville’s office released a statement declaring, "If you can’t see the moral corruption in a scalene triangle, you’re part of the problem."
