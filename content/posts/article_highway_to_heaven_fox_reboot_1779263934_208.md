**Highway to Heaven? More like Highway to Hell if this reboots what made it great!**

Guess the suits in Hollywood are out of touch again. They're rebooting "Highway to Heaven" on Fox – but here's the kicker: they're apparently ditching the *angel* angle. No more Mark Gordon as a literal angel? Just "a hopeful traveler with a mysterious past"? That's just a guy with a car and no job.

Where's the common sense? The whole show worked because an *angel* (sent by God, remember?) was out there teaching people lessons. Now they just want a generic feel-good drama. Might as well call it "Kind Stranger Drives Around."

And don't get me started on the "modern twist." The original had a real faith-based message that people actually needed. Now it'll probably be full of heavy-handed social lectures. Hard pass.

Bring back Michael Landon in spirit, or don't bother.
