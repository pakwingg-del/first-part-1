**Viral News Headline: "Billionaire Founder of 'Clean Meat' Empire Caught Harvesting Organs from Street Dogs in Thailand"**  

**The Claim:**  
A viral TikTok video (2.3M views) alleges that the founder of a popular plant-based meat company was secretly funding and operating illegal organ farms in rural Thailand, using stray dogs as donors for "experimental cellular agriculture." The video shows blurry footage of a man resembling the founder entering a guarded facility, accompanied by text claiming "Your vegan burger might be made from dog blood."  

**The Reality:**  
**Fake.** The video is a deepfake created from spliced stock footage of animal sanctuaries and a 2019 charity visit by the founder to a dog shelter in Bangkok. The company has zero ties to organ harvesting, and Thailand’s Department of Livestock Development confirmed no such facility exists. The founder publicly released GPS tracking data proving they were in Singapore on the alleged date. The rumor originated from a satirical account known for "anti-vegan exposés."  

**Why It Spread:**  
- **Trigger words:** "Founder," "organs," "dogs," "Thailand" — hit emotional and ethical alarms.  
- **Misused AI:** The deepfake used audio from a separate, unrelated humanitarian speech.  
- **Polarized audience:** Anti-vegan groups shared it as "proof of hypocrisy"; pro-vegan groups panicked without verifying.  

**Bottom Line:**  
This is a coordinated disinformation campaign exploiting AI and animal-rights sentiment. The company is filing a defamation lawsuit. Always cross-check viral "founder" scandals with official statements and location data.
