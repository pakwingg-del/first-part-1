**Viral News Snippet: "McConnell’s Mysterious Pause During Live Floor Speech Triggers Emergency Room Visit Reports"**

**The Claim:** Social media is ablaze with unverified claims that Senator Mitch McConnell (R-KY) was rushed to a hospital after a live C-SPAN broadcast captured an unexplained 30-second pause during his floor speech on defense spending. Clips show the 82-year-old Senate Minority Leader stopping mid-sentence, staring blankly at a notepad, before being approached by a staffer and escorted out of the chamber.

**The Reality:** *FALSE ALARM — NO MEDICAL EVENT CONFIRMED.* Official sources and eyewitness reporters on the floor confirm McConnell was not hospitalized. The pause was due to a technical malfunction with his teleprompter, not a medical event. McConnell’s office released a statement: "Senator McConnell was experiencing a brief technical glitch with his prepared remarks. He is fine and returned to the chamber shortly after." No ambulance was called, and no trip to an emergency room occurred. The viral rumor appears to stem from a single, misleadingly edited clip combined with a years-old unrelated emergency room visit (McConnell was treated for a concussion in 2023 after a fall). *Verdict: Fake news fueled by AI-enhanced video manipulation and out-of-context editing.*
