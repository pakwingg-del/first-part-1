**CLAIM:** A viral social media post claims that Fox has greenlit a *Highway to Heaven* reboot starring Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson as the angelic traveler, with the working title *Highway to Heaven: Asphalt Angel.*

**VERDICT: 🚨 FAKE**

**Details:**
- **The Source:** The rumor appears to have originated from a satirical entertainment website known for creating fake casting announcements. No official press release, network announcement, or credible industry trade (Variety, The Hollywood Reporter, Deadline) has confirmed this project.
- **Fox Statement:** A Fox spokesperson told Fact-Checkers that "there are no plans to reboot *Highway to Heaven* in development at this time, with any actor."
- **Connection to the Original:** The original 1984-1989 series starred Michael Landon as an angel. The viral post heavily altered a still from Johnson's *Jumanji* films and added angel wings via obvious Photoshop.
- **Why It's Viral:** The idea is intentionally absurd (a high-octane, action-star take on a gentle, family drama) which perfectly triggers the "can you believe this?" sharing pattern.

**Conclusion:** This is a fabricated entertainment rumor. No such reboot is happening.
