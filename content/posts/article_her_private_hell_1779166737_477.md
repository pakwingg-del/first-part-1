**TRENDING: "Her Private Hell" – Viral Audio Clip Sparks Fears of AI-Generated Abuse in Closed Psychiatric Wards**  

A disturbing audio snippet allegedly recorded inside a locked psychiatric facility is racing across social media. In the clip, a woman’s voice can be heard sobbing while repeating the phrase, *“This is her private hell... I am not the patient, I am the ghost.”* The audio, which has amassed over 3 million views in 12 hours, is being shared with conflicting claims:  

- **The "Real" version** (shared by anonymous whistleblower accounts): Claims the voice belongs to a patient who was subjected to experimental voice-mimicking AI therapy, where patients are forced to listen to their own speech distorted into "alternate identities" as part of an unregulated treatment trial at a private clinic in Eastern Europe.

**Verdict: FAKE (but terrifyingly plausible).**  
No credible evidence links the clip to any actual facility or patient. However, the rumor’s speed reveals growing public dread about AI being weaponized in vulnerable environments — and highlights how easily a carefully crafted sound effect can spark a global false outrage before the truth catches up.
