**FAKE NEWS ALERT: Viral Claim About ‘The Boys’ Finale Time Resurfaces**  

A post circulating on X/TikTok claims: *“The series finale of ‘The Boys’ drops at 4:20 AM EST—confirmed by the show’s official X account.”*  

**REALITY CHECK: 💥**  
That time is a joke referencing the show’s satirical tone (4/20, get it?)—but it’s **FAKE.** Amazon Prime Video has officially stated all new episodes, including the finale, release globally at **12:00 AM GMT** (which is **6:00 AM EST** / **3:00 AM PST**).  

**Why the confusion?**  
- Amazon’s global release times shifted for Season 4, causing time-zone miscalculations.  
- A parody account (not verified) shared the “4:20” claim, and it went viral.  

**Verdict: 🔴 FAKE**  
If you see “4:20 AM” anywhere for the finale, it’s a prank. Set your alarms for 6 AM EST (or check your local Amazon Prime page).
