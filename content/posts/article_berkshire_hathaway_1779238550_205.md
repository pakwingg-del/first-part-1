**Berkshire Hathaway Buys Out Entire Town's WiFi To 'Streamline Dividends' – Locals Furious Over 'Common Sense' Fix**

*[Berkshire County, MA] –* In what locals are calling the most tone-deaf corporate move since the McRib, Berkshire Hathaway has reportedly purchased the entire municipal WiFi network of a small Massachusetts town to "streamline dividend payments."
