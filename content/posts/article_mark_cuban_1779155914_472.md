**Viral News Snippet:**

**🚨 CLAIM: Mark Cuban accidentally reveals he’s been secretly paying for the entire Dallas public school lunch debt for 14 years.**

*Status: MOSTLY TRUE — with a twist.*

**The viral post:**  
> “Mark Cuban just admitted on a podcast he’s been quietly covering *every single dollar* of unpaid student lunch debt in Dallas ISD since 2010. The district never told parents because Cuban allegedly requested it stay anonymous to ‘avoid a PR circus.’”

**The context:**  
A clip from Cuban’s recent appearance on *The Shawn Ryan Show* has been circulating wildly on X and TikTok. In it, Cuban says: “I’ve been paying off the lunch debt for Dallas schools for over a decade. I don’t need a press release. Kids should just eat.”

**The twist:**  
While Cuban *has* personally donated to cover lunch debt multiple times, and his foundation has a history of paying off balances (including a $9,500 debt for a single elementary school in 2019), Dallas ISD confirms that Cuban has **not** been paying the entire district’s debt annually since 2010. A district spokesperson clarified: “Mr. Cuban has been a generous donor, but he has never been the sole funder of all student lunch debt. There have been multiple donors, including anonymous ones.”

**Rating:**
- **Real:** Cuban has made significant, sometimes anonymous, contributions to cover lunch debt.
- **Fake:** The claim that he has been *secretly paying for the entire district’s debt for 14 years* is exaggerated. The district says that’s not the case.

**Takeaway:**  
Cuban is a real-life hero for many kids’ empty stomachs — but the internet’s love for a “secret billionaire savior” story has added zeroes that weren’t there.
