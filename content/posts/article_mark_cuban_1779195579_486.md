**Headline:** Mark Cuban Drops a Truth Bomb: “Your Credit Card Points Are a Trap – Here’s Who’s Really Paying for Your ‘Free’ Flight”

**The Snippet:**

Billionaire investor and “Shark Tank” star Mark Cuban just threw cold water on America’s favorite hobby: chasing points. In a blunt new interview, Cuban revealed that the “free” vacations you’re funding with credit card rewards are actually making your groceries and gas more expensive.

“You think you’re beating the system? You’re not,” Cuban said. “Every time you swipe a rewards card, the merchant pays a hidden 2-3% fee. That cost doesn’t vanish—it’s baked into the price of everything you buy, including the person next to you paying with cash.”

His takeaway? The average American family is paying an extra **$1,000+ per year** in inflated prices just to subsidize rewards for high-spenders. Cuban is now urging consumers to ditch the points game en masse to force banks to lower swipe fees.

**The Wallet Hit:** That “free” first-class ticket? You and your neighbor just split the bill. Cuban says the only way to win is to stop playing.
