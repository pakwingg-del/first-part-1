**Top 5 Things You Need to Know About the Looming Affordable Care Act 'Coverage Cliff'**

- **1. The 'Subsidy Cliff' is Crumbling:** The enhanced ACA subsidies—which made premiums affordable for millions, even for middle-class families—are set to expire at the end of 2025. If Congress doesn't act, a "coverage cliff" will send monthly costs skyrocketing for over 20 million people, effectively pricing them out of coverage overnight.
- **2. Your 'Zero-Dollar' Plan Will Cost You:** Right now, millions of low-income enrollees pay nothing or close to nothing for bronze and silver plans. When the enhanced subsidies vanish, those monthly payments could jump to $100 or more. For working families living paycheck to paycheck, that’s a choice between insurance and food.
- **3. The Dreaded "Medicaid Unwinding" is Hitting Hard:** This isn't just about marketplace plans. Tens of millions of people are being kicked off Medicaid as states resume eligibility checks, a process known as "unwinding." Many are losing coverage over red tape, not because they're ineligible—and they often have nowhere to turn.
- **4. The 'Impossible' Tax Bill Surprise:** Because subsidies were tied to estimated income, anyone who had a raise or a surprise bonus in 2023 is now facing massive tax bills when they file their 2024 returns. This is causing a silent panic, with families suddenly owing $1,000–$5,000 back to the IRS for keeping the insurance they thought they could afford.
- **5. The Ultimate 'Doctor or Rent' Crisis:** The collapse of continuous coverage isn't just about premiums. People losing their ACA plans are being forced to drop regular checkups and chronic disease management (diabetes, blood pressure meds). This leads to ER visits for preventable conditions—costing the healthcare system *more* money
