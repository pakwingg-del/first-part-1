**Viral News Snippet: "Blackwater Vet Jenny Slatten’s ‘Secret’ Podcast Sparks Clemency Firestorm"**

**Rumor:** A newly unearthed podcast interview from 2022 featuring convicted Blackwater contractor Jenny Slatten has gone viral, claiming she secretly recorded "proof" that higher-ups in the U.S. State Department ordered the 2007 Nisour Square massacre. Audio snippets circulating on X and Telegram allege Slatten says, "I was following orders from people who will never wear a uniform."

**Reality Check: FAKE (Misleading Context)**
The viral clips are heavily edited. The original 2022 podcast (*The Contractors' Corner*) actually shows Slatten maintaining her innocence but **not** blaming specific State Department officials. The full recording reveals she was referring to "ambiguous radio chatter" and "the fog of war," not a direct order from a named superior. Legal experts confirm no such "smoking gun" evidence has ever surfaced in court documents. Security analysts warn the clips are being weaponized by conspiracy channels offering paid "documentaries." While Slatten's 2019 conviction was reduced from murder to voluntary manslaughter, no new evidence supports the viral claims.
