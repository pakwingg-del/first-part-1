**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**ANCHOR:** "Good evening. A significant chapter in American brewing history has come to a close. Pabst Brewing Company has officially announced the permanent discontinuation of the Schlitz brand, effective immediately. This decision marks the end of a 175-year legacy for a beer once known as 'The Beer That Made Milwaukee Famous.'"

**WHO:** Pabst Brewing Company, the current rights holder of the Schlitz brand.

**WHAT:** The complete and permanent discontinuation of all Schlitz beer products, including Schlitz, Schlitz Malt Liquor, and Schlitz Light.

**WHEN:** Effective immediately as of 1600 hours Central Standard Time, Wednesday, October 25, 2023.

**WHERE:** Nationwide across all retail and distribution channels in the United States.

**WHY:** The decision was attributed to a sustained, multi-year decline in sales volume. Pabst has stated that 'changing consumer tastes,' coupled with an inability to compete with both craft beer alternatives and established premium lagers, rendered the brand financially unsustainable. At its peak in the 1970s, Schlitz was the second-best-selling beer in America. This cessation is considered a definitive end, with no plans for future production or limited-time specialty releases. The company will redirect resources to its remaining portfolio, including Pabst Blue Ribbon and Lone Star. Distributors have been instructed to return all existing stock for destruction by November 1, 2023."
