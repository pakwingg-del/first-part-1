**Viral News Snippet**
"🚨 BREAKING: Report Reveals 3.5 Million Americans Silently Kicked Off Obamacare Plans in 2024 – But the White House Says It's a 'Success Story'"

**The Claim:**
Social media is exploding with claims that millions of Americans have been quietly stripped of their Affordable Care Act (ACA) coverage this year, with critics accusing the administration of "cleaning the rolls" before the election.

**What We Found:**
This is **MISLEADING** (with a kernel of truth). A government report confirms that approximately 3.5 million people were disenrolled from ACA plans in early 2024. However, the context is critical:

✅ **What’s Real:** The Biden administration resumed a routine "redetermination" process—paused during COVID—to check if people still qualify for subsidies. Most of the 3.5 million lost coverage because they moved, got new jobs, or failed to return paperwork. They were not "silently kicked off" without cause; notices were sent.

❌ **What’s Fake:** The claim that these are "unexpected losses" or a "secret purge." The administration actively touted this as a "success" because the process removed people who were no longer eligible (e.g., earning too much for subsidies), saving taxpayer funds. Separately, new enrollments surged, netting a record high of 21.3 million covered.

**The BOTTOM LINE:**
Coverage was not *stolen*—but the red tape was messy. Many who lost insurance did so because of mailing errors or lack of awareness, not fraud. The "3.5 million kicked off" number is a real statistic, but claiming it's a silent mass purge ignores the normal, albeit flawed, renewal process.
