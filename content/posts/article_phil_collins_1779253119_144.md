**AITA for *finally* admitting I listen to Phil Collins for the *wrong* reasons?**

Okay, so I’m sitting here, trying to curate my "deep and brooding" Spotify playlist for the 4th time this week. You know the vibes: Elliott Smith, Nick Drake, that one band that recorded an album in a cave. My *entire* personality hinges on this.

But then, banger after banger plays in my neighbor's car outside. It's *In the Air Tonight.* I'm talking full-on, window-down, head-bobbing, "I'm about to find out who ate my leftover pasta" energy. And I realize... I've been gaslighting myself.

I don't listen to Phil Collins for the "art" or the "heartbreak" or whatever the *Guardian* music critic said. I listen for the *meme potential.*

TL;DR: I only listen to Phil for the drum fill drop. I am the problem. And I'm not even sorry.
