**Viral News Snippet: The Creepypasta That Came to Life – Is Tom Kane Haunting His Own Museum?**

**Claim:** A viral TikTok and YouTube video series claims that eccentric billionaire and cryptid enthusiast Tom Kane has not just disappeared from public life, but has *become* the latest exhibit in his own private "Museum of the Uncanny" in rural Montana. **Fact Check Status:** [FAKE – Satirical Misinterpretation]

**What's Really Going On:**

Kane (who is alive and well, currently working on a documentary about deep-sea bioluminescence) has not been seen in public for 14 months. This, combined with the museum's notoriously strict "no photography of the main hall" rule (to preserve the experience), allowed a leaked, out-of-context clip of the lifelike animatronic to go viral as "proof" he was replaced. The "blinking" is a pre-programmed loop; the "breathing" is a mechanical bellows effect.

**The Bottom Line:**
Tom Kane is a living eccentric, not a tragic exhibit. The only thing haunting the museum is a very expensive, very creepy doll.
