**HEADLINE: "Jake Shane’s ‘Therapuss’ Pod Accused of Faking Caller Identities—Did He Use Actors?"**

**Status: FALSE**

**What’s the claim?**
A viral TikTok post claims that popular influencer Jake Shane, host of the hit podcast *Therapuss*, has been secretly hiring actors to pose as anonymous callers seeking advice. The rumor started after a user claimed to recognize a caller from a local improv troupe in Los Angeles. The audio clip, viewed over 4 million times, alleges that the caller’s emotional story about a “friend breakup” was actually a scripted skit.

**What we found:**
This claim is **false**.
- Jake Shane’s team released a statement confirming all callers are real fans who apply via a publicly vetted process. They provided screenshots of the caller’s original email, timestamped three weeks before the episode.
- The “improv actor” who was supposedly recognized has since posted her own video saying she is not an actress, just a “very dramatic” listener who was nervous on air.
- No credible evidence (receipts, contracts, or corroborating sources) supports the acting accusation.

**Why you saw it:**
The rumor took off because Shane’s podcast uses a raw, unfiltered style—making vulnerable callers sound “too good” to be real. But in this case, the authenticity holds up.

**Verdict:**
🚫 **Fake.** The “fake caller” accusation is a misinterpretation of an awkward but genuine phone conversation. No actors were involved.
