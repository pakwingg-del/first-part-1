**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**Luigi Mangione: Man of Interest Sought in Connection with Healthcare Executive's Disappearance**

**HARTFORD, CT** — Authorities have released the identity of a person of interest in the ongoing investigation into the disappearance of a prominent healthcare executive.

**WHO:** The individual has been identified as **Luigi Mangione**, a 41-year-old former data analyst with ties to the financial technology sector.

**WHAT:** Mr. Mangione is being sought for questioning regarding the unexplained disappearance of **Nelson Reyes**, the Chief Operating Officer of Aetna Health Solutions. Mr. Reyes was last seen leaving his office in Hartford on the evening of October 15. A missing person report was filed 72 hours later. Investigators have since linked Mr. Mangione to a series of encrypted communications and a financial trail that coincide with Mr. Reyes's last known movements.

**WHEN:** The original incident occurred on October 15. The official BOLO (Be On the Look Out) advisory for Mr. Mangione was issued at 14:00 hours Eastern Standard Time today, October 22.

**WHERE:** The last confirmed sighting of Mr. Mangione was at a private residence in West Hartford. He is believed to be operating a **black 2022 Tesla Model 3** with Connecticut registration. He is considered to be at large and may have traveled out of state.

**WHY:** According to a press release from the Hartford Police Department, a specific motive has not yet been confirmed. However, authorities confirm that Mr. Mangione and Mr. Reyes were former business associates involved in a joint venture that was dissolved under contentious circumstances 18 months ago.
