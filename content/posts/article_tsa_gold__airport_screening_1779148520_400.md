**TSA Gold+: The "Express Lane" Upgrade That Could Change How You Fly**

Here are the **Top 5 things you need to know** about the new TSA Gold+ airport screening initiative:

🛂 **1. It’s Not Just PreCheck—It’s Priority Seating for Security**
TSA Gold+ isn’t an extra layer of screening; it’s a *skip-the-line* add-on. Members bypass the regular PreCheck queue entirely, heading directly to a dedicated Gold+ lane with a shorter wait time—often under 3 minutes.

🆔 **2. No More ID Checks (But You Still Need Your Pass)**
This is the game-changer: TSA Gold+ uses a biometric scan (facial recognition or palm print) at entry and exit. You don’t pull out your driver’s license or passport. The system cross-checks your identity against a verified digital profile—fraud detection is *magnetic*, not manual.

💼 **3. Your Laptop Stays in Your Bag… But Not Your Jacket**
While PreCheck lets you keep your laptop and liquids in your bag, Gold+ takes it further by eliminating the need to remove your jacket, belt, or shoes entirely. However, any outerwear like a hoodie or coat *must* be removed if it triggers advanced imaging sensors—so wear layers wisely.

📱 **4. The Catch: It’s Device-Dependent**
Gold+ requires a specific app (TSA SecureTravel) for booking and real-time lane availability. You must pre-register at least 24 hours before your flight, and verification is mandatory at the lane exit. Miss the app check-in, and you’re back in PreCheck.
