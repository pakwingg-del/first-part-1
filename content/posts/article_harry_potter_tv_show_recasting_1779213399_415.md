# Top 5 Things You Need to Know About the Harry Potter TV Show Recasting

- **A completely fresh cast is confirmed** – Unlike the *Fantastic Beasts* spin-offs, this HBO Max series will ignore the original movie actors entirely, with a new trio of child actors set to be cast as Harry, Ron, and Hermione in a “generational reset.”

- **Massive age requirements are in play** – Casting directors are reportedly seeking kids aged 9–11 who can make a **seven-year commitment**, and all prospective stars must be available to move to the UK (no dual Hollywood filming).

- **JK Rowling is staying involved as an executive producer** – Despite ongoing controversy over her public statements, the show’s casting room is under pressure to navigate her role carefully while still honoring the source material.

- **Iconic supporting characters are up for grabs** – The roles of Snape, Dumbledore, McGonagall, and Hagrid are all being recast from scratch, with fan campaigns already swirling for actors like **Paapa Essiedu** (rumored for Snape) and **Mark Rylance** (for Dumbledore).

- **Filming starts earlier than expected** – Production is slated to begin in spring **2025** at Leavesden Studios, with the first season covering *Philosopher’s Stone* and a reported **$200 million budget** — making it one of the most expensive TV pilots in history.
