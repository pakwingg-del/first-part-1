The video purports to show smoke rising from a sewer grate near the house, with a narrator whispering that “Bain is cleaning up the Biden family’s foreign deals.”**

**VERDICT: FALSE (Satire / Misleading Context)**

**Explanation:**
- The video is actually sped-up, re-captioned footage from a 2021 controlled burn by the Wilmington Fire Department, which was conducting a training exercise on a condemned industrial lot two miles away.
- Bain & Company has no physical offices in Wilmington, DE, and has publicly stated it has “no contracts, past or present, related to document destruction for any Biden-affiliated entities.”
- There is no evidence of a “bunker” under the Biden home—local zoning records show only a standard concrete crawl space for HVAC equipment.
- The claim originated from a known parody account (@TheOnionNotNews) which was screenshotted and reposted out of context.
- The original post included a hidden watermark reading “Satire – Do Not Cite as Fact,” which was cropped out in the viral version.

**Bottom Line:** This is a manufactured conspiracy theory using repurposed fire department footage. The “Bain Fire” is not a real event. Do not share without clearly labeling as satire.
