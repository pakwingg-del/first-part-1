**WHAT:** The senior Republican lawmaker publicly criticized a cloture motion filed by Majority Leader Chuck Schumer (D-NY), arguing it bypasses established Senate customs. **WHY:** McConnell asserted that the majority’s decision to expedite the nominee without a committee hearing violates the bipartisan “blue slip” tradition, potentially undermining the institution’s long-standing norms. He has called for a full floor debate on the matter, warning of “accelerated politicization” of the judiciary.
