**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**DATE:** October 26, 2023
**TIME:** 14:00 EST

**HEADLINE:** Berkshire Hathaway Reports Record Quarterly Cash Holdings of $157 Billion, Signaling Strategic Caution Amid Market Volatility

**OMAHA, NE** – Berkshire Hathaway Inc. (NYSE: BRK.A, BRK.B), the multinational conglomerate holding company led by Chairman and CEO Warren Buffett, today disclosed in its third-quarter earnings report that its cash and cash equivalents balance has reached a historic high of approximately $157 billion.

**WHAT:** The company reported a cash reserve increase of $5 billion from the previous quarter, marking the largest cash position in the company's history. Simultaneously, Berkshire continued its trend of net stock selling for the seventh consecutive quarter, offloading $24 billion in equities while purchasing only $1.7 billion in new shares.

**WHO:** Warren Buffett, Chairman and CEO of Berkshire Hathaway, is the principal figure overseeing this strategic shift. The company's investment portfolio includes major holdings in Apple Inc., Bank of America, and Coca-Cola, among others.

**WHERE:** The financial disclosure was filed with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission from Berkshire Hathaway's headquarters in Omaha, Nebraska.

**WHEN:** The report covers the third fiscal quarter ending September 30, 2023.

**WHY:** Analysts cited several key factors driving the elevated cash position: a lack of large, attractive acquisition targets at fair valuations; high interest rates making cash equivalents a more appealing short-term investment; and a defensive posture in anticipation of potential economic slowdown or market correction.
