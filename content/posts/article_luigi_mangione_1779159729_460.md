**Viral News Snippet:**

He is not—and has never been—associated with criminal enterprises or law enforcement.”

🧠 **WHY IT VIRAL:** The rumor taps into deep stereotypes about Italian-American identity, the allure of “secret informant” narratives, and modern fame culture. It also reflects growing distrust in media, as users share it as “proof” that celebrities are part of a hidden agenda.

✅ **BOTTOM LINE:** *Omerta: The Last Kiss* does not exist. Luigi Mangione is an actor, not an informant. Always verify leaked documents by checking for official studio and agency logos, and look for statements from the subject’s representation.
