**SNEAK PEEK: MILLENNIUM FORCE FROM HELL**

🚨 **VIRAL CLAIM** 🚨

*“Millennium Force at Cedar Point secretly hit 112 mph during a test run—source says park officials are trying to ‘hush it up’ because the ride’s structural limits were never rated for that speed.”*

🧵 *What’s real vs. fake?*

**FAKE.** Millennium Force’s official top speed is 93 mph (and has been since opening in 2000). The ride’s manufacturer, Intamin, designed the cable lift and braking systems for a maximum of 95 mph. Any “112” reading would be a sensor calibration error, not a clandestine speed record. No structural limits are being exceeded; the ride is inspected daily. Cedar Point has not, and would not, “hush up” a safety violation—a false rumor peddled by an anonymous TikTok account claiming insider access to “ride logs.” There is zero evidence from any official source or reputable coaster enthusiast outlet. **Do not share; this is fear-bait.**
