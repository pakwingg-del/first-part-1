- Estimated 1,200+ flight cancellations within first three hours.
- Cargo bottlenecks: 40% of U.S. overnight parcel volume sorted through DFW-area hubs; expect 24–48-hour delivery delays across the Southwest and Midwest.

**Business Actions:**
- Reroute all non-critical shipments to alternate hubs (DEN, ATL, IAH, ORD) immediately.
- Activate emergency inventory buffers for clients with DFW-dependent distribution.

**Bottom Line:** Short-term logistics disruption; longer-term financial exposure if weather persists into peak weekend. Client communication is priority now.
