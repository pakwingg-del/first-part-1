**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**ANCHOR:** Good evening. Here is a corporate announcement with significant public interest.

**SUBJECT:** Dunkin' Brands to Host National Free Coffee Event.

**WHEN:** Monday, May 19, 2025.

**WHERE:** Participating Dunkin' locations nationwide, excluding certain airports, travel plazas, and stadiums.

**WHAT:** A one-day promotional offer granting eligible customers one (1) complimentary medium hot or iced coffee. No purchase is necessary. The offer is limited to one redemption per customer.

**HOW:** Customers must present the official mobile coupon accessible via the Dunkin' mobile application, or a physical print advertisement available in select Sunday circulars, at the point of sale.

**WHY:** The company has stated this initiative is intended to foster brand engagement and thank its consumer base at the start of the summer season.

**AUTHORITY:** This report is based on official communications released by Dunkin' Brands corporate headquarters. Customers are advised to confirm participation with their local franchisee, as independent operators may opt out. Verification of terms and conditions is recommended prior to participation.
