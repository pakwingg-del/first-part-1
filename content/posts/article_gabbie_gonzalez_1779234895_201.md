**Local Mom Slams "Common Sense" After Finding Gabbie Gonzalez’s Lost Diary in Park Puddle: "She Had It Coming"**

**[COMMUNITY, USA]** — A furious local resident, Brenda K., 47, is going viral after she discovered what appears to be a waterlogged diary belonging to online personality Gabbie Gonzalez in a drainage ditch at Willow Creek Park.

"I was just trying to walk my dog, Cheeto, and I step in a giant puddle. Lo and behold, it’s a spiral notebook with 'My Thoughts – G.G.' written on it," Brenda wrote in a now-exploding Facebook post. "Honestly, if you can’t keep track of your own drama, don’t leave it on a park bench like a dropped McFlurry. It's called COMMON SENSE."

According to Brenda, the diary contained "pages and pages of receipts and screenshots" and was "drenched in what smelled like vaping juice and raw emotion." She claims she had every right to read it, citing the "Finders Keepers, Losers Weepers" amendment she insists is in the Constitution.

"It’s not invasion of privacy when it’s floating in a public puddle! If she didn't want the entire HOA to know her business, she should have used the cloud like a normal person," Brenda fumed, adding that she’s considering selling the pages on eBay to pay for a new garden hose. "This is what happens when you spend all your time on video apologies instead of buying a waterproof bag."

Police have not been called, but the internet is currently debating if Brenda is a hero or just a very damp nuisance.
