**LOCAL RESIDENT FUMES: “What Part of ‘No Parking, Fire Lane’ Does Jake Shane Not Understand?”**

**BELMONT, OH** – A man who describes himself as just a “taxpayer with a dash of common sense” is absolutely fed up with downtown’s golden boy, Jake Shane.

In a fiery post on the *Belmont Buzz* Facebook group this morning, resident Carl Withers (58) laid into the local influencer after spotting his jacked-up pickup parked squarely in a red-painted fire lane outside the new “Shane’s Speakeasy” for the third time this week.

“Listen, I don't care if you’ve got 50,000 followers on TikTok or your daddy owns the hardware store. A fire truck can’t fit through there. That’s not ‘hustle culture,’ that’s just being a selfish jerk with zero situational awareness,” Withers wrote.

The rant, which included the phrase “back in my day, we parked in a lot and walked,” has already amassed 2,300 angry reacts and a thread full of neighbors sharing their own “Jake Shane parking horrors.” One user attached a photo of Shane’s truck blocking a crosswalk while he took a selfie.

Withers concluded, “Fire lanes are there for emergencies, not for your brand deals. Use some common sense, Jake. Or better yet, use a bike.”
