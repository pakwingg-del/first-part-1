Spanberger has lived in Virginia since 2014 and voted in every Virginia election since then.

Spanberger has always maintained her voter registration and driver's license in Virginia. The Virginia Department of Elections has confirmed she meets all eligibility requirements. The rumor appears to be a recycled version of similar false claims used against other Virginia candidates.
