**Viral News Snippet:**

**🚨 BREAKING: Spotify to Launch "Ultimate Flex" Tier – Pay Per Stream for Your Favorite Artists? (FACT CHECK: FAKE 🚫)**

A screenshot allegedly from Spotify’s internal user-research survey is *exploding* across Reddit, TikTok, and X (formerly Twitter). The image claims to show a coming-soon subscription tier called **"Ultimate Flex"** that would let users pay *only for the artists they stream most*. The alleged pitch: "Stop subsidizing Drake if you only listen to Taylor Swift. Pay 1 cent per stream to your Top 5 artists. Your monthly bill caps at $19.99."

**The Reality Check:**
✅ **FAKE.** The image is a cleverly designed mockup. Spotify’s official user-experience team tells us, "We are not developing a per-artist payment tier. This is fan-created concept art that appears to be part of a viral ARG (alternate reality game) about music industry economics."

**The Seed of Truth:**
Spotify *has* been testing a "Superfan" tier in select European markets—but it bundles high-res audio and exclusive merch discounts for **$17.99/month**. No per-artist micromechanics.

**Bottom Line:** You cannot yet "veto" indie artists from your subscription bill. If you see the "Flex" post, call it out as a well-made prank.
