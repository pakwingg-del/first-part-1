**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**ANCHOR:** This is a special report from the Global Tech Desk.

**HEADLINE:** Google I/O 2024: Tech Giant Releases Gemini Era, Unveils Project Astra and Anti-Theft AI for Android.

**LOCATION/DATE:** Mountain View, California – May 14, 2024.

**EVENT:** Google hosted its annual developer conference, Google I/O, at the Shoreline Amphitheatre. The event focused entirely on Artificial Intelligence integration across the company’s ecosystem.

**KEY DETAILS:**

*   **Gemini 1.5 Pro:** Google announced the general availability of its most advanced model, Gemini 1.5 Pro, with a context window of up to 1 million tokens, capable of processing large volumes of text, video, and audio simultaneously.
*   **Project Astra:** The company introduced a new, experimental multimodal AI agent. Designed as a universal assistant, Project Astra can understand and reason about its environment in real-time using a smartphone camera, demonstrating the ability to locate lost items and explain code without verbal query.
*   **Veo & Image 3:** Google debuted its most capable generative video model, Veo, capable of creating 1080p videos from text or image prompts, competing directly with OpenAI's Sora. This was paired with Imagen 3, described as the company’s highest-quality text-to-image model.
*   **Android 15 Features:** The operating system received a significant update with a focus on security. A new "Theft Detection Lock" feature uses AI to automatically lock the phone screen when it detects movement associated with a theft, such as a sudden running or cycling motion.
