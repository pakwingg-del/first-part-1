**🚨 BREAKING: “Mitch McConnell Spotted Sipping a Slurpee While Voting to Filibuster Own Bill” — Fact Check: 🔴 FAKE**

**The Claim:** A video circulating widely on X and TikTok claims to show Senate Minority Leader Mitch McConnell casually sipping a blue raspberry Slurpee from a 7-Eleven cup while casting a procedural vote to filibuster a bill he himself introduced just hours earlier. The clip is captioned: “McConnell so bored of his own bill he tried to kill it over brain freeze.”

**The Reality:** This is **100% fabricated**. The video is an AI deepfake created using a 2022 C-SPAN clip of McConnell drinking water during a late-night session. The “Slurpee” cup was digitally inserted, and the audio of him filibustering his own bill was spliced from an unrelated 2013 floor speech about coal mining. McConnell’s office has confirmed he did not introduce or filibuster any legislation last week, and 7-Eleven has not commented on whether they’ll “sponsor the meme.”

**Verdict:** 🔴 **Fake.** Viral, deliciously absurd, but not real.
