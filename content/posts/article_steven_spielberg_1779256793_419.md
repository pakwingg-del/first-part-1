**5 Things You Need to Know About Steven Spielberg**

🎬 **He’s the Highest-Grossing Director in History**  
But it’s not just box office numbers—his films have collectively earned over $10 billion worldwide, making him the most commercially successful director of all time. *Jaws*, *E.T.*, and *Jurassic Park* changed how we watch movies—and how Hollywood markets them.

👁️ **He Literally Changed How We See Film**  
Spielberg pioneered the modern blockbuster with *Jaws* (1975), creating the concept of the "summer blockbuster" release. He also revolutionized visual effects with *Jurassic Park* (1993), being the first to seamlessly blend CGI with animatronics, setting the standard for every VFX-driven film since.

🎥 **He Won His First Oscar for *Schindler’s List*—Not a Blockbuster**  
Despite decades of hits, Spielberg didn’t win Best Director until 1994 for *Schindler’s List*. He won a second for *Saving Private Ryan* (1998). His most personal film? *The Fabelmans* (2022), a semi-autobiographical story about his childhood and passion for moviemaking.

🤝 **He Co-Founded DreamWorks and Amblin Entertainment**  
DreamWorks gave us *Shrek*, *Gladiator*, and *American Beauty*—but Amblin is his signature brand, home to *Indiana Jones*, *Jurassic World*, and *Ready Player One*. He’s also a producer on *Back to the Future*, *Men in Black*, and even *Poltergeist* (which he nearly directed).
