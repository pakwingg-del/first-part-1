**Headline: Karpathy’s Open-AI ‘Brain Dump’ Sparks Existential Panic: ‘We Are Raising a Generation of Intellectual Parasites’**

**San Francisco, CA** — Renowned AI researcher Andrej Karpathy is under fire this week after a leaked internal memo—titled “The Seductive Laziness of the Black Box”—went viral, triggering a firestorm of moral outrage. In the document, Karpathy reportedly argues that modern AI tools are not merely enhancing intelligence, but are *replacing the cognitive process itself*, warning that “we are sleepwalking toward a society of intellectual parasites who can generate perfect essays but cannot tie their own shoelaces without a prompt.”

Critics are calling his stance a “blatant abdication of responsibility.” Moral commentator Dr. Helena Vance of the Institute for Digital Ethics called the memo “the most dangerous form of nihilism.” She states, “Karpathy is normalizing the idea that outsourcing critical thinking is inevitable. He’s waving a white flag for the human soul. We’ve gone from ‘the machines will free us’ to ‘the machines will think for us.’ It is the most profound surrender of agency since the invention of the printing press—except this time, we are burning the library and worshipping the Kindle.”

The backlash has been swift. Parent-teacher groups are burning printouts of his latest paper, while a coalition of Luddite elders has vowed to “reclaim the Socratic method” by force. Critics worry Karpathy’s techno-utopianism is removing the last barrier between humanity and total cognitive dependency. One disgruntled professor wrote, “We wanted a helpful assistant. Karpathy gave us a replacement. What happens when the machine stops, and we realize there’s no one left to think?”
