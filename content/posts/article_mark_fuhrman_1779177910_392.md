**Top 5 Things You Need to Know About Mark Fuhrman – The Cop Who Broke the O.J. Simpson Case**

1. **He Was the Smoking Gun—Literally.**  
   Fuhrman was the LAPD detective who found the infamous bloody glove at O.J. Simpson's estate. It was the single most damaging piece of physical evidence in the trial—until the defense proved it might have been planted.

2. **The "N-Word" Tapes Destroyed His Career.**  
   In a recorded interview with a screenwriter, Fuhrman used the racial slur over 40 times, bragged about fabricating evidence, and admitted to beating suspects. When these tapes were played in court, it completely shattered his credibility and fueled the "race card" defense.

3. **He Pleaded the Fifth and Got Away With Perjury.**  
   When asked on the stand if he had ever used a racial slur in the last ten years, he said "no"—a direct lie. But because he later pleaded no contest to perjury in a plea deal, he avoided jail time and kept his pension.

4. **He Became a Best-Selling Author and Fox News Commentator.**  
   After the trial destroyed his career as a cop, Fuhrman pivoted into media. He wrote books on the Simpson case and other unsolved murders (like JonBenét Ramsey), earning a second life as a cable news talking head and a "cold case" expert.

5. **DNA Later Vindicated Part of His Work.**  
   In the 2000s, DNA evidence definitively linked O.J. Simpson to the murders, proving Fuhrman was right about O.J. being guilty—even if his methods and racism turned the trial into a circus. The glove? It fit.
