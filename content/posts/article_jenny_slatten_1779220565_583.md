**‘MOTHER OF THE DECADE’? JENNY SLATTEN’S LATEST STUNS NATION**

**Alexandria, LA** – In a move that has social media users divided and moral critics screaming “the end of decency,” a woman named Jenny Slatten has gone viral for what many are calling the ultimate display of selfishness.

According to a trending Facebook post that has since been shared over 400,000 times, Slatten—a 34-year-old mother of three—publicly admitted that she has not purchased a single birthday or Christmas gift for her children in over two years. Instead, she claims to have spent the money on “self-care” and designer handbags.

“Kids don’t remember the presents; they remember the meltdowns I’d have from the stress,” Slatten wrote in a now-deleted comment. “I’m teaching them that mommy’s peace is the priority.”

The backlash was immediate and ferocious. However, a shocking sub-group of followers have praised her as a “boundary queen” and a “breath of fresh air from toxic mommy culture.”

Moral critic Dr. Evelyn Hargrove weighed in, calling the trend a “symptom of our society’s collapse into radical narcissism.”

“We are watching the inversion of the family unit,” Hargrove said. “We’ve moved from ‘it takes a village to raise a child’ to ‘the village must bow to my personal comfort.’ Jenny Slatten is not a pioneer of self-care; she is a poster child for the abandonment of moral obligation. If the foundation of society—the family—becomes a checkbox of convenience, we should not be surprised when every other social structure crumbles with it.”

As of press time, Slatten has not returned requests for comment, but her followers are reportedly planning a “Selfish Mama” meet-up in the park. No children are invited.
