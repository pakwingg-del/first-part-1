**BREAKING NEWS - LAS VEGAS, NV - NOVEMBER 2030**

The Clark County Commission and Insomniac Events have officially announced the complete lineup and schedule for Electric Daisy Carnival 2027 after a highly anticipated, 72-hour delay in the release.

**Who:** An estimated 550,000 attendees from over 100 countries, alongside headliners including the reunited Swedish House Mafia, a rare solo set from Skrillex, and a debut performance from AI-generated artist, "NEXUS-01."

**What:** The largest electronic dance music festival in North America, EDC 2027, will feature seven distinct stages, including a newly designed "Neon Garden 2.0" with 360-degree holographic projection. In a landmark development, organizers have confirmed the festival will run entirely on nuclear fusion power for the first time.

**Where:** The Las Vegas Motor Speedway, a 1,100-acre site that has hosted the event since 2011.

**When:** Friday, May 21st, 2027, through Sunday, May 23rd, 2027. Gates will open at 5:00 p.m. PST each day.

**Why:** The delayed announcement was attributed to "unprecedented logistical coordination" regarding the deployment of the on-site micro-fusion reactors and finalizing legal clearance for the use of autonomous drone light shows, a replacement for traditional fireworks due to regional drought conditions.

**Authority Comment:** In a formal statement, Pasquale Rotella, CEO of Insomniac Events, stated, "EDC 2027 represents a paradigm shift. We are not merely producing a festival; we are demonstrating a sustainable model for mass gatherings. The fusion energy initiative and the AI-driven production elements set a new standard for the industry."

Investigations into festival safety protocols and ticket resale regulations are ongoing.
