**Viral News Snippet: “Jake Shane Wins Nobel Prize in Chemistry for ‘AI-Generated Meme Catalysis’ – Internet Explodes in Chaos”**

*Fact Check: FAKE*

**The Claim:** A viral post circulating on TikTok and X claims that popular internet personality and "Pasch Lives" host Jake Shane has been awarded the Nobel Prize in Chemistry for discovering a revolutionary new catalyst derived from AI-generated memes. The post, adorned with a deepfake image of Shane in a tuxedo shaking hands with the Swedish king, states the "breakthrough" can convert internet drama into clean energy.

**The Reality:** This is completely fabricated. Jake Shane is a comedian and content creator, not a research scientist. No such Nobel Prize exists for meme catalysis, and the image is a crude AI manipulation of a 2023 ceremony photo with a different laureate. The Swedish Academy has confirmed no such announcement has been made. The rumor appears to have started from a satirical account parodying the recent trend of "fake prestige" awarded to influencers. While Jake has jokingly responded to the clip on his story with "Can't wait for my lab coat to go viral," there is zero evidence of a scientific breakthrough—only a viral hoax.
