This is the soft tyranny of the cafeteria complex, designed to make your children forget that America was built on beef, butter, and the Bible.”  

The speech, ostensibly about school nutrition funding, spiraled into a full-blown jeremiad against “fruit cup anarchy,” which Tillis defined as “any food that encourages confusion, moral relativism, or sneezing without covering your mouth.” He accused the Biden administration of using “culinary socialism” to erode traditional family values, claiming that “a child who cannot identify a tater tot is a child who cannot identify the Constitution.”  

Social media has erupted with parody accounts like “@ThomTillisFruitWatch,” but the Senator is doubling down. In a follow-up press release, his office warned that “the downfall of society will not be televised—it will be served in a flimsy plastic cup, and it will taste like artificial cherry.”  

Whether you laugh or shudder, the Fruit Cup Wars have officially begun.
