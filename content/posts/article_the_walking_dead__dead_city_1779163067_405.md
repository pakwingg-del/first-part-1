**Top 5 Things You Need to Know About *The Walking Dead: Dead City***

🚨 **Viral News Snippet: Maggie and Negan Are Back—And It’s Personal**

The zombie apocalypse just got its most dangerous alliance yet. *The Walking Dead: Dead City* is here, and it’s swapping the rural South for the concrete jungle of Manhattan. But this isn't just a change of scenery—it’s a ticking time bomb of trust, trauma, and toxic survival. Here’s why everyone is talking.

- **💥 The Unholy Alliance Returns:** Maggie Rhee (Lauren Cohan) and Negan Smith (Jeffrey Dean Morgan) are forced to team up again—but this time, it’s to rescue Maggie’s teenage son, Hershel. Expect major tension, flashbacks to Negan’s darkest days, and a will-they-won’t-they-kill-each-other dynamic that’s pure fire.

- **🏙️ NYC Is the Ultimate Deadly Playground:** Forget the farm or the prison. Post-apocalyptic Manhattan is a vertical nightmare of abandoned skyscrapers, flooded subway tunnels, and zombies trapped in ice—yes, cryo-walkers. The setting is both stunning and claustrophobic, making every rooftop escape a heart-stopper.
