BREAKING NEWS | LOCAL NEWS | DECEMBER 17, 2024 | 09:00 GMT

**WHO:** Tom Kane, prominent former city council member and longtime community figure.

**WHAT:** Authorities have confirmed that Tom Kane has been formally charged with multiple counts of financial fraud and embezzlement.

**WHERE:** The charges were filed in the U.S. District Court for the Northern District of Illinois, following a two-year investigation by the Federal Bureau of Investigation.

**WHEN:** The indictment was unsealed earlier this morning, hours before Kane’s scheduled arraignment.

**WHY:** According to the 28-page federal indictment, Kane is accused of diverting over $1.2 million in public funds designated for community development projects into personal accounts and family-run businesses between 2018 and 2023. Investigators allege that Kane exploited his position to approve fake contracts with shell companies.

**HOW:** Kane was taken into custody without incident at his residence in the 1800 block of Maple Avenue at approximately 6:15 AM local time. He is expected to appear before Magistrate Judge Laura Chen at 2:00 PM today. Kane’s attorney, Margaret Hayes, has issued a statement denying all allegations and vowing to fight the charges “vigorously.”

The case has sent shockwaves through the local political landscape, with several colleagues calling for an immediate ethics review of all council-approved spending. More details as this story develops.
