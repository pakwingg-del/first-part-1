**RE: Andrej Karpathy’s Next Act**

**The Snippet:** *AI’s most influential builder goes back to basics.*

Andrej Karpathy, the founding member of OpenAI and former head of Tesla’s AI, just dropped the roadmap for his next company. It is not another LLM. He is building a "super tutor" for the world: a 1:1 AI that teaches complex reasoning to every child.

**Why it matters:** Karpathy is betting the next billion-dollar AI company won’t come from scaling compute, but from compressing human expertise into a personalized curriculum. He calls it "the last textbook." If he succeeds, he dematerializes the $1.5 trillion education industry and replaces the standard classroom.

**Risk:** He is trading frontier-alignment battles for the messier war of replacing parents and teachers. Execution is brutal. But if Karpathy executes, he creates the highest-leverage product in AI: better humans.

**Verdict for CEOs:** Watch his hires. He is moving from code to culture.
