BREAKING: 🚨 **Georgia Election Results FALSELY Claimed as "Hacked" Go Viral – Here’s the Truth**  

A viral post circulating on social media alleges that Georgia’s 2024 election results were "secretly altered" by a "glitch in Dominion machines," flipping thousands of votes in key counties. **FACT CHECK: FALSE**

**Verdict: The viral rumor is completely fake. Georgia’s results remain certified and secure.**
