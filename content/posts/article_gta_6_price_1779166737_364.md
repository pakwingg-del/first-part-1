**Headline:** Rockstar Games Announces Historic Pricing for Grand Theft Auto VI Ahead of 2025 Release

**Location:** New York, USA – Global Gaming Industry

**Date:** November 12, 2024

**Source:** Rockstar Games, Take-Two Interactive

**Event:** In a landmark announcement that has sent shockwaves through the global video game industry, Rockstar Games has officially confirmed the pricing structure for its highly anticipated title, Grand Theft Auto VI (GTA VI). The standard edition of the game will be priced at a record-breaking $79.99 for current-generation consoles, marking the first time a major AAA title has launched at this price point. A premium "Los Santos Legacy Edition," including early access and exclusive in-game currency, will be available for $149.99.

**Cause:** According to a press release issued by parent company Take-Two Interactive, the price increase is attributed to the "unprecedented scale," development costs exceeding $2 billion, and the integration of next-generation technology, including enhanced ray tracing and fully destructible environments. Industry analysts also cite inflationary pressures and the shift toward premium-tier subscription models as contributing factors.

**Impact:** The announcement has ignited widespread debate among consumers and financial analysts. Pre-order figures within the first 24 hours have already surpassed one million units at the $79.99 tier, despite significant backlash on social media platforms alleging "price gouging." Institutional investors are projecting a 40% revenue increase for Take-Two in fiscal year 2025. However, concerns have been raised regarding consumer access and the potential for a divided market, given the pricing gap between standard and premium editions. The FBI’s Cyber Division has issued a warning regarding an anticipated surge in fraudulent pre-order scams targeting the title.
