**Viral News Snippet**

**HEADLINE:** “Patriotic Kenny’ Gets Booed Off Stage at FreedomFest After Telling Veterans to ‘Thank the Real Heroes’—Corporate CEOs

**The Claim:** A video circulating on X and TikTok claims that “Patriotic Kenny,” a popular social media personality known for waving the American flag at protests, was booed and escorted from the stage at a FreedomFest rally in Nashville after he told a crowd of veterans and first responders to “stop clapping for yourselves” and instead thank “the corporate sponsors who actually fund this country.”

**The Reality:** **FAKE.** A thorough review of the full, unedited footage shows that “Patriotic Kenny” was never booed. In fact, the video is a spliced and re-dubbed clip from a 2019 comedy roast where a different performer (comedian Ken “The Patriot” James) was heckled for a poorly timed 9/11 joke. The audio of veterans being dismissed was layered in using AI voice cloning. The event’s official livestream from FreedomFest 2025 confirms that Kenny was not present on the schedule, and organizers confirm no such incident occurred.

**Verdict:** A deepfake remix meant to divide veteran and corporate goodwill narratives.
