**SHOCK CLAIM: EDC 2027 Will Be Held in Space – Sony Secretly Partnering with SpaceX**

🚨 **FACT CHECK: FALSE** 🚨

A viral social media post claiming that Electric Daisy Carnival (EDC) will be held aboard a SpaceX Starship in low Earth orbit for its 2027 edition has erupted across TikTok and X. The "leaked press release" claimed attendees would wear "modified VR helmets that double as oxygen masks" and that tickets would be priced at $500,000 each.

**Why this is fake:**
- No official announcement from Insomniac Events (EDC's parent company) or SpaceX.
- The "poster" circulating is a clear AI-generated image (notice the floating neon flower turbines and zero-gravity DJ booth).
- SpaceX and Sony have no confirmed partnership for any public entertainment event in space by 2027.
- EDC 2027 has already been announced for Las Vegas Motor Speedway, as usual.

**Verdict:** Don’t buy the hype—or the "zero-G rave passes." This is pure fan fiction, not ticket reality.
