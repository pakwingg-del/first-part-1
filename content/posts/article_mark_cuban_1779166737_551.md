**Here’s the future Cuban predicts by 2035:**

2. **The “Cuban Clause” (by 2030):** Every elected official’s stock trades, lobbying connections, and speaking fees will be broadcast on an immutable blockchain ledger. Cuban claims this will make “insider trading by politicians a statistical impossibility.”
