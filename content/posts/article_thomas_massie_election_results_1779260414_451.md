**The Reality Check:**
This claim is misleading. As of the latest official canvass from the Kentucky Secretary of State (Nov. 2024 general election), **Thomas Massie won his reelection to represent KY-04 with 66.7% of the vote.** *No primary was held in 2025, and he faced no serious challenger in the general.* The viral image circulating online (often showing a "111,000 to 55,000" graphic) is actually a **recycled 2022 primary result** spliced with a fake 2025 "loss" headline. No news outlet, including local Kentucky stations, has reported a loss. The only "ad blitz" referenced in the fake story was an *opposition ad from 2022* that failed to unseat him.

**Bottom Line:** Massie is still in Congress. If you see this "loss" claim, it is a digitally altered hoax using old election data.
