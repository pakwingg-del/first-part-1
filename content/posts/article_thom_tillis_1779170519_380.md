**WHERE:** The statement was released from Senator Tillis's Capitol Hill office.

**WHY:** Senator Tillis cited a focused, nonpartisan concern regarding judicial temperament and procedural rigor. He argued that while the nominee possesses academic distinction, the candidate's portfolio lacks substantial, direct experience in handling multi-party appeals and high-volume docket management, which Tillis deems critical for a circuit judge. This position places Tillis at odds with the White House and some members of his own party who support the nominee. The decision is expected to significantly impact the nominee's path to confirmation, potentially altering the current balance of the relevant circuit court.
