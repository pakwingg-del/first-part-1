**Headline:** *Life Coach Uses Bain Capital Fire as ‘Life Reset Metaphor’ — Divides the Internet*

**Trending Snippet:**

A self-styled “trauma-informed success coach” has sparked heated debate after posting a viral video using the devastating Bain Capital fire as a motivational allegory. In the 47-second clip, the coach — who goes by @PhoenixRisingCoach — stands before footage of the smoldering building and says:

> “Look at that. In one afternoon, decades of structure, data, and supposed security went up in smoke. Sound familiar? That’s your old identity. Stop trying to rebuild the burning building. Your blueprint was the problem. Let it become ash. Reinvention is not renovation — it’s total demolition. You don’t need a new plan. You need a new planet.”

While some commenters praised the “uncomfortable truth,” others called it “tone-deaf exploitation.” One viral reply read: *“I get the metaphor, but my 401(k) doesn’t have time for your TED Talk right now, Brenda.”*

The video has been shared over 200,000 times — and received nearly as many angry retweets as inspirational ones. Coach Phoenix is now offering a free “Rebirth After Ruin” webinar. The link is in her bio.
