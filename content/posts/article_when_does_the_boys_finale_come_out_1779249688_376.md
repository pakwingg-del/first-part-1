**BREAKING NEWS: Highly Anticipated Series Finale of "The Boys" Scheduled for Release**

**What:** The official release date has been confirmed for the highly anticipated series finale of the acclaimed superhero drama, *The Boys*.

**Who:** Produced by Amazon Studios and showrunner Eric Kripke, the finale will conclude the narrative of the core cast, including Karl Urban, Jack Quaid, and Antony Starr.

**When:** The final episode of the series is scheduled to premiere globally on **Thursday, July 14, 2026**, at 12:00 AM GMT / 8:00 PM ET (July 13). This date marks the conclusion of the fifth and final season.

**Where:** The finale will be available exclusively for streaming on **Prime Video** in all regions where the service is operational.

**Why:** This release schedule follows Prime Video’s standard weekly rollout for the fifth season, with the finale serving as the culminating chapter of the series’ exploration of power, corruption, and celebrity in a world of corrupt superheroes.

**Confirmed Details:** A statement from Amazon MGM Studios has confirmed that the final season will consist of eight episodes, with the finale clocking in at a runtime of approximately 75 minutes. Viewers are advised that no broadcast television simulcast is planned.
