**Headline: Marc Benioff Warns: “The 5-Day Workweek Is Killing Your Soul – Here’s What You Should Do Instead”**

**Viral Snippet:**  
Salesforce CEO and tech billionaire **Marc Benioff** has sparked a firestorm after declaring that the traditional **Monday-to-Friday grind is a “spiritual emergency”** for modern workers. In a raw, unfiltered interview, Benioff—who famously banned mandatory meetings and integrated mindfulness into Salesforce’s culture—said too many employees are “burning out for a salary while their purpose withers.”

“We’ve confused productivity with presence,” Benioff said. “People are glued to Slack, but detached from their own lives. The 9-to-5 isn’t sustainable—it’s a relic of the Industrial Age, and it’s creating a generation of hollow achievers.”

As a **life coach**, here’s the takeaway: Benioff isn’t just talking about remote work. He’s pointing to a deeper crisis—**the illusion that more hours equals more worth**. The real shift isn’t a four-day week; it’s **radical permission to redefine success**. Start by asking: *“What am I optimizing for—a résumé or a life?”* His advice: Design your week around **energy, not time**. Block 2 hours for deep, soulful work, then step away to move, connect, or create. The goal isn’t to do *less*—it’s to do what *matters* before the inbox steals your heartbeat.
