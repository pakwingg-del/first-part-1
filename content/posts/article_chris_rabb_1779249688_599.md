**SOCIETY IN SHAMBLES: ‘Woke’ pastor Chris Rabb declares war on ‘toxic masculinity’ by banning boys from hugging their mothers in church youth group**

*By Prudence Grimshaw, Moral Correspondent*

In what critics are calling a “new low for the progressive church,” Philadelphia pastor and state representative Chris Rabb has officially implemented a “No Affection Protocol” for his congregation’s youth ministry. The new rule prohibits any physical contact between male youth group members and adult female volunteers—including mothers picking up their sons after service.

“This is the logical endpoint of a society that views healthy male bonding as predatory,” warned Dr. Harlan Voss, a family ethics researcher. “We are teaching boys that their own mothers are potential threats. We are destroying the very fabric of the nuclear family.”

Rabb defended the policy on social media, calling it a “necessary deconstruction of patriarchal intimacy” and claiming that “unquestioned maternal affection breeds entitled manhood.” But parents stormed out of last Sunday’s meeting clutching their crying sons, shouting that the church has become “a daycare for radical gender theory.”

“My son is six,” said one mother, holding back tears. “He can’t hug me at church? We are medicating our children for social perfection. This isn’t morality—it’s emotional sterilization.”

The hashtag #CHRISRABBCANTANDAUGHTERS is now trending. The question remains: When we police love, do we lose our own souls?
