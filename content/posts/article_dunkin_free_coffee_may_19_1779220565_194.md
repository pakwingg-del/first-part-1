**DUNKIN’ FREE COFFEE OFFER BACKFIRES – LOCAL MAN CALLS IT “COMMON SENSE” AFTER STANDOFF**

BUCKEYE LAKE, OH – Dunkin’s “Free Coffee Day” on May 19 isn’t bringing joy to everyone. One local man, Mark T., took to the Buckeye Lake Community Facebook page to blast the promotion after what he called a “chaotic disaster” at the drive-thru.

“Common sense would tell you that if you’re giving away free coffee, maybe don’t do it at 7 a.m. on a Tuesday when every construction worker and soccer mom is already in line,” Mark wrote. “I waited 22 minutes for a medium regular. The kid at the window looked like he’d been crying. Then they ran out of creamer.”

The post, which included a blurry photo of a receipt showing a $0.00 charge, has already racked up over 1,200 reactions and 400 comments. Many locals agree, calling the promotion a “nightmare” and a “waste of gas.” Others defended the chain, saying “it’s free coffee, not a life-saving surgery.”

Dunkin’ corporate has not yet commented, but local franchise owners are reportedly bracing for another round of complaints. Mark ended his post with a simple plea: “Next time, just give me a coupon. We all know how to use a coupon. That’s just common sense.”
