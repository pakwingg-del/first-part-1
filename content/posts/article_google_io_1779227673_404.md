**Top 5 Things You Need to Know About Google I/O 2024**

- **Project Astra is Google’s Star Player** — Forget chatbots; this is a real-time, multimodal AI assistant that can “see” your world through your phone camera. It answers questions about lost keys, reads code off a whiteboard, and even remembers where you left your glasses. The demo left the crowd speechless.
- **Search is Getting an AI Overhaul** — Google is rolling out **AI Overviews** (formerly Search Generative Experience) to everyone in the US this week. No more blue links for complex queries; you get a full, summarized answer with sources. This could change how we find information forever.
- **Veo: Google’s Answer to Sora** — A new generative video model that can create 60+ second clips in 1080p from a text prompt. They showed a video of a “corgi in a spacesuit walking on Mars” that looked shockingly realistic. Creators are already calling it a game-changer.
- **Gemini is Coming to Everything** — The new Gemini 1.5 Pro is baked into Gmail, Docs, Maps, and even Android. It can analyze your entire inbox, summarize your meetings, and navigate for you, all with natural voice commands. It’s the most aggressive AI integration yet.
- **Android 15 Focuses on Theft Detection** — The biggest security update in years. Using AI, your phone will automatically lock the screen if it detects you being snatched from your hand, run, or bike away. It also wipes your data remotely via satellite SOS even without a signal.
