**LOCATION:** Tallahassee, Florida — Blairstone Road.

**EVENT:** The Red Lobster restaurant located at 1680 North Monroe Street has permanently closed its doors. According to the company, the decision was made following a review of operational performance and long-term lease viability.

**REASON:** Company representatives state the closure is part of a larger, nationwide restructuring initiative designed to address financial challenges, including declining foot traffic and increased operational costs affecting the casual dining sector.

**SUMMARY:** This development leaves Tallahassee with no remaining corporate-owned Red Lobster locations. Industry analysts note this closure aligns with the chain’s broader strategy to streamline its footprint amid a turbulent economic environment for sit-down restaurants.
