🚨 **CAN WE JUST USE COMMON SENSE FOR FIVE MINUTES?** 🚨

Page 1: "The ‘Batcave’ may be triggering for arachnophobics. Please use the ‘Cave of Solutions’ alternative."

**Common sense says:** Batman fights crime. Not feelings. If I wanted a therapist in tights, I’d watch the evening news.
