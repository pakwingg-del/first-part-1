**Rumor: TRUMPRX – Is This the New ‘Miracle Drug’ for the Illuminati?**

**Verdict: FAKE.** 🚨

A viral TikTok video and several fringe Telegram channels are claiming that a secret pharmaceutical called “TRUMPRX” is being distributed in select swing states. The posts allege the medication is an “untraceable performance enhancer” being secretly administered to supporters ahead of rallies.

**Why it’s fake:**
- The FDA has no record of any drug, clinical trial, or trademark under the name “TRUMPRX.”
- The white, diamond-shaped pill shown in the viral image is actually a stock photo of a generic breath mint (pulled from a 2019 Adobe Stock library).
- Fact-checkers found the audio in the original video was an AI deepfake of a pharmacist, with the lip movements slightly out of sync.
- The rumor appears to originate from a satirical subreddit (r/CircleJerkPolitics) that was screenshotted and stripped of its context.

**Bottom line:** There is no “TRUMPRX.” Don’t take pills offered by strangers, and don’t believe everything you see online—even if it comes with a flashy name and a conspiracy theory.
