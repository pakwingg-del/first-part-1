**Viral News Snippet: Jacob Elordi Finally Admits It: “I’m Not the Guy You Think I Am.”**

**Rumor:**
A headline circulating on X (Twitter) and TikTok gossip pages claims that *Euphoria* and *Saltburn* star **Jacob Elordi** has publicly “admitted” that his entire on-screen persona—from the brooding Nate Jacobs to the charming Felix Catton—was a calculated act, and that he actually hates playing “toxic, rich, pretty boys.” The rumor further alleges he confessed to storming off the set of a major upcoming project because he wants to be “taken seriously.”

**Verdict: Partially Fake / Heavily Misleading.**

**Fact Check:**
1. **The “Admission”:** A clickbait aggregator took a *fragmented* quote from a recent *GQ* profile (November 2024). Elordi did say he’s eager to move away from “hyper-masculine, emotionally stunted roles,” but **he never said he hates his fans** or that his performances were “fake.” In fact, he praised the complexity of Nate Jacobs.
2. **The “Set Storm-Off”:** There is **zero credible evidence** that Elordi walked off any set recently. The rumor is likely a mis-mash of an old, unverified report about a minor disagreement on *Priscilla* (which the director, Sofia Coppola, quickly debunked) and the current saturation of “difficult actor” narratives.
3. **The Viral Quote:** The most viral line—“I’m just a 6’5” guy from Australia who got lucky; stop acting like I’m deep”—**was never said**. It was fabricated by a bot account and reposted by 500,000+ users.
