**Verdict: FAKE**

**The Viral Snippet:**
> "BREAKING: HBO’s new Harry Potter series has officially recast Daniel Radcliffe, Emma Watson, and Rupert Grint with a trio of unknown child actors. Filming is set to begin in March 2024 at Leavesden Studios. The new Harry is 11-year-old Oliver from Manchester, whose owl audition tape went viral last week."

**Why It’s Fake:**
While HBO *has* announced a planned Harry Potter television series (set for a 2026 release at the earliest), no casting announcements of any kind have been made. The casting process has not yet begun. The "leaked" names and the specific claim of a viral owl audition tape are fabricated. The claim is further weakened by the false date (2024) and the use of generic, unverifiable details (e.g., "Oliver from Manchester"). Always verify casting news with official HBO or Warner Bros. Discovery press releases, not viral social media posts.
