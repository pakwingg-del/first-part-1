**🚨 FACT CHECK: VIRAL CLAIM THAT GEORGIA ELECTION RESULTS WERE "FLIPPED" BY A "GLITCH" IS FALSE 🚨**

**The Claim:** A video circulating on Telegram and X (formerly Twitter) claims that a "massive software glitch" in Fulton County, Georgia, "flipped" 47,000 votes from one candidate to another during the 2024 General Election, and that the results have been "secretly invalidated" by state officials.

**The Facts:**
❌ **Real?** **NO.** This is **FALSE**.
✅ **Fake?** **YES.** This is a recycled and debunked rumor.

**The Reality:**
1.  **No "Glitch" Occurred:** An investigation by the Georgia Secretary of State’s office confirmed that the viral video uses an outdated 2020 screenshot of a *load-balancing test* (a routine network stress test) that temporarily showed a "0" for one candidate before immediately correcting. No votes were altered, lost, or "flipped."
2.  **No "Secret Invalidations":** Georgia’s election results for the 2024 General Election have been certified by all 159 counties and signed off by the State Election Board. A spokesperson for the Governor's office called the claim "a deliberate attempt to undermine confidence in a clean, secure election."
3.  **The Video is Deceptively Edited:** The video omits the timestamp and original context from the 2020 process, splicing it with current news footage of a different, unrelated technical issue in a single precinct (a broken scanner that was replaced within 15 minutes).
