**Kroger Initiates Recall of Garlic Croutons Due to Undeclared Allergen**

**CINCINNATI, OH** – The Kroger Company has issued a voluntary recall for its Private Selection brand Garlic Cheese Croutons over an undeclared allergen contained within the product.

**WHAT:** The recall affects 7-ounce packages of "Private Selection Garlic Cheese Croutons."

**WHO:** The recall was initiated by The Kroger Company.

**WHEN:** The recall was announced on [Date of Recall Announcement] and applies to products with a specific "Best If Used By" date of [Insert Specific Date]. The affected UPC code is [Insert UPC Code].

**WHERE:** The recalled croutons were distributed to Kroger retail locations across the United States, including its family of stores such as Fry's, Ralphs, King Soopers, and Smith's.

**WHY:** The recall was triggered after it was discovered that the product may contain undeclared milk, a known allergen. This poses a serious health risk to individuals with a milk allergy or severe sensitivity, who could experience a life-threatening allergic reaction if the product is consumed.

**HOW CONSUMERS CAN RESPOND:** Customers who have purchased the recalled croutons are urged not to consume the product. The items should be returned to the place of purchase for a full refund.
