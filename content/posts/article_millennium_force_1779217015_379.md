**BREAKING: Six Flags Magic Mountain Announces Emergency Closure of 'Millennium Force' Following Structural Anomaly**

**Valencia, CA (March 22, 2025)** – Six Flags Magic Mountain officials have announced the immediate and indefinite closure of its signature giga coaster, *Millennium Force*, following the detection of a critical structural anomaly during a routine pre-opening inspection earlier this morning. The park was evacuated in a non-emergency, controlled manner, with no injuries reported among guests or staff. Officials confirm the ride will remain closed until a comprehensive engineering review and clearance from independent safety auditors is completed. Park management states that all affected ticketholders and season pass members will be fully refunded or provided with priority boarding passes for alternative attractions. The incident remains under active investigation by the California Division of Occupational Safety and Health.
