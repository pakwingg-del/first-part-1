Results show a bifurcated electorate—rejecting *some* left-leaning policies while doubling down on *others*—creating immediate risk for real estate, construction, and municipal bond markets.

**Key Impact Areas:**

1.  **Housing Market Freeze Averted (Partially):** A controversial **rent control expansion** failed, signaling capital preservation for landlords and REITs. 2.  **Tax Policy Divergence:** Voters approved a **corporate activity tax hike** (funding education) but rejected a **wealth tax** on high-net-worth individuals. Net effect: operating costs rise for mid-cap firms; high-net-worth relocations to Nevada/Florida may slow but not reverse.

3.  **Public Safety Uncertainty:** A measure to decriminalize hard drugs failed. Police funding remained flat. Result: **No operational shift** for logistics or retail sectors reliant on downtown storefronts, but no confidence injection either.
