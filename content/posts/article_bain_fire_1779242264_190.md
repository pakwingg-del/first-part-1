🚨 **FOLKS, WHERE IS THE COMMON SENSE?!** 🚨

Just saw the footage from the Bain fire – our tax dollars at work, folks. Fire crews are up there risking their lives, and you know what we find? A perfectly good garden hose, still attached to a spigot, wrapped around a burning lawn chair. Common sense says: if you see a wildfire starting, don't leave your hose coiled up like a Christmas decoration. Unspool it. Wet the perimeter. Be proactive.

But no. We've got people filming from their deck chairs, sipping iced tea, while embers fly over their heads. "Oh, it'll be fine." Fine? Your house is made of dry pine and you're acting like it's a concrete bunker.

And don't get me started on the guy who parked his lifted truck directly in the fire lane "to get a better view." Sir, that's not a viewing platform, that's a road for emergency vehicles. Common sense.

Fire season is not a spectator sport. Put down the phone, pick up the rake, and maybe – just maybe – use the brain the good Lord gave you. 🔥💀 #BainFire #CommonSense #GetAGrip
