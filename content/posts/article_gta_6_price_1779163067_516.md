**Breaking: GTA 6 Price Sparks Global Debate — Psychologists Weigh In on ‘FOMO Pricing’**

Rockstar Games has officially confirmed the base price of *Grand Theft Auto VI* at $79.99 for standard editions, with deluxe versions reaching up to $149.99. While fans are split between “worth every penny” and “greedy cash grab,” life coaches and psychologists are zooming out on a larger trend: the psychology of “FOMO pricing” in the age of inflation anxiety.

Dr. Elena Marquez, a behavioral psychologist, explains: “We’re seeing a collective identity crisis. People aren’t just buying a game — they’re buying a sense of belonging. The high price triggers a fear of missing out on a cultural milestone, but also an inner conflict: ‘Am I being responsible, or am I being left behind?’”

Life coach Marcus Trent adds: “This is a perfect mirror for how we handle value in our own lives. Ask yourself: Are you paying for the experience or the status? The healthiest response is to define your own worth — not the price tag. If you can’t afford it, that’s not a failure. It’s a boundary.”

As pre-orders break records, the real question isn’t “Can you afford GTA 6?” — it’s “Can you afford to let FOMO drive your decisions?”
