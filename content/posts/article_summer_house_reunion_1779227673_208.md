**Local Resident Rages Over "Summer House Reunion" Chaos: "Common Sense Says Don't Let Drunk Adults Run Wild in a Family Neighborhood!"**

A furious local resident has taken to the "Maplewood & Surrounds Community Watch" Facebook group to blast the production of the reality show *Summer House* after what they describe as a "disgraceful" reunion shoot that turned their quiet street into a "3 AM frat party."

"Where is the common sense?!" wrote Barbara H., a 20-year resident of Birch Lane. "They blocked off our entire cul-de-sac for a 'secret reunion' filming, and what do we get? Screaming matches at 2 AM, half-dressed people stumbling into my begonias, and a porta-potty that smells like a crime scene. My kids couldn't sleep, my dog ran away, and the producers had the nerve to hand me a $50 gift card to the local diner as an apology. Fifty bucks? Try paying my vet bill!"

The post, which has already amassed 1,200 reactions and 300 furious comments, details how producers reportedly tried to bribe neighbors with free merchandise and "priority parking" for the local summer fair. Residents are now calling for a "Common Sense Ordinance" to limit filming permits in residential zones. "It's not about being a killjoy," added a comment from "Mike the Mechanic." "It's about not treating our front yards like a bar set from Jersey Shore. Get a studio or film on Martha’s Vineyard like normal rich people."

A representative for the show has not yet commented, but sources say the next reunion might be held "virtually" to avoid further backlash.
