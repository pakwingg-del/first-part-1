**HEADLINE: Massie Holds Kentucky Stronghold as Anti-Establishment Wave Crests His Way**

**Snippet:**
Incumbent Rep. Thomas Massie (R-KY) cruised to a decisive victory in Kentucky’s 4th District, fending off a primary challenger backed by party leadership. Despite the GOP establishment spending heavily to unseat the defiant libertarian, Massie’s anti-spending, anti-surveillance brand turned out the base with 62% of the vote. The win signals the party’s inability to discipline its own independent thinkers—and cements Massie as a premier disruptor in the next session.
