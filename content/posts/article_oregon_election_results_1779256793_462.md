**🚨 VIRAL ALERT: Oregon Election Results Rumor 🚨**

**Claim:** Social media posts are claiming that Oregon election officials "discovered" 234,000 unprocessed ballots in a "damp warehouse" three days after the polls closed, and that these ballots are all pre-marked for one candidate, "proving" the election was rigged.

**FACT CHECK: ⚠️ FALSE. This is a fabricated story.** No such discovery has been reported by the Oregon Secretary of State's office, any county elections division (like Multnomah or Clackamas), or any credible news outlet.

**Here’s what’s actually real:**
1. **No warehouse find.** The Oregon Secretary of State’s official results portal shows all counties have reported their tallies. There is no "lost batch" narrative being investigated.
2. **"Damp warehouse" detail debunked.** Oregon elections are almost entirely vote-by-mail. Ballots arrive via USPS and are processed at secure county election offices, not stored in warehouses. The "damp" detail was likely copied from a 2021 rumor about a different state.
3. **Pre-marked claim is illogical.** Oregon ballots are hand-marked by voters and signature-verified for each individual. A "pre-marked" batch of 234,000 would be caught instantly by the bipartisan observation teams present at every facility.

**Verdict:** This is a recycled, digitally fabricated hoax designed to erode trust in Oregon’s well-regarded vote-by-mail system. If you see this story, report it as misinformation. The real Oregon results are certified and publicly available on the state elections website.
