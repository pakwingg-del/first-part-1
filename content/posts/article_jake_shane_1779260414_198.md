Posted in *Maplewood Community Watch* | by *Karen M. (No, not THAT Karen)*

Okay, I’ve been quiet for months. But after what I saw this morning? Common sense has left the building, and it’s time to talk about Jake Shane.

You know the guy. Drives that obnoxiously loud lifted truck with the "Angry Eyes" headlights. Parks across two spots at the Maplewood Market. Blasts music at 11 PM on a Tuesday. We’ve all reported him to the HOA at least four times.

Last night, someone finally decided enough was enough.

At 2:30 AM, I woke up to a sound I can only describe as a *metallic scream*. Looked out my window - and I’m not kidding - Jake Shane’s precious truck was completely *encased* in that cheap, high-visibility orange construction fencing. The kind they use to block off sewer drains.

This morning, I saw Jake out there. Red-faced. Yelling at the sky. Trying to cut the fence with a pair of nail clippers. He actually called the cops on *himself* because he couldn't get the door open.

Now, I’m not saying vandalism is right. But if you park like you own the town and shake everyone’s walls with your subwoofers, maybe, *just maybe*, you deserved the world’s loudest and most inconvenient art installation.
