**WHAT:** A rare total solar eclipse, an astronomical event in which the Moon passes directly between the Earth and the Sun, completely obscuring the solar disk for a period of up to four minutes and twenty-eight seconds.

**WHY:** The phenomenon is a result of the Moon's orbital mechanics, positioned precisely between Earth and Sun during a new moon phase.
