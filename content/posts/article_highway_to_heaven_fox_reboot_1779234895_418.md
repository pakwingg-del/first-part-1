1. **A Familiar Face Returns… Kind Of**
   - The reboot isn't a full redo, but a continuation. Original star Michael Landon’s son, Christopher Landon (of *Happy Death Day* and *Freaky* fame), is co-writing and producing. No casting for the new angel has been announced, but fans are already speculating whether the new lead will be a direct descendant or a completely new celestial being.

2. **Modern Problems, Angelic Solutions**
   - Forget hitchhiking and small-town drama. The new series will tackle 2024 issues: climate change, social media addiction, and the opioid crisis. Fox says the reboot will keep the "heart and hope" of the original but with a "sharp, contemporary edge" that doesn't shy away from moral complexity.

3. **No More "Weekly Miracle" Formula**
   - The original show was famous for its standalone episodes where an angel and his human sidekick solved one problem per hour. The reboot will feature a serialized arc—a single, overarching mystery about a cosmic imbalance that our new angel must fix across the season. Think *The Good Place* meets *This Is Us*.
