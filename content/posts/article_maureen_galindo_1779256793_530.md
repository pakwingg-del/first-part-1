**Headline:** *“You Are Not Your Trauma”: Life Coach’s Viral Takedown of ‘Healing Hustle’ After Maureen Galindo Controversy*

**Body:**

In the wake of the Maureen Galindo scandal—where the influencer was accused of monetizing her followers’ pain through performative vulnerability—life coach **Dr. Simone Hart** has gone viral for a raw, unfiltered rebuke of the “healing hustle.”

“Maureen isn’t the outlier. She’s the symptom of a culture that turns scars into currency,” Hart said in a clip now viewed 4.2 million times. “If your ‘breakthrough’ requires you to buy a course, follow a guru, or perform your trauma for a stranger’s likes—you are not healing. You are leasing your pain.”

Hart’s advice for those triggered by the scandal? **“Stop measuring your worth by how fast you bounce back. Real growth doesn’t go viral. It goes quiet, private, and profound. The only person who needs to validate your journey is you—not a comment section, and certainly not a guru with a grid of curated breakdowns.”**

Her message has sparked a wave of #SlowHealing posts, with users ditching the “glow-up” pressure for what Hart calls “the messy, unsexy work of just being okay.”

“You don’t owe anyone your healed version. You owe yourself permission to take your time.”
