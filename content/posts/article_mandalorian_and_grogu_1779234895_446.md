**Subject Line: The Mandalorian’s $0 Salary Revenge**

**The Story:**
In a move that cratered the stock of struggling streaming rival *Disney+*, *The Mandalorian & Grogu* just confirmed its theatrical release strategy: **zero upfront streaming rights**. The film will bypass Disney+ for a minimum of 12 months post-release, forcing a $30M+ P&A spend from competitors for a single window—effectively turning Grogu into the most expensive child in Hollywood.

**Why It Matters:**
This isn't a movie release. It’s a cash-flow trap. By starving the linear streaming ecosystem, Disney forces rivals to bid on a single launch window or watch subscribers bleed to the big screen. Estimated impact: $200M+ in incremental box office pressure on Q4 2025 earnings. Paramount and Warner Bros. Discovery just lost sleep.

**The Lesson:**
When your IP is a cultural nuke, you don't split the bomb. You hold the trigger and charge rent.
