**Viral News Snippet:**
**"Did Blackwater’s Only Convicted Killer Jenny Slatten Just Get a Secret Pardon? Internet Conspiracy Explodes"**

*Fact-check: This is FALSE. There is no evidence of a pardon or release.*

A viral post circulating on X and TikTok claims that former Blackwater guard Nicholas "Jenny" Slatten—the only contractor convicted of murder for the 2007 Nisour Square massacre in Baghdad—has been granted a "quiet, secret pardon" by the Biden administration. The post, viewed over 2 million times, includes a manipulated screenshot of a supposed Justice Department memo.

**The Reality:**
No such pardon exists. Slatten is currently serving a life sentence at a federal penitentiary in Colorado. The rumor appears to stem from confusion over a 2020 commutation granted by President Trump to other Blackwater contractors, which Slatten was explicitly excluded from because his conviction was for first-degree murder, not voluntary manslaughter. Legal experts note that any pardon would require a public Federal Register notice. The White House has not commented, and no credible news outlets have reported a release. **Verdict: Fake.**
