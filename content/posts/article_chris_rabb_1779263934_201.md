**Chris Rabb to local drivers: "Use your damn mirrors!" - Angry resident's rant goes viral**

**Chris Rabb (no relation to the politician) is officially fed up.** The Kensington local took to the *Kensington & Fishtown Neighbors United* Facebook group this morning to vent about the near-daily gridlock on Amber Street, and his post is already clocking over 8K shares.

"Common sense, people. It's not a complex concept," Rabb wrote alongside a dashcam clip of a minivan blocking the entire intersection because the driver refused to inch forward to make room for the turning bus. "You have side mirrors. You have a rearview mirror. Look in them. If you see a 40-foot SEPTA bus trying to make a corner and you're sitting there scrolling Instagram while blocking two lanes of traffic, you are the problem. Not the bus. Not the city. **You.** "

The post exploded, with over 400 comments in less than an hour. Many hailed Rabb as a "local hero," while others accused him of "public shaming."

Rabb isn't backing down. In a follow-up comment, he added: "This isn't about being mean. This is about being a functional member of society. Pull your car over. Let the bus turn. Then go about your day. It's literally kindergarten-level sharing."
