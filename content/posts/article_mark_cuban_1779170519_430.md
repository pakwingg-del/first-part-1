**Viral News Snippet: Mark Cuban Ditches the Rulebook**

**Headline:** **Mark Cuban: "Company Culture is a Myth. I’m Paying for Speed."**

**The Gist:** In a blunt interview, billionaire investor and *Shark Tank* alum Mark Cuban declared that most startup advice on "culture" is wasted time. He argued the only two metrics that matter are *speed of execution* and *profit per employee*.

**Why It’s Viral:** He claims the "vibe" of a company is a luxury for failing enterprises. "If you are moving fast enough to kill your competition, you don't have time for a ping-pong table," Cuban said.

**The Bottom Line for CEOs:** Cuban is betting that in a tight economy, loyalty is dead. He wants a lean, high-velocity team that can pivot in 24 hours—not a "family" that burns cash on perks.
