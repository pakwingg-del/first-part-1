**Top 5 Things You Need to Know About the San Diego Shooting**

- **The Scene:** Gunfire erupted late Tuesday evening in the Gaslamp Quarter, a normally bustling nightlife hub, sending hundreds of panicked revelers running for cover as multiple shooters opened fire near a popular concert venue.

- **The Victims:** At least four people are confirmed injured, with two in critical condition, though police say the number could rise. Witnesses describe chaotic scenes of patrons diving behind cars and barricading themselves inside bars.

- **The Suspects:** A high-speed chase has been underway since the shooting, with police sources confirming a vehicle linked to the suspects was found abandoned just north of the city. At least two shooters are believed to be involved, and they remain at large.

- **Motive Unknown:** The investigation is in its earliest stages. Unlike recent targeted incidents, this appears to be the result of a violent dispute spilling out of a private event, with no evidence of terrorism or hate crime intent so far.

- **What’s Next:** The entire Gaslamp Quarter remains cordoned off as a crime scene, and a citywide tactical alert has been declared. Police are urging downtown residents to shelter in place and report any suspicious activity as the manhunt intensifies.
