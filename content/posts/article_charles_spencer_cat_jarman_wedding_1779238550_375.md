**London, UK — 15:00 GMT**

A significant social event has been formally concluded, as Mr. Charles Spencer, the 9th Earl Spencer, has married author and television personality Catherine "Cat" Jarman.

**WHY:** The wedding marks the culmination of the couple's relationship, which had been made public in 2023 following the Earl's separation from his third wife, Karen Spencer. The divorce was finalized earlier this year.

**CONTEXT:** This is the fourth marriage for the Earl and the second for Ms. Jarman.
