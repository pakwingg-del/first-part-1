**HEADLINE: MILLIONS TO LOSE ACA COVERAGE IN SNEAK ATTACK ON YOUR WALLET: The “Subsidy Cliff” is Here**

**THE SNEAK ATTACK ON YOUR HEALTH BUDGET**
If you are one of the millions who signed up for a $0 or low-premium Obamacare plan thanks to the enhanced subsidies from the Inflation Reduction Act, your monthly bill is about to triple. The “subsidy cliff” is back by default unless Congress acts, and the clock is ticking.

**WHAT THIS MEANS FOR YOUR WALLET**
- **The Price Jump:** If you earn just $1 above 400% of the federal poverty level (about $60,000 for a family of two), you lose the subsidy. That $50 plan? It becomes $700 next month.
- **The “Family Glitch”:** Employers are quietly dropping coverage or making plans unaffordable, pushing workers onto the marketplace—just as subsidies vanish.
- **The Hidden Fee:** Even if your income is low, states are adding invisible “health plan taxes” to your monthly premium to cover COVID-era unpaid claims.

**THE RETALIATION MOVE**
Don’t wait for the government to fix this. Here’s how to fight back:
1. **Recalculate now:** An extra side hustle or a raise could cost you thousands in lost subsidies. Defer that income if possible.
2. **Short-term plan trap:** Avoid short-term plans. They look cheap but have “lifetime limits” and exclude pre-existing conditions. One ER visit = bankruptcy.
3. **The loophole:** Use a Health Savings Account (HSA) to pay premiums tax-free if you switch to a high-deductible plan this open enrollment.

The real cost? If you lose coverage and get sick, one hospital stay can drain your savings—and your credit score will tank.
