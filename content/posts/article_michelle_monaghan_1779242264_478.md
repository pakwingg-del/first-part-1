**Viral News Snippet:**

**FACT CHECK: Did Michelle Monaghan refuse to work with Tom Cruise on 'Mission: Impossible 8' because of his 'Scientology views'?**

**Rating: FALSE**

**The Claim:** A viral post circulating on Facebook and X (formerly Twitter) claims actress Michelle Monaghan, who played Julia Meade-Hunt in the *Mission: Impossible* franchise, has publicly refused to return for the eighth installment, citing a "moral objection" to star Tom Cruise's involvement with the Church of Scientology.

**The Reality:** There is zero evidence to support this claim. Michelle Monaghan has not made any public statements about Scientology or refusing to work with Tom Cruise. Production sources confirm her character was *never written* into the script for *Mission: Impossible – The Final Reckoning*. Monaghan last appeared in *Fallout* (2018) via a cameo, and her exit from the main storyline was a creative decision made by director Christopher McQuarrie years ago.

**Why the rumor?** This appears to be a fabricated story designed to capitalize on renewed online debate about Scientology following recent documentaries. A fan-edited quote falsely attributed to a "2024 interview with *The Guardian*" is the sole source—an interview that never happened. Monaghan has repeatedly praised Cruise, calling him "one of the most professional and generous actors" she's ever worked with.

**Verdict: FAKE.** There is no feud, no refusal, and no moral objection. The real reason Monaghan isn't in the new film is simple: the story doesn't call for her character.
