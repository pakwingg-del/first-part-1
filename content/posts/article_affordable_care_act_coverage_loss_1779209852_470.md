🚨 **BREAKING: 3.2 Million Americans Secretly Unenrolled Under ACA by Data ‘Glitch’? Here’s What We Know** 🚨

**Viral Claim:** A TikTok video posted Tuesday claims that the Biden administration is hiding a “massive purge” of Affordable Care Act enrollees. The user alleges that 3.2 million people have lost coverage in 2024 due to a “secret algorithm glitch” and that the White House will only admit to it *after* Election Day.

**The Reality Check:**
- **FAKE/Exaggerated.** The statistic is taken out of context.
- The *actual* number (approx. 2.7 million) refers to people who lost Medicaid over the past year—not ACA marketplace plans—and this is due to the **“Medicaid Unwinding,”** a legally required process (established *before* the Biden administration) to re-check eligibility after the COVID-19 public health emergency ended.
- **The “glitch” is real—but not a conspiracy.** A small number of people (estimated in the *thousands*, not millions) experienced a system error with income calculations in a few states (like Texas and Florida). The administration has already fixed it and extended deadlines.
- **Verdict: FALSE.** No evidence of a 3.2 million mass purge. The viral claim blends a real bureaucratic headache (the unenrollment process) with a fabricated number and a cover-up narrative.

**Bottom Line:** Millions *are* losing coverage, but it’s a planned administrative reset, not a hidden scandal. If you see this TikTok, know the numbers don’t add up. 👩‍⚖️📉
