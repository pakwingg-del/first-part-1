**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**Kim Kardashian West Launches Global Legal Reform Initiative: "The Justice Project 2.0"**

**LOCATION:** Washington, D.C.
**DATE:** October 26, 2023

**WHO:** Businesswoman and media personality Kim Kardashian West, in conjunction with a bipartisan coalition of lawmakers and non-profit organizations.

**WHAT:** Ms. Kardashian West formally announced the launch of "The Justice Project 2.0," a comprehensive, multi-million dollar initiative aimed at reforming the United States criminal justice system. The program will focus on three primary areas: funding for pro-bono legal representation for non-violent offenders, technology-driven training for re-entry into the workforce, and bipartisan lobbying for the elimination of mandatory minimum sentencing for certain federal drug offenses.

**WHERE:** The announcement was made at a press conference held at the National Press Club in Washington, D.C.

**WHEN:** The press conference occurred at 10:00 AM Eastern Standard Time this morning. The initiative is expected to begin immediate deployment of resources across twelve pilot states, including California, Texas, and Ohio.

**WHY:** Ms. Kardashian West cited a "moral imperative" to address what she described as persistent racial and economic disparities within the legal system. She presented data indicating that over 40% of incarcerated individuals in the pilot states are serving sentences for non-violent drug offenses, and she argued that the current system disproportionately impacts communities of color. The initiative officially designates a $50 million fund for legal scholarships and re-entry programs over the next five years.
