**BREAKING NEWS: NEW POLL SHOWS ROY COOPER HOLDING NARROW LEAD OVER MICHAEL WHATLEY IN HYPOTHETICAL MATCHUP**

**Location:** Raleigh, North Carolina  
**Date:** November 22, 2024  

**Who:** Democratic Governor Roy Cooper and former RNC Chairman Michael Whatley.

**When:** The survey was conducted between November 14 and November 20, 2024.

**Where:** Statewide in North Carolina.

**Why:** The poll comes amid intense speculation regarding Governor Cooper's potential Senate bid, following the announcement of Senator Tillis's retirement. Political analysts note that Cooper’s slight lead, while within the margin of error, underscores his continued popularity in a pivotal swing state. However, Whatley’s strong base of support among conservative voters could narrow the gap as the campaign progresses.

**Key Findings:** Governor Cooper received 47% support, while Michael Whatley garnered 44%, with 9% of voters undecided. The poll also highlighted that 52% of respondents identified the economy as their top priority, ahead of education and healthcare.
