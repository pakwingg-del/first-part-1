**Top 5 Things You Need to Know About Thom Tillis**

- **The Senate's "Deal-Maker in Chief"** : North Carolina Senator Thom Tillis is known as one of the most bipartisan members of the Senate. He frequently breaks rank to forge compromises on immigration, gun safety, and infrastructure, making him a key swing vote in a polarized chamber.

- **A Key Architect of the 2022 Gun Safety Bill** : Tillis was a co-author of the Bipartisan Safer Communities Act, the first major federal gun safety legislation in nearly 30 years. The bill enhances background checks for buyers under 21 and provides funding for red flag laws, earning praise from both sides but criticism from some conservatives.

- **Immigration Hardliner Turned Pragmatist** : Once a staunch immigration hawk, Tillis has evolved into a pragmatist. He co-led the bipartisan border security talks in 2024, proposing stricter asylum rules alongside Ukraine aid—proving he's willing to anger his base for a deal.

- **The Tar Heel State's Electoral Power Broker** : Tillis is the go-to GOP strategist for Senate races in North Carolina. He helped recruit and elect conservative candidates, but his moderate style sometimes puts him at odds with the state’s Trump-aligned faction, keeping him in the crosshairs of primary challenges.

- **Controversial Friend of Big Tech** : Tillis has drawn fire for his cozy ties to Silicon Valley. He's a top recipient of Big Tech PAC money and has led efforts to restrict Section 230 liability while also blocking antitrust crackdowns, leading progressive critics to dub him "Big Tech's Favorite Senator."
