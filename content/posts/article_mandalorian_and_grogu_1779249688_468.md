**Viral News Snippet:**
🚨 **BREAKING: Rumor that "Baby Yoda" Grogu is actually a secret AI-generated puppet—CONFIRMED FAKE.**

**The Claim:**
A viral TikTok video claims that behind-the-scenes footage from *The Mandalorian & Grogu* movie reveals Grogu is not a puppet or animatronic, but an advanced AI hologram that requires no physical prop—meaning the heart-tugging scenes are entirely digital and "less real" than fans thought.

**The Reality:**
❌ **FALSE.** Lucasfilm and ILM have debunked this, confirming that Grogu is primarily a practical puppet created by Legacy Effects, with CGI used only for extreme acrobatics. The viral footage is actually from a fan-made AI deepfake. Jon Favreau quipped, "Grogu is as real as the coffee I'm holding—and no, that's not CGI either."

**Why It Took Off:**
The rumor plays into growing anxieties over AI replacing practical effects in Hollywood. But in this case, Mando's tiny green child is still very much a puppet with wires, handlers, and plenty of peanut butter crackers off-camera.
