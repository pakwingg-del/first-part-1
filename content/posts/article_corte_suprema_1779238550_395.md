**Top 5 things you need to know about the Supreme Court's latest blockbuster decision**

📋 **The Ruling is a Landmark Shift:** In a 6-3 decision, the court has fundamentally redefined the scope of executive power, limiting the president's ability to issue unilateral regulations without explicit congressional approval. This is the biggest curtailment of the administrative state in decades.

⚖️ **The "Chevron" Doctrine is Effectively Dead:** The court essentially overturned the landmark Chevron deference principle. Now, federal courts—not government agencies—will have the final say on what ambiguous laws mean. This shifts power from bureaucrats to judges.

🗓️ **It Kills a Major Climate & Tech Agenda:** Experts say this ruling immediately hollows out pending regulations on carbon emissions from power plants and online content moderation. Any agency interpretation that wasn't codified by a law is now vulnerable to legal challenge.

🧑‍⚖️ **The Immediate Fallout is Chaos:** Lower courts are expected to be flooded with hundreds of lawsuits challenging existing rules on everything from student loans to worker safety. Legal experts predict a period of "regulatory uncertainty" that could slow down the economy for months.

🔮 **Congress is Now the Only Game in Town:** This decision forces lawmakers to write much more detailed and specific laws, or risk having policies blocked in court. If Congress remains gridlocked, the government's ability to address modern problems (e.g., AI, online fraud, environmental crises) could be significantly hampered.
