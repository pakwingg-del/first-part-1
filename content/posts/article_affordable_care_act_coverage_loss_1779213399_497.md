**🚨 BREAKING: MILLIONS COULD LOSE HEALTH COVERAGE—AND YOUR WALLET IS NEXT 🚨**

If you’re one of the 21 million Americans on Affordable Care Act plans, listen up: A massive coverage “unraveling” is coming, and it could cost *you* thousands.

**The Sitch:** The federal “enhanced subsidies” that made plans dirt-cheap (some as low as $10/month) are set to expire at the end of 2025. If Congress doesn’t act, your premiums could **skyrocket 70% or more**. 

**Your Wallet at Risk:** 
- A family earning $75k/year could see their monthly bill jump from $200 to **$800+**. 
- 4 million people are expected to simply drop coverage (too expensive), turning emergencies into **six-figure medical debt**. 
- Small business owners and gig workers? You’re hit hardest—no employer safety net, just a giant price tag.

**The Kicker:** Insurers are *already* hiking backup plans. One third of all ACA enrollees could be uninsured by 2026. 

**What You Can Do:** 
- ⏳ **Brace for open enrollment** (Nov 2025). Get quotes NOW to see the cliff coming. 
- 📢 **Call your reps**—tell them “keep subsidies or my budget breaks.” 
- 💡 **Explore cheaper alternatives**: Short-term plans, HSAs, or Medicaid if your income dips.

**Bottom Line:** This isn’t a political fight—it’s a **personal finance crisis**. Your health insurance could soon cost more than your rent.
