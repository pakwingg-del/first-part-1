**CLAIM:** Mountain Dew is bringing back White Out nationwide in January 2024, with a "Mystery Flavor" rebrand that tastes like a mix of original and Baja Blast.

**REAL vs. FAKE:** This is **FAKE**. As of the current date, Mountain Dew has made *no* official announcement regarding a nationwide re-release or rebranding of White Out. The 2024 Dewcision contest allowed fans to vote for the return of an old flavor, but that vote was for *Pitch Black*, which was discontinued shortly after its limited return. A fake viral graphic circulating on X (Twitter) and Facebook, using a faux Walmart inventory screen and a manufactured "Dew Insider" Press Release, has duped thousands of fans. The text in the image even misspelled "White Out" as "White Outt." Always check the official Mountain Dew website (MountainDew.com) or their verified corporate X account (@MountainDew) before getting your hopes up.
