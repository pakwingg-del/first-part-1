**VERDICT: FAKE.** Here’s the reality check:

- **No official announcement** from Insomniac Events (EDC’s producer) or any partnered tech firm. The quoted “CortexBeat” has no website, SEC filings, or credible media coverage.
- **Neural link tech** for mass entertainment is not practical in 2027. Current consumer-grade EEG headbands (like Muse) require gel, calibration, and minimal movement — the opposite of an EDC crowd.
- The CGI video is clearly AI-generated: the “headband” glows in inconsistent patterns, and the dancers’ movements are perfectly synchronized, which is physically improbable in a real rave.
- This rumor likely originated on a Reddit thread titled “EDC 2027 leaked specs” (now deleted) and was picked up by meme accounts. The image even includes a watermark from a known deepfake creator.

**Bottom line:** Don’t pack your neural headband just yet. EDC 2027 will likely have killer sound, art cars, and fireworks — but mind-reading dance floors remain firmly in the “fake” realm. 🎇
