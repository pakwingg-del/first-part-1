**🚨 CONSUMER ALERT: Jake Shane’s “Humble” Comeback Could Cost YOU Real Money**  

We all love a redemption story, but **Jake Shane’s** latest move has consumer watchdogs raising red flags. The influencer—once known for luxury hauls—just launched a line of “budget-friendly” home goods. Sounds great, right?

And that “limited-time” bundle deal? It auto-enrolls you in a monthly subscription—with **hidden restocking fees**.
