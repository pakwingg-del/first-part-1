**SUBJECT LINE: Google Just Killed the Smartphone App**  

Google’s new Gemini Omni model does what Apple and Meta couldn't: make the phone a true AI agent. No more swiping, tapping, or browsing. Speak a command, and it acts across apps, emails, calendars, and even live video feeds—without a single GUI call.  

**Why it matters:**  
- User sticks to one “app”: the assistant.  
- Third-party developers lose direct-to-consumer touch.  
- Google bypasses the traditional app store economy entirely.  

**Bottom line:** This is the end of the smartphone app marketplace as we know it. The next trillion-dollar battle isn’t search—it’s *agent dominance*. Google just fired the first shot.
