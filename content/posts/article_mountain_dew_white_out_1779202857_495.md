**The Dew is Drying Up.**
PepsiCo has officially pulled the plug on Mountain Dew White Out, and your next 12-pack just got a whole lot more expensive.

**Why This Hits Your Wallet:**
For years, White Out was the budget-friendly citrus alternative. Now, with its removal, resellers are already flooding eBay and Facebook Marketplace with *overpriced* cases. We’ve spotted 12-packs listed for **$85**—a 400% markup. If you’re a fan, you’re either paying the scalper tax or switching to the remaining flavors, which means less competition and higher prices across the board.

Grocery stores are already adjusting. With one less SKU on the shelf, remaining limited-edition Dews (like VooDew or Baja Blast) are seeing price bumps of 15-20% in secondary markets. Expect your next convenience store run to feel a little lighter in the pocket.
