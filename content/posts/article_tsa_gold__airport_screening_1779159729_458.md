**Viral News Snippet:**

🚨 **BREAKING: TSA Gold+ Program Leaked – Skip Every Line for $1,200 a Year?** 🚨

A leaked internal memo is setting the internet ablaze this morning, claiming the Transportation Security Administration is piloting a new "TSA Gold+" tier that would allow members to bypass *all* airport screening—including metal detectors and body scanners—for a premium fee of $1,200 annually. The rumor, spread by a viral TikTok user claiming to be a former TSA agent, alleges that Gold+ holders would simply walk through a separate "verified identity" lane where AI facial recognition matches them to a pre-vetted database, eliminating the need for bag checks or pat-downs.

**Verdict: FALSE.** 🛑

The TSA has officially denied the existence of any "Gold+" program, calling the memo a "fabricated hoax." While real programs like TSA PreCheck and CLEAR already expedite screening (for $78–$189/year), *no* current program allows passengers to skip physical security measures entirely. TSA spokesperson Lisa Farbstein confirmed: "All travelers, regardless of status, must pass through some form of screening. This claim is categorically false."

So before you grab your wallet, remember: The only thing getting fast-tracked here is misinformation. ✈️❌
