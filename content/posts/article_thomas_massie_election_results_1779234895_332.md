Fast forward 220 years: Massie’s district is the equivalent of that rugged frontier. His "Liberty" voting record (alone against party lines on farm bills, surveillance, and debt) mirrors the **Lone Explorer** archetype—dependent on local support (Kentucky voters) but cut off from the supply chain of establishment funding. Win today proves he still has the *guides*—the rural voters who trade trust for independence. Lose, and he’d be the explorer who ran out of bullets.

Key parallel: When Lewis failed to secure backup from the Mandan tribe, his expedition stalled. Massie just proved his tribal ties in his district are stronger than D.C.’s maps.
