Call me old-fashioned, but if it's not ready, don't sell it. And for the love of all that is holy, just say "October 12th at 6 PM" like a normal person. 

So, to answer the question: Forza Horizon 6 comes out when common sense does. And we all know that's been delayed indefinitely.
