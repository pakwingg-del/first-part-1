**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**HEADLINE:** National Coffee Chain to Offer Free Beverage on May 19; Details Confirmed

**LOCATION:** Nationwide, United States

**DATELINE:** May 12, 2025

**SOURCE:** Dunkin’ Brands Corporate Communications

**SUMMARY:**

In an announcement that is generating significant consumer interest, officials from the multinational coffee and doughnut company, Dunkin’, have confirmed a nationwide promotional event scheduled for Monday, May 19, 2025. The promotion, dubbed "National Free Beverage Day," will offer customers one complimentary medium hot or iced coffee.

**DETAILS:**

The event is structured as follows:

**WHO:** All active members of the Dunkin’ Rewards loyalty program are eligible. Customers not currently enrolled may sign up via the official mobile application or website prior to the offer date.

**WHAT:** Participants will receive a free medium-sized coffee. This includes the chain’s signature Original Blend hot coffee or a medium iced coffee. According to the official terms, the offer applies exclusively to hot and iced coffee; specialty beverages, such as lattes, cold brew, or frozen drinks, are excluded from this promotion.

**WHEN:** The offer is valid for one use per eligible Rewards member on Monday, May 19, 2025, during regular store operating hours.

**WHERE:** The redemption is limited to participating Dunkin’ locations across the United States. The company advises that certain franchise-operated outlets may enforce independent terms, and the promotion is not available at restaurants in airports, stadiums, or other non-standard venues.

**WHY:** A spokesperson for the brand cited the initiative as a "celebration of the community’s loyalty as we transition into the summer season." The event is also interpreted by industry analysts as a strategic move to bolster app downloads and customer data acquisition through the Rewards platform.
