**HEADLINE:** Salesforce CEO Admits the "Revenue Miracle" is Over – And Your Next Bill is About to Get Ugly

**The Scoop:**

In a bombshell earnings call that sent shockwaves through Wall Street, Marc Benioff dropped the facade: "The era of endless software discounts is over. We’re entering the 'Value Harvest.'"

**What This Means for YOUR Wallet:**

If you or your employer use Salesforce, Slack, or Tableau, your monthly subscription is about to spike. Benioff explicitly stated the company will now "aggressively price for AI add-ons" that were previously included for free. Translation: That $50-per-seat plan you’re on? Expect a mandatory $15-$25 "AI Copilot" surcharge by Q3.

**The Dark Reality:**

Small businesses are the first victims. The "Revenue Miracle" Benioff referenced was the pandemic-era boom of free trials and low-cost contracts. Now, to please investors, he’s pulling the plug. One leaked internal memo seen by this outlet warns sales teams to "retire all grandfathered pricing" by July 1st.

**The Viral Takeaway:**

Don’t click that "accept new terms" email. If you’re a consumer using a free tier of Slack – prepare for limits. If you run a business using Salesforce – start budgeting 20% more, or start migrating now. As one analyst on the call put it: "Benioff just rang the bell for the software price war of 2024. The only question is: Can your budget survive the new 'value'?"
