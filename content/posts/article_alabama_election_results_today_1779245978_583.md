"The Founding Fathers are rolling in their graves," said Dr. Amelia Cross, a prominent moral critic and civil liberties scholar. "We have officially crossed the line from 'In God We Trust' as a passive motto to actively inserting a political weapon in the hands of children. This isn't faith—it's a loyalty test. It tells every Jewish, Muslim, Hindu, and agnostic child that they are not fully American."

The text of the approved statute reads: *"To honor the Almighty's covenant with this land, the Yeshua-Cross flag shall be displayed as a symbol of righteous governance."*

Critics warn this is the logical endpoint of a decade-long trend: replacing civic unity with tribal blood-signal. We didn't see it coming because it came wrapped in a cross."

As of press time, the ACLU has already filed an emergency injunction, calling the law "an unconstitutional establishment of religion and a direct assault on the First Amendment."
