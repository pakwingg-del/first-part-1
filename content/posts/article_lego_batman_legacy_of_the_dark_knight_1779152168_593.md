**LEGO FANS OUTRAGED AS NEW 'BATMAN: LEGACY OF THE DARK KNIGHT' SETS INCLUDE "ETHICAL DILEMMA" CHALLENGE CARDS**

In a move that has parents and cultural watchdogs up in arms, the LEGO Group has announced its latest expansion for the *Lego Batman* line: *Legacy of the Dark Knight*. While fans expected a simple tribute to the Caped Crusader, the set instead ships with a deck of "Moral Choice Cards" that force children to roleplay the Joker’s perspective and justify his crimes.

The controversy erupted when a leaked instruction booklet showed card #7: "The Joker thinks Batman is the real villain. In three sentences, build a LEGO scene that proves the Joker is right."

"This isn't play—this is propaganda," said Dr. Patricia Hume, a child psychologist and outspoken critic. "We are teaching our kids that moral relativism is a toy. First, we deconstruct the hero. Next, we normalize the villain's worldview. LEGO has officially jumped the shark, and society is paying the price."

Furious parents have taken to social media, burning build manuals and vowing to boycott the brand. Conservative commentator Gerald Stone called it "the final nail in the coffin for Western values," while one viral TikTok shows a mother dismantling the $150 "Arkham Asylum Takeover" set with a hammer, declaring, "My son will not be building empathy for terrorists."

LEGO has yet to comment, but insiders whisper the cards were designed by a "progressive think tank" to encourage "critical thinking." Critics argue the only thing being built here is a generation that can’t tell the difference between a hero and a killer.
