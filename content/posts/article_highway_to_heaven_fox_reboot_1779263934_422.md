**TOP 5 THINGS YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT THE *HIGHWAY TO HEAVEN* FOX REBOOT**

*   **The Core Premise Gets a Modern Twist:** The 2025 reboot keeps the heart of the original—a mysterious angel helping people in crisis—but updates the setting. **Mark-Paul Gosselaar** stars as a cynical, modern-day "Angel" who doesn't remember his past lives, forced to navigate a world of social media drama, viral scandals, and climate anxiety to earn his wings (and his memories).
*   **It's a Generational Buddy Story:** The show isn't a solo act. The new *Highway* pairs Gosselaar's hesitant angel with a grounded, **Gen Z human partner**—a brilliant but burnt-out video journalist who only believes in what she can fact-check. Their dynamic is pure "Millennial meets Zoomer meets celestial being," creating the show's emotional core and comedic friction.
*   **The "Case of the Week" Formula is Back (But Fiercer):** Each episode sends the duo to a different small town to solve a specific crisis. Think a bankrupt family farm, a viral conspiracy ruining a local teacher, or a community torn apart by a hydro-fracking debate. The stories are smaller in scale, but the stakes are **emotionally massive**, with a clear "invisible hand" (hint: God's) nudging them into place.
*   **Expect Major Easter Eggs and a Legacy Character:** Fox knows the original has a cult following. **Victor French's spirit** is heavily rumored to appear in a flashback or as a guiding voice. The reboot also promises a "reveal" in the pilot that connects the new Angel to Jonathan Smith in a way that will make long-time fans gasp.
