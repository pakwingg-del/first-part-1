**TRENDING: Life Coach Says Kroger Crouton Recall Is a "Wake-Up Call" for Your Boundaries**

As news spreads that Kroger has recalled its Garlic Cheese Croutons over potential undeclared allergens, one life coach is going viral for reframing the crisis as a metaphor for sabotaged self-worth.

"The soft recall message, 'may contain undeclared milk,' is exactly what happens when you let people into your life who don't declare their true intentions," says certified life coach Dr. Mara Kline in a clip that's racked up 3 million views. "You think you're getting a safe, crunchy topping. Instead, you get inflammation, confusion, and a trip to the ER of heartbreak."

Kline's advice? "Check your pantry. Check your circle. That 'harmless' addition to your life—a colleague, a partner, a habit—might be causing a reaction you're ignoring. The recall isn't about cheese. It's about learning to read your own labels before you consume someone else's energy."

The viral moment has sparked a hashtag: #CroutonBoundaries.
