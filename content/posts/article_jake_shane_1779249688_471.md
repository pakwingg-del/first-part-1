**The Claim:** A grainy 15-second clip currently circulating on X (formerly Twitter) and TikTok shows influencer Jake Shane (27) mid-sentence during a Tuesday morning "Get Ready With Me" livestream. According to the viral audio, Shane suddenly freezes, whispers "They found me," and then the camera cuts to static before the feed goes completely dark for 90 seconds. The account hosting the clip claims Shane has not been seen or heard from since.

**Our Rating:** ❌ **FAKE (Digitally Manipulated)**

**The Reality Check:**

- **The Missing 90 Seconds:** The "outage" was actually a planned technical break during a sponsored stream for a mattress company. Shane’s feed returned with him lying on a new bed, saying, "I told you guys I was tired." The "Men in Black" audio was deep-faked using an AI voice clone found on a fan-created SoundCloud page.
- **Jake’s Response:** Despite being tagged over 3,000 times, Shane addressed the rumor at 11:40 PM EST the same night. He posted a selfie holding a burrito with the caption: “I’m not missing, I’m just a guy who disappears when my burrito gets here. That was a joke from a month ago. Stop calling the cops, my mom is embarrassed.”
