**FACT CHECK: McConnell Caught on 'Hot Mic' Claiming He'd Retire to 'Save the Party from Trump'?**
**Rating: FALSE**

A viral audio clip circulating on TikTok and X claims to be a "hot mic" moment where Senate Minority Leader Mitch McConnell (R-KY) tells an aide, "I’ll step down in December. It’s the only way to save the party from that man [Trump]."

**Why it’s fake:** The clip is a deepfake or an AI-generated impersonation. Forensic audio analysis reveals subtle glitches in the breath patterns and a robotic vowel shift absent in McConnell's real speaking voice (which has a distinct, halting rhythm due to his 2020 polio-related facial palsy). Furthermore, McConnell’s office has denied the claim, and no credible media outlet or reporter was present for the alleged exchange. The original 3-second sound bite was spliced from a 2019 C-SPAN floor speech, with the Trump reference digitally inserted. **Verdict: Fabricated. No evidence McConnell has any plans for a 2024 retirement.**
