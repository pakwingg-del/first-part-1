**BREAKING NEWS: FIRST LOOK AT ‘THE MANDALORIAN & GROGU’ FILM CONFIRMED DURING INDUSTRY EVENT**

**Who:** Actor Pedro Pascal (voice of Din Djarin) and Lucasfilm executives.

**What:** The first official footage and production details for the upcoming feature film, *The Mandalorian & Grogu*, were unveiled.

**Where:** The announcement took place at the Star Wars Celebration Europe convention in London.

**When:** Footage was released on Friday, April 11, 2025.

**Why:** The feature film will serve as a continuation of the critically acclaimed Disney+ series, bridging the gap between the television show’s third season and the next major chapter in the Star Wars timeline.

**How:** According to director Jon Favreau, the movie will utilize virtual production technology pioneered on the series, featuring a larger-scale narrative focused on the bounty hunter’s struggle to protect the Force-sensitive child, Grogu, from a new Imperial remnant threat. The film is slated for a theatrical release on May 22, 2026.
