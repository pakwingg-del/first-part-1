**Rumor:** Former LAPD detective Mark Fuhrman has been secretly consulting on the controversial Karen Read murder case in Massachusetts.

**Reality: FALSE.**

Despite a flurry of viral TikTok videos and a Facebook post shared over 50,000 times claiming Fuhrman—known for his role in the O.J. Simpson case—is advising the defense, no court records, official case filings, or credible news reports confirm his involvement. Representatives for both the Norfolk County District Attorney’s office and Karen Read’s defense team have explicitly denied the claim. The rumor appears to have originated from a satirical blog post that was stripped of its "satire" disclaimer before being shared widely. Fuhrman himself has made no public statement about the case.
