- **It’s a Theatrical Feature Film, Not a Season 4.**
  Forget the small screen. This is a full-blown, theatrical event titled *The Mandalorian & Grogu* (directed by Jon Favreau). It’s set to hit cinemas, not Disney+, marking the first feature film in the franchise since *2019*.

- **The Timeline is a Huge Spoiler.**
  The official word? This movie takes place *during* the events of *The Mandalorian* (presumably after Season 3). That means the war for Mandalore, the Armorer’s decree, and the foundling hunt are all still in play.

  The *Alien* legend has joined the cast in an undisclosed role—but insiders whisper she’s playing a "shadowy Imperial remnant" figure. Expect a cold, calculating villain with zero tolerance for cuddly Jedi in training.
