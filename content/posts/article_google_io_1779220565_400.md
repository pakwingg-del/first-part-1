**TOP 5 THINGS YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT GOOGLE I/O 2024**

- **AI is Taking Over Search:** Forget just links. Google is rolling out "AI Overviews" to everyone in the US. Now, when you ask a complex question, you get a full, multi-step AI-generated answer right at the top of the results, complete with shopping links and planning tools. No more clicking through 10 tabs.

- **Project Astra is Here to Watch You:** In a wild demo, Google showed off a real-time, always-listening AI assistant called Project Astra. It can “see” through your phone camera, identify objects (like a broken speaker part), and tell you exactly how to fix it. It remembers where you left your glasses, too. Creepy? Useful? Both.

- **Gemini is Coming for Your Chats:** Google is embedding its massive Gemini model directly into Gmail and Google Messages. The biggest reveal? "Ask Photos"—a new feature where you can literally say "Show me my car’s license plate from last month," and Gemini will scan your entire photo library to find it. No more scrolling.

- **Detect Fraud with AI Calls:** A terrifyingly helpful feature: Gemini will soon be able to listen to *live* phone calls and alert you if it detects a scammer’s script (like someone pretending to be a bank). It acts as a real-time warning system for the call you’re already on.

- **Android is Getting a Theft Lockdown:** Your phone will now know if someone snatches it and tries to run, bike, or drive away. Using motion sensors and AI, it will automatically lock the screen, keeping thieves out of your apps and data before they can even turn off the internet.
