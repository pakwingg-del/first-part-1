**VERIFIED: FALSE**

**Claim:** A viral video shows Chris Rabb, a Pennsylvania state representative, using taxpayer funds to install a gold-plated toilet in his Harrisburg office.

**Fact-Check:** The video circulating on social media is fabricated using AI-generated footage. A reverse-image search reveals the clip is spliced from a 2019 parody video by a satire account, originally titled "Politician Purchases Golden Throne." Rabb’s official office confirmed to multiple news outlets that no such purchase was made, and his office remains “standard-issue, complete with a standard-issue $300 toilet.” The Pennsylvania Department of General Services also verified that no taxpayer funds were used for any "non-essential luxury fixtures." **Bottom line: Fake.**
