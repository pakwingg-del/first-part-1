**Verdict: FAKE**

**Viral Snippet:** "Breaking: Astronaut Jake Shane, who was part of the 2024 Polaris Dawn crew, has been officially diagnosed with 'Space-Time Regression Syndrome' after returning to Earth. Doctors say his cells are literally aging in reverse—he is now biologically 12 years old and is expected to speak at Comic-Con next month."

**Reality Check:**
This is entirely fabricated. "Jake Shane" is a fictional persona (a popular TikToker/comedian known for his "Therapuss" character and ancient mythology humor). He has never been an astronaut. The Polaris Dawn mission was a real SpaceX flight (September 2024), but crew members included Jared Isaacman, Scott Poteet, Sarah Gillis, and Anna Menon—not Jake Shane. No verified medical condition called "Space-Time Regression Syndrome" exists. The story was likely generated by mixing the name of a real internet personality with a real space mission for absurdist humor, which then got clipped and shared out of context.
