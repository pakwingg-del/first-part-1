## HE GSETH STORMS KENTUCKY: "The Pentagon Is Broken. We’re Fixing It."

**LEXINGTON, KY** — In a high-octane rally that electrified the Bluegrass State, former Fox News host and veteran Pete Hegseth launched his Kentucky campaign blitz with a blunt, battle-ready message: “The Pentagon is broken. We’re fixing it.”
