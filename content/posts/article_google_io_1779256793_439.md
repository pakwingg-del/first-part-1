**HEADLINE: Google Kills Search as We Know It – CEO Sundar Pichai Fires Shot at Entire Tech Industry**

**SNIPPET:**

“Search is dead. Long live Gemini.”

That was the unspoken ultimatum delivered during Google’s 2024 I/O keynote, where the company officially turned its core cash cow—internet search—into an appendage of its AI. In a move that sent shockwaves through Silicon Valley, Google announced that 70% of all standard search results will now be replaced by AI-generated "overviews" for complex queries, effectively cutting out traditional link-based monetization.

But the real bombshell: a direct attack on OpenAI. Sources inside the room say Pichai was visibly agitated during the Gemini 2.0 demo, taunting rivals with a simple line: "We didn't pivot, we just evolved faster."

The market reaction was immediate. Alphabet stock dipped 3% before recovering, while competitors scrambled to respond. **Key takeaway for CEOs:** If your business depends on organic search traffic, your model just evaporated. Google just eliminated the very pipeline it built—and replaced it with a subscription-based ecosystem no competitor can replicate.

**Bottom line:** Google just ate its own lunch to starve the rest of the table. Are you ready for a post-search world?
