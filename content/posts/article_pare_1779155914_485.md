**HEADLINE: 🚨 YOUR GROCERY BILL IS ABOUT TO GET WEIRDER (And Pricier!) 🚨**

**The "Pare" Trap: Why Cutting Your Veggies is Now a Luxury Expense**

Are you paying *more* for *less* produce? We’ve uncovered the latest kitchen trend being pushed by influencers and pricey meal kit services: **The Pare.**

You’ve seen it—that perfectly peeled, deseeded, and trimmed cucumber sitting in a $12 smoothie. Or the "crudité platter" where every carrot is a perfect, bite-sized shard. But here’s the gut punch: this isn’t just a flex. It’s costing you hundreds per year.

**Here’s how it hits your wallet:**

1.  **The "Veggie Tax"** – Pre-chopped, pre-pared "snack packs" are costing up to **300% more** per pound than whole produce. You’re literally paying for the scraps.
2.  **The 25% Shrink** – When you “pare” at home (peeling that potato or trimming that broccoli stem), you throw away roughly a quarter of your food—and the money you paid for it.
3.  **The "Gourmet" Hustle** – Restaurants and meal plans are charging $7 for a "pared" side salad that used to be $4. The mark-up is on the *labor* you used to do yourself.

**The Consumer Cheat Code: Stop Paring, Start Holding**

Your wallet is begging you to stop. The peel is where the fiber and 90% of the nutrients live. The stems are perfectly edible (and delicious when roasted).

**Our tip:** *Don't give in to the pare.* Buy whole. Buy ugly. Eat the peel. Your grocery bill (and your gut health) will thank you.
