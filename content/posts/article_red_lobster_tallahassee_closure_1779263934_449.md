**CLAIM:** Red Lobster in Tallahassee has permanently closed all locations following a sudden "Cheddar Bay Biscuit shortage" amid bankruptcy restructuring.

**VERDICT: FAKE** ❌

**Why it's fake:**  
- While Red Lobster has closed dozens of locations nationwide due to financial struggles, **no Tallahassee locations are on the official closure list.**  
- The claim of a "Cheddar Bay Biscuit shortage" is fabricated; the chain's biscuit mix is supplied by a separate company unaffected by the bankruptcy.  
- Viral posts on Facebook and TikTok misidentified a temporarily closed restaurant undergoing routine maintenance as a permanent closure.  

**Real story:** Red Lobster filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy in May 2024, closing nearly 100 locations. However, the **Tallahassee stores remain open** as of this writing, serving full menu items—including unlimited Cheddar Bay Biscuits.
