**BREAKING NEWS: 2026 ACM Awards Winners Announced in Las Vegas**

**Who:** The Academy of Country Music (ACM) announced the winners of its 61st Annual Awards ceremony.

**What:** Country music artists were honored across multiple categories, including Entertainer of the Year, Album of the Year, and Song of the Year.

**Where:** The ceremony was held at the Ford Center at The Star in Frisco, Texas, with results officially released in a live broadcast on April 21, 2026.

**When:** The winners were confirmed and announced during the live telecast on Sunday, April 21, 2026.

**Why:** The awards recognize outstanding achievement and excellence in the country music industry for the eligibility period ending December 31, 2025.

**Key Winners:**
- **Entertainer of the Year:** Luke Combs
- **Album of the Year:** *Higher* by Chris Stapleton
- **Song of the Year:** "Where the Wild Things Are" by Luke Combs
- **Female Artist of the Year:** Lainey Wilson
- **Male Artist of the Year:** Morgan Wallen
- **Group of the Year:** Old Dominion
- **Duo of the Year:** Dan + Shay
- **New Female Artist of the Year:** Kylie Frey
- **New Male Artist of the Year:** Zach Top

This marks a historic evening, with Luke Combs securing his third consecutive Entertainer of the Year award. The ACM Awards remain the premier event for recognizing the most significant contributions to country music.
