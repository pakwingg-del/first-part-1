**Headline:** Grand Theft Auto VI Price Confirmed at Industry-Record $79.99; Rockstar Games Cites Production Scale

**Location:** New York, NY

**Date:** October 26, 2023

**Source:** Take-Two Interactive / Rockstar Games

**Event:** In a landmark announcement that has sent shockwaves through the gaming industry, Rockstar Games and parent company Take-Two Interactive have officially confirmed the retail price for the upcoming title, Grand Theft Auto VI.

**Details:** The standard edition of the highly anticipated game will launch at a suggested retail price of $79.99 in the United States. This price point represents an unprecedented $10 increase over the current industry standard for major triple-A titles, which typically debut at $69.99. Industry analysts attribute the hike to the game’s reported record-breaking development budget, rumored to have surpassed one billion dollars, as well as escalating production costs and the sheer scale of the open-world experience. Pre-order stock for special and collector’s editions, priced as high as $149.99, has already seen significant sell-through within the first 24 hours. The company has stated that the pricing reflects the value of the complete runtime and content volume, though they did not dismiss the possibility of seasonal passes for future downloadable content. Consumer reaction has been divided, with many expressing concern over the normalization of higher price points for future releases.
