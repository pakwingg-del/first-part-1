In a move that has the creator economy buzzing, Gabbie Gonzalez has officially launched her "Tier 4: Ultimate Inner Circle" membership. **The Wallet Impact:**
This isn't just about one influencer. Consumer advocates are sounding the alarm. If this pricing model sticks, it sets a dangerous new ceiling for "emotional monetization."

*   **The "Friendship Premium":** For the price of a full Netflix, Spotify, and Hulu bundle, you get... a digital pen pal who doesn't know your name.
*   **The Hidden Math:**
    *   $99/mo = $1,188/year.
    *   That is the equivalent of **three full years of Spotify Premium.**

*   **The Trap of FOMO:** This model preys on the fear of missing out. You aren't paying for content; you are paying to feel "chosen." Advocates call this "emotional rent."
