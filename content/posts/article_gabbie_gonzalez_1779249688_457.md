**VERDICT: MISLEADING / SATIRE**

**SNIPPET:**

**Headline:** *"Gabbie Gonzalez Announces 'Silence is Violence' Charity Single One Week After Filing for a Gag Order"*

**Viral Claim (Spreading on TikTok/X):**
A rapidly circulating audio clip appears to show YouTuber and musician Gabbie Gonzalez stating, *“I’ve realized that if you care about mental health, you have to be loud. So I’m donating all proceeds from my next single to a foundation that fights ‘weaponized silence.’”* The post claims the single drops this Friday and that she filed for a gag order against a former collaborator just seven days prior.

**Fact Check:**
- **The clip is real but out of context.** The audio comes from a 2023 Instagram live stream where Gonzalez was discussing her *Songwriting vs. Drama* series, not a new project.
- **No new single has been announced.** Her label and Spotify page show no upcoming releases for this week.
- **The gag order story is outdated.** Gonzalez did file a temporary restraining order against a former friend in October 2022—it was dismissed by a judge three months later.
- **No charity foundation for “weaponized silence” exists** under that name in any public database.

**Bottom Line:** The post is a blend of real (her past legal filing) and fabricated (the new charity single). The claim is **misleading** and likely satirical in origin, though it is being shared as fact. Do not pre-order a single that does not exist.
