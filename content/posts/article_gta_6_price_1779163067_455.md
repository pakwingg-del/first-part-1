**Claim:** *GTA 6 will cost $150 at launch for the standard edition.*

**Verdict:** ❌ **Fake / Misleading**

**The Rumor:** A viral post claimed that a leaked internal document from Take-Two Interactive showed the standard edition of GTA 6 would be priced at a record-breaking $150, with a Premium "Forever Edition" at $250. The post included a blurry "screenshot" that resembled a corporate memo.

**The Reality:** No official pricing has been announced. The screenshot is a known hoax, originally created by a satire account. Furthermore, industry analysts (and a statement from Take-Two's CEO in early 2025) explicitly denied any plan to breach the $100 price point for a standard game this generation. While a higher price is possible, the $150 figure is fabricated. *Warning: Do not pre-order based on this image.*
