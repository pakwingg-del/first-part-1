Simpson case and exposed for his history of racist language and evidence tampering—will serve as the lead ethics consultant for the department’s new AI-powered “Fair-Forecast” predictive policing system.

The announcement, posted quietly on the LAPD’s public data portal late Friday, claims Fuhrman brings “unparalleled insight into the psychological pitfalls of bias in human-led investigations.” The AI, which uses 40 years of crime data to “pre-empt social unrest,” will supposedly use Fuhrman’s controversial testimony and later expert witness work as a baseline to identify “patterns of behavioral false positives.”
