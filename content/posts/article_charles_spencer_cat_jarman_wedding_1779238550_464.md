**Viral News Snippet: Charles Spencer's "Cat Jarman Wedding" Sparks Online Frenzy – But Is It Real?**

**The Claim:** A flurry of social media posts claim that Earl Charles Spencer, Princess Diana’s brother, has secretly married Danish archaeologist and author Dr. Cat Jarman in a private ceremony at Althorp. The rumor, fueled by a viral TikTok video showing a blurred photo of a couple and a caption reading "The Earl finds his Viking Queen," alleges the wedding took place last week with only immediate family present.

**The Reality: Fake.** Fact-checkers have confirmed there is no evidence of a marriage between Charles Spencer and Cat Jarman. The two are professional associates—Spencer wrote the foreword for Jarman’s 2021 book *River Kings: A New History of the Vikings from Scandinavia to the Silk Roads*—but neither party has announced a romantic relationship. The viral photo is actually a still from a 2022 literary festival panel, altered to appear like a wedding kiss. Althorp representatives have declined to comment, calling the rumor "baseless internet fiction."

**Bottom Line:** While the internet loves a good aristocratic-archaeologist love story, this one is strictly fantasy. No rings, no vows, just a book foreword and a misleading edit.
