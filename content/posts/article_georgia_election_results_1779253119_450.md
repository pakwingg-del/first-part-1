**CLAIM:** A viral video shows an election worker in Georgia being physically removed from a counting station for "stealing ballots" on Election Night 2024.

**VERDICT: FALSE.** The video is a deceptively edited clip from a 2020 Georgia election certification hearing, showing a security guard escorting a disruptive member of the public out of the room for shouting, not for handling ballots. No official investigation or credible news source supports the claim of ballot theft at this location. The video has been repeatedly debunked by the Georgia Secretary of State’s office.
