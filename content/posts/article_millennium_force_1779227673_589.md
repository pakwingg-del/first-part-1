**HEADLINE: 📉 MORAL DECAY RATED: THRILL-SEEKERS “DESENSITIZED” BY MILLENNIUM FORCE’S ‘OBSCENE’ DROP**

**Cedar Point, OH** – The "Millennium Force" roller coaster has officially been declared a "Moral Catastrophe" by the Coalition for Traditional Values (CTV). While park-goers rave about the 310-foot, 93-degree drop, critics argue the coaster represents the "final nail in the coffin of human temperance."

“This is not a ride; it is a lesson in depravity,” stated Dr. Helena Voss, a cultural critic for the CTV. “By normalizing a 90-second adrenaline rush that bypasses the soul entirely, we are breeding a generation incapable of restraint. They no longer seek joy through patience, scripture, or community. They seek a *mechanical defilement* of the senses.”

The critique points to the coaster's marketing, which boasts of “conquering fear.” Voss argues this is a toxic, pagan ritual. “We have replaced the fear of the Lord with the fear of a steel incline. We are now worshiping velocity, and the result is a shattered attention span and a calloused heart. Every scream is a prayer to the god of 'More.'”

The report goes on to link the coaster’s popularity to the rise of "chronic self-gratification" and a "collapse in marriage rates." When pressed for a solution, Voss suggested the ride be retrofitted with slow, meditative narration and seat belts that require a signed emotional wellness pledge.

“We used to watch a sunset and feel awe. Now, we need a 90 mph inversion to feel alive. The drop is obscene, not for its physics, but for its soul-crushing emptiness.”
