**Viral News Snippet: "Million+ Americans Losing ACA Coverage Overnight – Is Your Plan Next?"**

*Rumor:* A viral social media post claims the Biden administration has *quietly canceled* coverage for over 1.2 million people who signed up for Affordable Care Act (ACA) plans in 2024, citing a "glitch" in the federal marketplace. The post includes a screenshot of a panic-inducing email from HealthCare.gov reading: "Your plan is no longer active. Re-enroll immediately or lose all subsidies."

*Reality Check:* **FALSE – Misleading & Out of Context.** The viral email is real, but it was sent last year (2023), not 2024, and was for a routine renewal period, not a mass termination. The actual number of people affected by *technical disenrollment* in 2024 is about 0.2% of enrollees—mostly due to expired immigration documents or outdated income info, not a "quiet cancellation." No one lost coverage overnight. The screenshot is being shared as if it's a new "secret purge," but it's a recycled warning from a standard system update. **Bottom line: No mass ACA coverage loss has occurred.**
