**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**ANCHOR DESK, GLOBAL NEWS NETWORK**

**DATELINE: NEW YORK, NY**

**SUBJECT:** PEPSICO REINSTATES MOUNTAIN DEW "WHITE OUT" FOLLOWING UNPRECEDENTED CONSUMER CAMPAIGN

**WHAT:** In a move that has sent shockwaves through the beverage and marketing industries, PepsiCo has officially announced the limited-time return of Mountain Dew "White Out," a citrus-flavored, translucent beverage that had been discontinued from permanent retail shelves in 2019. The product is scheduled for a nationwide rollout beginning July 15th.

**WHO:** The decision was made by PepsiCo's Senior Leadership Team following what the company describes as an "unprecedented, organic consumer mobilization." The campaign was spearheaded by the online fan group "Dew Drinkers United," which amassed a petition of over 250,000 signatures and generated more than 15 million social media impressions using the hashtag #BringBackWhiteOut.

**WHEN:** The product was originally launched in 2010 as the winner of the "Dewmocracy" consumer-vote campaign. It was phased out of the company's core portfolio five years prior to this announcement. The limited-edition re-release will be available for a six-week window, from July 15th through August 31st.

**WHERE:** The beverage will be available at all major national retailers, including Walmart, Target, Kroger, and convenience store chains. Distribution will be limited to the continental United States.

**WHY:** PepsiCo officials cited "an overwhelming, sustained demand from the consumer base" as the primary catalyst for the product's revival. A spokesperson for the company confirmed that internal metrics indicated White Out retained a "cult-like following" and that the volume of digital engagement over the last eighteen months made a re-release "a clear strategic imperative."
