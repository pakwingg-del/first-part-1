**Top 5 Things You Need to Know About 'LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight'**

The internet is buzzing after leaked footage and rumors suggest a massive new project titled *Legacy of the Dark Knight*. Here’s the breakdown of why this is a must-watch news story:

- **This is NOT a movie—it's a next-gen open-world game.**
  Forget a simple film or TV show: leaks point to a sprawling, AAA video game where you can freely explore a fully destructible Gotham City made entirely of LEGO bricks. Think *LEGO City Undercover* meets *Batman: Arkham Knight*, but with the classic LEGO humor intact.

- **It retcons the classic 2008-2012 trilogy.**
  The game reportedly *merges* the plots of *LEGO Batman 1, 2, and 3* into a single, cohesive narrative. Iconic moments (like the Joker's bank heist and the League of Shadows invasion) are being completely rebuilt with new levels, voice acting, and a darker, more mature tone—but still rated E for Everyone.

- **Multiple Batmen from across the multiverse are playable.**
  Players can switch between different *Batmen* on the fly. The leaked roster includes the classic LEGO Batman, the grim *Batman v Superman* armored Batman, the campy Adam West Batman, and even the *The Batman* (Robert Pattinson) variant, each with unique gadgets and combat styles.

- **The “Dark Knight” mechanic changes everything.**
  A new "Fear" system lets you use Batman's reputation against enemies. You can terrify goons by leaving holographic bats, dark silhouettes, or even full audio logs of villain monologues. This replaces the old “build and fight” loop with psychological stealth puzzles.
