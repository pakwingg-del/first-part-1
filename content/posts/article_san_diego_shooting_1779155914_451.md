**CLAIM:** Posts circulating on X claim that the San Diego shooting on August 7, 2025, was an "active shooter drill that went wrong" and that police initiated the gunfire, not a suspect.

- **Injuries:** One officer sustained a graze wound to the shoulder. The suspect was struck and transported to Scripps Mercy Hospital in critical condition.

A dispatcher used the term "scenario" to describe the ongoing situation—a standard term for any critical incident—which was then selectively clipped and reposted with false captions claiming it was a training exercise.

**BOTTOM LINE:** No drill. No cover-up. One officer injured, one suspect hospitalized. The viral claim is baseless. Always verify before sharing.
