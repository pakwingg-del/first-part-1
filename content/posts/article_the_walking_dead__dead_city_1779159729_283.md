**THE WALKING DEAD: DEAD CITY – FANS SPOT "GLITCH IN THE MATRIX" AS MAGGIE’S WATCH KEEPS REVERSE-TIMING**

**NEW YORK, NY** – A bizarre continuity error in Sunday’s episode of *The Walking Dead: Dead City* has sent the fandom into a spiral of speculation, with some calling it a “glitch in the matrix.”

During a tense chase scene in a radioactive subway tunnel, die-hard viewers noticed that Maggie’s (Lauren Cohan) wristwatch consistently runs *counter-clockwise* during the sequence. What makes the "glitch" truly viral? The same watch faces forward—normally—in every other scene of the season.

Social media exploded with split-screen comparisons. User **@GlennsGhost** wrote: “In the dark, the watch ticks BACK. In the light, it ticks FORWARD. It’s like the editors used two different time signatures for the same 90-second clip.”

But the weirdness doesn’t stop there. Astute fans also noticed that a digital clock in the background of the Manhattan skyline—supposedly frozen during the apocalypse—shows the **exact same timestamp** (3:14 AM) from a flashback scene in Season 2 of the original *The Walking Dead*.

“3:14 has been a hidden number since the barn scene,” tweeted analyst **@MatrixWalker**. “Now it’s on a skyscraper in a city that’s supposed to be dead. Either the writers are messing with us, or the simulation is breaking.”

Showrunner Eli Jorné has remained silent, but a deleted tweet from a VFX editor read: “We fixed it three times. It kept reverting.” That tweet was scrubbed within minutes.
