**Subject:** Immediate Action Required: San Diego Active Shooter Incident

**SITUATION OVERVIEW**
An active shooter incident at a San Diego transit hub has resulted in multiple casualties. The assailant was neutralized by law enforcement within minutes, but the attack underscores critical gaps in public venue security protocols.

**BUSINESS IMPACT**
- Operations disrupted across downtown San Diego; transit systems halted until further notice.
- Potential liability and insurance implications for companies with on-site public access.
- Employee safety concerns may increase absenteeism and lower morale.

**NEXT STEPS**
- Verify employee safety and provide immediate counseling resources.
- Review security protocols with facilities and legal teams.
- Prepare internal and external communications to address stakeholder concerns.

**BOTTOM LINE**
This tragic event is a stark reminder: passive security measures are no longer sufficient. Leaders must proactively invest in threat detection and crisis response to protect people, reputation, and operational continuity.
