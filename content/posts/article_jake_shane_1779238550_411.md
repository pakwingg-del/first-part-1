**TOP 5 THINGS YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT JAKE SHANE** 🚨

- **The Viral "Bridgerton Carriage" Moment That Started It All**  
  Jake Shane, the 24-year-old NYC content creator, exploded onto TikTok in 2023 with a rapid-fire parody of the *Bridgerton* carriage scene. His chaotic, over-the-top impressions of "horny historical romance" characters—complete with dramatic breathing and nonsensical British accents—racked up millions of views and launched him as Gen Z’s reigning king of absurdist humor.

- **He’s the Voice Behind "Pasghetti" and "The Butt Pillow"**  
  Shane’s signature bits include him acting as a toddler whining for *pasghetti*, a "morally gray" pillow that loves his butt, and a 2018-era Art Basel influencer. His humor thrives on surreal, hyper-specific characters that feel like inside jokes with ten million of your closest friends.

- **The "Ther-apy" Controversy and the Apology Tour**  
  In early 2024, Shane faced backlash after a resurfaced podcast clip showed him mocking therapy and mental health awareness as "cringe." Critics called it tone-deaf given his massive young audience. He posted a tearful apology—but TikTok was divided: fans defended his "dark comedy" style, while others claimed it revealed a pattern of dismissive, “edgy” takes.

- **From Meme Lord to Podcast King**  
  Not content with just viral clips, Shane launched *Therapuss* (yes, with the misspelling) with social media star Brooke Schofield. The podcast pivots from his controversial therapy comments—they now discuss mental health, but with his signature chaos. It’s consistently hit the top of Apple’s comedy charts, proving his brand of "messy honesty" sells.
