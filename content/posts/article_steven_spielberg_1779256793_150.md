**AITA for telling my friend that Steven Spielberg is just the world's most expensive Hallmark Channel director?**

Mark loses his mind. Says Spielberg is the GOAT. I laugh and point out that the man has made exactly *one* movie that wasn't dripping in saccharine sentimentality (*Jaws*, and even that has a dad who just wants his kids to see the beach). The rest is just *E.T. holding a lamp* but stretched into 3 hours.

I told him Spielberg is the cinematic equivalent of a safe space. Everything is resolved by a guy with a strong jawline and a 1980s synth score. Then this happens:

Mark says, "He defined our childhoods."

I say, "Yeah, and McDonald's Happy Meals defined our childhoods. Doesn't mean I frame the wrapper."

Now he won't talk to me. AITA for roasting the man who gave us *Hook* (the single greatest argument for "the original was fine actually")? TL;DR: My friend thinks Spielberg invented cinema. I told him he invented cinematic comfort food. Left my popcorn in his microwave.
