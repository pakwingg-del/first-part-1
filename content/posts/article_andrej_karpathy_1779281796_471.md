**Viral News Snippet:**

**FACT CHECK: Did Andrej Karpathy predict that AI will replace all software engineers by 2028?**

**CLAIM:** A clip circulating on TikTok and X (formerly Twitter) shows AI researcher and former OpenAI/ Tesla engineer **Andrej Karpathy** stating: *“By 2028, we won’t need human software engineers. All code will be written and maintained by AI.”*

**VERDICT: MISLEADING / OUT OF CONTEXT**

The clip is real, but it is from a **2023** interview on the *Lex Fridman Podcast*, not a new 2025 prediction. In the full, unedited segment, Karpathy was speaking **hypothetically** about the *technical ceiling* of AI code generation, not making a specific timeline prediction.

In the surrounding context, Karpathy actually said: *“Humans are still very much in the loop. The bottleneck is shifting from *writing* code to *verifying* code. I don't see a world where we just hit a button and fire everyone—at least not in the next decade.”*

Karpathy has consistently argued that AI will *augment* engineers, not replace them entirely, and that AI still struggles with coherent, long-term system design. The viral clip is a classic example of **missing context**—and is rated **FAKE** as a literal prediction.
