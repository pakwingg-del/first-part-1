**Claim:** AI researcher and former OpenAI/Tesla engineer **Andrej Karpathy** has announced that he is leaving all current projects to launch a "Neural School" for gifted children, where students will be taught exclusively by custom-trained Large Language Models in a fully virtual environment, without human teachers.

**Status: FAKE (Satirical)**

**The Facts:**
The viral snippet circulating on social media claims Karpathy is developing a "pedagogical singularity," arguing that human teachers are now "the bottleneck in the learning loop." While it is true that Karpathy is deeply focused on AI education—specifically his *Neural Networks: Zero to Hero* YouTube series and his recent work with **Eureka Labs**—the claims about a fully AI-run school for children are entirely fabricated.

The actual context: In July 2024, Andrej Karpathy did announce **Eureka Labs**, an "AI-native" education company. However, his stated goal is to *augment* (not replace) human teachers. Eureka Labs aims to create a "Teacher + AI symbiosis," where an AI assistant helps guide instruction, but a human educator still designs the curriculum, handles mentorship, and runs physical or hybrid classrooms. Karpathy has repeatedly stated that he believes "the best learning environments are deeply human," contradicting the viral "no teachers allowed" angle.

**Source of Confusion:** A satirical tech newsletter generated a fake press release mimicking Karpathy’s technical writing style, swapping "autonomous driving" jargon for "autonomous education." The AI-generated image included in the post—showing a child wearing a VR headset with Karpathy’s face rendered as a glowing hologram—was shared without any watermark or disclaimer, leading many to believe it was a legit product launch.
