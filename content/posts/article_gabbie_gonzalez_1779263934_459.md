**🚨 TRENDING: Gabbie Gonzalez FACT-CHECK 🚨**

**Rumor:** Viral posts claim that Gabbie Gonzalez, the founder of the skincare line *Gloss & Grow*, was arrested last night in Austin, Texas for allegedly operating an illegal "testimony farm"—paying influencers to lie about her products curing acne and eczema.

**Status: 🚫 FAKE**

**Fact Check:**

- **Origin of hoax:** The rumor appears to stem from a satirical TikTok account (@glossgateconfessions) that posted a fake "breaking news" graphic. The account’s bio reads: *"Just here to start drama. Don’t believe everything you see."*

**Bottom line:** This is an AI-generated hoax mixed with a trending satirical post. Gabbie Gonzalez has not been arrested. Always cross-check "breaking news" screenshots with official sources. 🛑
