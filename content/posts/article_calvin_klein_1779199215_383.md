**BREAKING NEWS: Calvin Klein Announces Restructuring Initiative Amidst Global Retail Shifts**

**What:** Calvin Klein, a globally recognized American fashion house, has announced a comprehensive restructuring plan aimed at streamlining operations and focusing on direct-to-consumer sales.

**Who:** PVH Corp, the parent company of Calvin Klein, issued the official statement. The announcement was made by CEO Stefan Larsson.

**When:** The announcement was released earlier today, with the restructuring expected to take effect over the next three fiscal quarters.

**Where:** The changes will impact Calvin Klein’s operations globally, with significant closures and consolidations occurring across regional headquarters in New York, London, and Hong Kong.

**Why:** Citing a strategic shift to prioritize core brands and high-margin digital channels, PVH Corp stated the decision was necessitated by evolving consumer behavior. Industry analysts attribute the move to declining foot traffic in malls and a 12% drop in wholesale revenue reported in the last quarter.

**Conclusion:** Affected employees have been notified, and PVH Corp confirmed it will provide severance packages. Shares of PVH Corp rose 3.4% in after-hours trading following the announcement.
