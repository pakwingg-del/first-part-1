Critics aren't holding back. "National Acai Bowl Day has become a capitalist nightmare," argues @EcoCritic, an environmental activist. The backlash centers on two key issues: first, that the day whitewashes the cultural significance of acai in Afro-Brazilian communities; second, that it fuels unsustainable farming practices. Memes mocking $20 bowls and 'basic' Instagram posts are going viral, with many calling for a boycott or rebranding to 'Ethical Acai Day.'
