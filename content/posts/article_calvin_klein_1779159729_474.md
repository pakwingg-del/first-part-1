# 🔍 FAKE ALERT

**Viral Snippet:**
"So... Calvin Klein just announced they're launching a '99% Off Everything' flash sale on Instagram for the next 24 hours only.💥 Free shipping worldwide! I already grabbed 5 pairs of jeans for $12 total. 👖🔥"

**Real vs Fake Check:**
🚫 **FAKE.** No such sale has been announced by Calvin Klein. This is a classic phishing scam designed to steal your credit card info. Always verify deals on the official Calvin Klein website or verified social media pages, not random reposts.

**What to do:** Report the post, block the account, and never click suspicious links promising extreme discounts.
