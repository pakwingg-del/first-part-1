# Top 5 Things You Need to Know About Thom Tillis

- **The Swing Vote King in a Key Senate Race**  
  Tillis is locked in one of the toughest re-election battles of 2026 in North Carolina. As a moderate Republican who has often broken with his party on issues like immigration reform and gun safety, he's become a major target for both the far right and Democrats. His vote could decide Senate control.

- **Immigration Deal Maker or Breaker?**  
  He was the lead Republican negotiator on the bipartisan border security bill that collapsed in early 2024. Tillis claims the deal was torpedoed by Trump's interference, making him a central figure in the ongoing GOP civil war over immigration policy.

- **Survived a Brutal Primary Challenge**  
  In 2020, Tillis faced a primary challenger backed by the far-right Club for Growth and Trump allies. He survived by tacking right on some issues, but he's still viewed as too moderate by many base voters—a tightrope walk that defines his political survival.

- **Key Vote on Supreme Court Nominees**  
  He voted to confirm both Brett Kavanaugh and Amy Coney Barrett, but his cautious approach during hearings (including a memorable moment where he lectured Democratic senators on "decorum") made him a flashpoint for judicial battles.

- **Strangest Viral Moment: The "Cat Walker" Photo**  
  In 2023, a photo of Tillis walking a cat on a leash outside his D.C. townhouse went viral—not for politics, but for the sheer absurdity of a senior senator casually pet-parenting in the middle of a debt ceiling crisis. It humanized him, but also sparked endless memes about "the most normal guy in Congress."
