**BREAKING: Influencer Gabbie Gonzalez Files Federal Lawsuit Against Former Manager Over Alleged Breach of Contract**

**Who:** Gabbie Gonzalez, the 31-year-old social media influencer and content creator with over 12 million followers across multiple platforms.

**What:** A lawsuit has been filed in the United States District Court for the Central District of California, alleging breach of contract, fraud, and unjust enrichment.

**When:** The legal complaint was formally submitted earlier this morning, with court documents unsealed at 9:00 AM Pacific Time. Sources indicate the alleged misconduct occurred between January 2022 and December 2023.

**Where:** The case, Gonzalez v. Brightside Media Group, is being heard in Los Angeles, California, where both parties are headquartered.

**Why:** The 47-page complaint alleges that former manager Thomas Hartwell misappropriated an estimated $1.3 million in sponsorship revenue, failed to secure promised brand deals, and engaged in unauthorized financial transactions using Gonzalez’s business accounts. Gonzalez claims Hartwell leveraged her influence for personal gain without proper authorization, resulting in significant reputational and financial damages.

**Additional Context:** Hartwell’s attorney issued a statement this afternoon calling the allegations “baseless and a transparent attempt to shift blame for declining engagement metrics.” A preliminary hearing has been scheduled for February 15. Gonzalez has publicly stated she will release a detailed video explanation on her YouTube channel later this week.
