**Founder Claims His 'Genius' New Productivity Method Will Replace Coffee**

*Silicon Valley, CA –* In a move that shocked absolutely no one, startup founder and self-proclaimed "visionary" Chad Moistbottom, 28, announced his groundbreaking new app, *Nootro-Nap™*, which he claims will replace caffeine forever.

"Look, I was tired of being tired," Chad explained, adjusting his AllBirds and standing in front of a stark white wall. "So I realized: what if we didn't consume stimulants, but instead just *simulated* the feeling of having all your work done already?"

The app, which costs $49.99/month, allegedly uses "proprietary neuro-soundscapes" to trick your brain into thinking you've already completed your 2-hour morning deep-work session. Users simply close their eyes for 90 seconds, listen to a faint track of a keyboard clacking and someone saying "Great pivot," and then, supposedly, feel *fully charged*.

One early beta tester, Sarah, reported, "I tried it. I felt a mild sense of panic and regret, which is pretty much the same vibe I get after my third espresso, so... I guess it works?"

When asked for evidence, Moistbottom cited a study "we funded ourselves" showing a 0.3% increase in self-reported "zest." Critics were quick to point out that the only thing *Nootro-Nap* replaces is your money, but Chad remains unfazed.

"It’s not a scam, it’s a lifestyle. Most people just don’t have the hustle mindset to realize that feeling rested is a choice," he said, visibly trembling from what could be either genius or stage-three caffeine withdrawal.
