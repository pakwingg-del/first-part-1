**Headline: 🚨 "Cozy" $300 TikTok Trend Is a Fire & Wallet Hazard: That Viral Hanging Lantern Could Burn Down Your Home (Or Your Budget)**  

**Body:**  
Influencers are calling it the “instant cozy vibe”—but consumer safety experts are calling it a ticking time bomb. Thousands of TikTok and Instagram users are snapping up cheap, decorative hanging lanterns from viral online shops, paying $20–$60 for what looks like a steal compared to boutique versions.  

But here’s the reality check hitting bank accounts and emergency rooms:  

🔥 **Fire risk**: Many lack UL certification, meaning they overheat in minutes. Reports of melted bases, singed ceilings, and one near-fire in a Seattle apartment have already surfaced.  
💰 **Hidden costs**: Replacement bulbs burn out in weeks, not months, and the “bargain” lanterns often rust or shatter within a season—forcing repeat purchases.  
🏡 **Insurance fine print**: Using uncertified lighting can void your renter’s or homeowner’s fire damage claim. A $40 “deal” could cost you $10,000+ in deductibles.  

**Consumer tip**: If the ad says “boho” but not “UL Listed,” **skip the scroll**. Your wallet (and smoke alarm) will thank you.
