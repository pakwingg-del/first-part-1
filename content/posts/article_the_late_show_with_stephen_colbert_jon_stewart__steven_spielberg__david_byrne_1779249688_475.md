**VIRAL NEWS SNIPPET – Status: FAKE**

**Headline:** *“Stephen Colbert Announces Jon Stewart Will Take Over ‘The Late Show’ After Steven Spielberg & David Byrne Offer to ‘Reboot Late Night as Performance Art’”*

**The Rumor:** A flurry of viral posts claim that during Tuesday night’s taping, Stephen Colbert dramatically announced his semi-retirement, handing the reins to former *Daily Show* host Jon Stewart. The twist? The announcement was allegedly triggered by a secret pitch from director Steven Spielberg and Talking Heads frontman David Byrne, who supposedly proposed turning the final season of *The Late Show* into a “living, interactive concert film” shot entirely in a New York subway station.

**The Reality: COMPLETELY FAKE.** No such announcement occurred. Steven Spielberg has no public involvement with the show, and David Byrne recently stated he is “finished with theatrical broadcast experiments.” The only thing being “rebooted” is the internet’s willingness to believe a headline that sounds too perfectly weird to be true.
