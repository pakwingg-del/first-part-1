**Verdict: FALSE (Misleading Context)**

The ‘mountain’ is finally out. BVO got you cancelled. Pour one out for the citrus.”*

However, **Mountain Dew White Out has not contained BVO since 2020.** PepsiCo removed BVO from all its products – including White Out – years before the FDA ban to stay ahead of regulatory scrutiny. The current formula uses glycerol ester of rosin (ester gum) as a stabilizer, which is still legal.
