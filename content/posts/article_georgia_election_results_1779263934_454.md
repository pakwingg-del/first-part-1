**BREAKING: FAKE NEWS ALERT – Viral Claim That Georgia Election Results Were “Switched by Algorithm” Is DEBUNKED**

A video circulating on Telegram and X claims that a “secret algorithm” in Georgia’s voting machines flipped 47,000 votes from a Republican candidate to a Democrat in the 2024 general election. The video shows a fuzzy screenshot of a “server dashboard” allegedly from inside the Secretary of State’s office.

**VERDICT: FALSE. (🔴 Red Flag)**

**Fact-Check:**
- Georgia’s Secretary of State, Brad Raffensperger, has publicly confirmed that the screenshot is a crude Photoshop forgery and that the IP address shown does not match any state server.
- Independent audit teams from both parties observed the post-election risk-limiting audit, which found zero instances of vote flipping. The audit matched 100% of the paper ballots to the machine counts.
- The viral video’s “expert” is a self-described “blockchain analyst” with no ties to election cybersecurity and a history of spreading vaccine misinformation.
- The Georgia Bureau of Investigation has confirmed there is no evidence of any algorithm manipulation.

**Bottom line:** This claim is recycled from a 2020 hoax and has been officially debunked by state election officials. Do not share.
