**Top 5 Things You Need to Know About the Google I/O 2024 Keynote**

- **Gemini is the New Android Standard:** Google announced that **Gemini Nano**, its on-device AI model, will be replacing Assistant as the default digital helper on Pixel phones. It can now detect scam calls in real-time, summarize notifications without an internet connection, and even understand your emotions from your voice.

- **Project Astra: The "Omniscient" AI Agent:** In a stunning demo, Google showed off **Project Astra**, a real-time AI agent that can see, hear, and converse. It can identify your lost glasses by "memorizing" where you left them seconds ago and will help you navigate complex tasks live. Think of it as Jarvis—but working directly through your phone camera.

- **Search is Now "Multi-Step Reasoning":** Google Search is no longer just a search bar. With the new **"Ask with Video"** feature, you can point your camera at a broken bike chain, and Search will analyze the visual, identify the specific problem, and play a step-by-step repair video without you needing to type a single word.

- **Circle to Search Goes Academic:** The popular "Circle to Search" feature got a massive brain upgrade. You can now circle complex math equations, physics diagrams, or even chemical formulas, and Google will not only give you the answer but also break down the **entire step-by-step logic** behind the solution.

- **Android's "Theft Detection Lock":** A major safety push: new AI analyzes your phone's accelerometer and gyroscope to detect if it's been **snatched out of your hand**. If it senses a sudden running motion or a car pickup, the phone will auto-lock the screen and disable all notifications, making the device useless to a thief.
