**Viral News Snippet: Chris Rabb**

**Headline:** State Rep. Chris Rabb Vows to Block Amazon Tax Breaks — “No More Corporate Handouts in Philly”

**Body:**  
Pennsylvania State Representative Chris Rabb is going viral after introducing legislation to ban the use of taxpayer funds for corporate subsidies — taking direct aim at Amazon’s proposed HQ2 expansion in Philadelphia.  

“We’re giving away the store for jobs that don’t pay living wages,” Rabb said in a blistering press conference. His proposed Fairness in Economic Development Act would require any company receiving state incentives to pay workers at least $20/hour, offer 12 weeks paid leave, and prove they didn’t outsource jobs elsewhere.

Amazon has not commented. Local business groups call the bill “economically reckless,” but Rabb’s grassroots support is surging — with #StopCorporateWelfare trending in Philly.  

**Net take:** Rabb is betting that voters care more about wage equity than stock buybacks. If this bill passes, it could set a national precedent — and cost Pennsylvania billions in future corporate investment.
