# 🚨 WALLET ALERT: Dunkin’ Just Gave America ONE Day to Get Free Coffee—But There’s a ‘Wallet Trap’ You Need to Know

**MAY 19, 2025** — If you thought “free coffee” meant zero strings attached, think again, America. Dunkin’ is rolling out a National Free Coffee Day on **May 19**, but consumer advocates are warning that this “gift” could actually cost you more in the long run if you’re not careful.

Here’s the deal: Dunkin’ is offering a **free medium hot or iced coffee** with any purchase. Sounds great, right? But here’s the wallet trap—the “any purchase” part. Consumer advocates say that minimum spend can be as low as a $1 donut, but psychological research shows that once you’re in the store for that “freebie,” the average customer spends **$4.50 to $6 extra** on impulse items like breakfast sandwiches, donuts, and overpriced bottled water.

**The Real Cost:**
- Free coffee value: ~$2.79
- Average additional spend: $5.00
- **Net loss to your wallet: $2.21** (plus tip guilt)

Even worse, the fine print reveals that the free coffee **cannot be customized** — no oat milk, no extra shot, no cold foam. So if you’re a “lavender oat milk cold brew with oat milk cold foam” person, that free coffee is basically a plain cup of joe that doesn’t match your lifestyle. You’ll end up paying for the upgrade anyway.
