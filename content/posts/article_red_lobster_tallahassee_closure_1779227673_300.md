**Gone Fishing—Into Bankruptcy Court?**

**Tallahassee, FL** — When Red Lobster shuttered its Apalachee Parkway location this week, the official line was “underperformance.” But locals are asking: who really got hooked? The seafood chain, owned by a Thai conglomerate, recently gutted its all-you-can-eat shrimp deal—after luring in millions of budget-conscious diners. Now, with rising rents and supply chain chaos, 23 more locations are on the chopping block. Coincidence, or a calculated play to consolidate real estate and squeeze out competitors? One worker told us, “They’re fishing for a bankruptcy filing—and we’re the bait.”
