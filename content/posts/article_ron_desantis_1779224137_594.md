The new “Clean Parks, Clean Conscience” initiative, effective immediately, targets everything from corporate picnic sangria to the beloved “Sunset Sip-and-Savor” event at Myakka River State Park.

The moral calculus, according to a press release from the Governor’s office, is to return the parks to their “original, sober purpose.” “God gave us the Everglades to worship His creation, not to get a contact buzz off a cabernet,” one policy aide was quoted as saying.
