**Headline: TSA Gold+ Leaked: Pay $99 to Skip the Line? Your Wallet’s Next Shock at the Airport**

**The Snippet:**

If you thought PreCheck was the last word in airport convenience, buckle up. Leaked internal memos confirm the TSA is testing **"Gold+"** —a premium, fast-track screening tier that promises to cut your wait time to under *two minutes*—but only if you fork over an extra **$99 per year** on top of your existing PreCheck fee.

Here’s the cash-grab that should make your wallet wince: Gold+ members get their own dedicated lane, skip the shoe removal, and wave laptops straight through. Non-members? Expect even longer lines as TSA reallocates resources to the pay-up tier.

Consumer advocates are sounding the alarm: This is “pay-to-play” security. The TSA insists it’s “testing consumer demand,” but critics say it’s a quiet price hike for the rest of us. If you fly twice a year, that’s a $50 surcharge per trip just to avoid the cattle call.

**The Real Cost?** 
Your time or your money. With Gold+, the airport is officially selling your patience back to you. Is a shorter line worth $99? Or will this just become another subscription you reluctantly buy?
