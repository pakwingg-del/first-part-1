**WHAT:** U.S. Customs and Border Protection, or CBP, has released an operational warning for all international ports of entry. The agency is urging travelers to prepare for significant processing delays due to anticipated high volumes of traffic.

**WHO:** The advisory targets all persons crossing into the United States via land, air, or sea. This applies to U.S. citizens, lawful permanent residents, and international visitors.

**WHEN:** The heightened alert period is in effect from Friday, May 24th, through Monday, May 27th, 2024, coinciding with the national holiday weekend.

The agency’s final instruction is clear: verify documentation, declare all goods, and anticipate extended wait times.
