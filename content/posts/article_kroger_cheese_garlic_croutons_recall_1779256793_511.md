**TRENDING: Life Coach Says Kroger Crouton Recall Is a "Spiritual Sign to Crumb Your Comfort Zone"**

In a viral video that’s racking up millions of views, life coach Dr. Mara Ellison is telling clients that the Kroger recall of their Garlic Cheese Croutons isn’t just about Salmonella fears—it’s a "cosmic plot twist."

"Your croutons are gone. So what? That’s the universe telling you to stop putting crunchy armor on your salad—and your life," Ellison says in the clip, which has sparked a fierce debate.

Her advice? "You don’t need that extra crunch to feel full. Stop hiding behind cheese-dusted bread. What are you really avoiding? A hard conversation? A new career? The Salmonella was the excuse. The real recall is your resistance to change."

Critics call it tone-deaf exploitation of a public health issue. Supporters say it’s a "shake-up we needed."

Meanwhile, Kroger is urging customers to return the product for a full refund—and maybe a side of self-reflection.
