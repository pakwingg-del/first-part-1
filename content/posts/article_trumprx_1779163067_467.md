**Viral News Snippet:**

**"BREAKING: Trump’s Secret ‘Rx’ Plan Leaked – Would Force Drugmakers to Sell to Feds at ‘Patriot Price’ or Face Export Ban"**

*Rumor Level: MIXED – Partially True, Highly Exaggerated*

**The Claim:** A leaked memo from an anonymous White House advisor claims former President Donald Trump is drafting an executive order dubbed "Operation Rx Relief" that would require all pharmaceutical companies to sell prescription drugs to the U.S. government at a fixed "Patriot Price" — allegedly 40% below the average market rate. According to the viral post, companies that refuse would be banned from exporting any medications abroad for five years.

**What’s Real:** During his 2024 campaign, Trump did float the idea of creating a "favored nations clause" for drug pricing, referencing a model used by European countries. His public statements included threats to revoke patents or ban exports if drugmakers refused to lower prices for American seniors. The concept of using export restrictions as leverage has been discussed among his economic advisors.

**What’s Fake:** The specific "leaked memo" circulating with a formal name ("Operation Rx Relief") and detailed pricing percentages has not been verified by any major news outlet or government watchdog. The text in the viral post contains grammatical errors and uses an unofficial White House letterhead format that several digital forensics experts have flagged as amateurish AI-generated imagery. No credible source has confirmed that a “Patriot Price” formula exists.

**Bottom Line:** The rumor is *partially true* in its intent (Trump has aggressively discussed radical drug pricing measures), but the specific leaked document and its enforcement details are **fabricated**. Treat this as a “truth-bomb with fake packaging” – the core idea is real, but the viral snippet is doctored. ✅⚠️
