**VIRAL NEWS SNIPPET**

**🚨 BREAKING: "Supercell" Watch Issued for 11 States – Officials Warn of 'Instant Tornado' Risk as Pop-Up Storms Could Strike Without Warning**

**Verdict: REAL (with context)**

The National Weather Service has issued a *Severe Thunderstorm Watch* for parts of 11 states, stretching from the Ohio Valley through the Mid-Atlantic. While the term "supercell" is accurate for the storms expected, the phrase "instant tornado" is FAKE — no official source has used that wording.

**What's actually happening:** The Storm Prediction Center warns of a "highly unstable atmosphere" producing storms capable of damaging wind gusts up to 75 mph, large hail (up to 2 inches in diameter), and isolated tornadoes. The watch does **not** guarantee tornadoes — but conditions are favorable.

**Bottom line: Real watch, exaggerated phrasing.** Stay weather-aware, but don't panic. 🌩️
