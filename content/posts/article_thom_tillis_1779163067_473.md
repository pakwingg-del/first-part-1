**Viral News Snippet: "Senator Thom Tillis Spotted with Unlikely Co-Star in Secret Government Facility – Here’s Why Experts Say It Changes Everything"**

**The Claim:**
A blurry, grainy video circulating on X (formerly Twitter) and TikTok claims to show North Carolina Senator Thom Tillis exiting a nondescript government warehouse in Washington, D.C., alongside a person digitally disguised with a pixelated face. Accompanying posts allege that Tillis is collaborating with a "whistleblower" to leak classified documents that would "prove" the U.S. government has been harboring a "non-human intelligence" (NHI) entity since the 1950s.

**Why It’s Going Viral:**
- The video was posted by an account calling itself "Anon_Truth_Seeker_2086," which claims to have hacked into a "secure government server" to obtain the footage.
- The caption reads: "Thom Tillis, a key player in UFO/UAP hearings, was just caught meeting with a source who has eyes on the Roswell evidence. This is why they’re keeping him quiet."
- Hashtags like #UAP, #ThomTillis, and #FullDisclosure are trending, with users claiming this "proves" Tillis’s recent push for government transparency on UAPs is part of a larger cover-up operation.

**The Reality (Fact Check):**
- **Fake.** Official records from the U.S. Capitol Police confirm Tillis was attending a closed-door Senate briefing on national security threats—not a "secret facility"—at the time the video was allegedly filmed. The briefing involved classified satellite technology, not extraterrestrial matters.
- The "digitally disguised" figure is a common trope used by hoax accounts. The original video is a repurposed, de-pixelated clip from a 2021 Congressional tour of a data storage facility;
