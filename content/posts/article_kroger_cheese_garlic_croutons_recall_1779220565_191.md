⚠️ **LOCAL ALERT: Kroger Cheese Garlic Croutons Recall – “WHO EATS THIS STUFF?”** ⚠️

Posted in *Springfield Community Watch & Chat* 🛑

---

Just got the push notification from Kroger. They’re recalling their **Cheese Garlic Croutons** (sold in the produce section and bulk aisle) because of potential *undeclared milk allergens*.

I’ll be honest – who is actually buying croutons that taste like garlic bread mixed with Parmesan dust in a bag? That’s not “salad topper,” that’s a **sodium bomb with delusions of grandeur**. 🧂

But here’s the real head-scratcher: **Common sense would tell you if it says “cheese garlic” on the bag, it probably has cheese in it.** It’s right there in the name. You think they’re using “cheese flavor” that’s just air and regret? No – it’s dairy, people.

I’m not even mad about the recall. I’m mad that someone with a serious milk allergy looked at a product called “CHEESE Garlic Croutons” and thought, “This seems safe.” Common sense: don’t eat things that literally have the allergen in the title. 🧠

Anyway, check your pantries. Return for a refund or toss in the compost where they belong. And maybe while you’re at it, buy a head of lettuce and some real garlic. Your salad doesn’t need to be a science experiment.

**Verdict:** This recall is a win for everyone except the person who thought “cheese” was a suggestion, not a fact.
