**Viral News Snippet: “Tom Kane’s ‘Time Capsule’ Recording Leaked—Did He Predict His Own Stroke?”**

🚨 **CLAIM:** A recently leaked audio clip from actor Tom Kane, recorded in 2020, allegedly contains him saying, “One day I’ll wake up and my voice will be gone, but I’ll still see everything.” The clip is being shared widely on TikTok and X (formerly Twitter), with fans claiming it was a “prophetic warning” about his 2022 stroke and aphasia diagnosis.

🔍 **FACT-CHECK: REAL OR FAKE?**
- **Source:** The 12-second clip was originally pulled from a private 2020 Zoom interview with a fan podcast called *The Clone Corner*, where Kane (best known as the voice of Yoda and Dexter’s Dad) discussed the physical toll of voice acting. The full interview includes him saying, “Your vocal cords age just like your knees. One day I’ll wake up and my voice will be gone, but I’ll still see everything—the scripts, the fans, the future of the character. That’s the hard part.”
- **Context Missing:** The snippet cuts out the full context. He was **not** predicting a stroke, but rather describing the natural decline of his voice over time. The phrase “see everything” referred to watching the *Star Wars: The Clone Wars* franchise continue without him.
- **Updated Status:** Tom Kane did suffer a stroke in 2022 that left him with aphasia and limited speech. However, no evidence ties the audio to a psychic prediction. The clip went viral after a fan account added a slow, reverb-heavy filter and the caption: “He knew.”
