**HEADLINE: IS JAKE SHANE BEHIND “OPERATION CASCADE”? FAKE WHISTLEBLOWER OR REAL INSIDER SCANDAL?**

**UPDATE — September 26, 2024 | Verified by Fact-Checkers**

A wild rumor is spreading across X (formerly Twitter) and fringe Telegram channels: that former Disney Channel star **Jake Shane** — best known for his viral “Therapuss” podcast — is actually the anonymous whistleblower behind a massive celebrity blackmail ring, codenamed “Operation Cascade.”

**THE CLAIM:**
Anonymous posts allege Shane, 27, has been secretly feeding information to a private investigator about a shadow network using AI-generated deepfakes to extort high-profile influencers. The posts claim he is guided by a “mysterious handler” named only as “Dr. X.”

**THE VERDICT: FALSE**

Our investigation found no evidence linking Jake Shane to any whistleblower operation. The rumor appears to have originated from a now-deleted parody Reddit account, “Cascade_Leaker_01,” which explicitly described itself as a “satirical fiction narrative.”

**THE REAL STORY:** Shane has publicly disclosed that he is taking a break from social media to focus on his mental health and a new documentary project about parasocial relationships — a topic that has nothing to do with blackmail rings.

**BOTTOM LINE:** No legitimate journalist, law enforcement agency, or credible source has confirmed the existence of “Operation Cascade.” Before sharing, ask: *Who profits from making a podcast host look like a whistleblower?* In this case, it’s a literary prank, not a leak.
