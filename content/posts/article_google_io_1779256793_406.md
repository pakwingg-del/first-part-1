**Top 5 Things You Need to Know About Google I/O 2024**

- **AI is the star of the show:** Google is rebranding its core AI model from "Bard" to **Gemini** and is weaving it into nearly every app—including a massive overhaul of Google Search that summarizes answers instead of just showing links.
- **Your phone becomes a real-life translator:** A new feature called **"Circle to Search"** now works across video and audio. You can circle any audible sound or song on your screen, and Google will identify it instantly, translating speech in real time.
- **Android gets a "Theft Detection" lock:** If your phone senses it’s been grabbed forcibly (running, biking, or sudden acceleration), it automatically locks the screen, protecting all your banking and private data instantly. No password needed.
- **Google Photos finally fixes memories:** An AI-driven **"Ask Photos"** tool lets you type natural questions like, "Show me my daughter’s best beach day from last summer," and it will find the exact photo—even if it wasn't labeled.
- **Project Astra: the sci-fi assistant is real:** Google demoed a new AI assistant that uses your camera to see the world—identifying objects, remembering where you left your keys, and even helping you code just by looking at a whiteboard. No release date yet, but it’s coming.
