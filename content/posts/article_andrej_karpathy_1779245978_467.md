**Headline:** BREAKING: Andrej Karpathy’s “Brain-Text” Interface Leaked? (FALSE)

**Viral Claim:**
Social media exploded Thursday after anonymous posts claimed former Tesla AI Director Andrej Karpathy had unveiled a prototype of a “neuro-texting” device, allowing users to type messages at 200 words per minute using only brain signals.

**What’s Real:**
Karpathy *did* post a cryptic tweet showing a blurry photo of a custom keyboard with an unusual electrode harness. The tweet simply said, “testing new input methods.”

**What’s Fake:**
No “brain-texting” exists. The harness is a *wireless EMG (electromyography) armband* that reads muscle twitches in the forearm, not the brain. He confirmed in a reply: “It’s literally just a super-responsive keyboard shortcut device—no mind reading yet. Sorry to disappoint.”

**Verdict:**
❌ **FALSE.** A clever misinterpretation of a wearable tech demo. Karpathy is not turning thoughts into text.
