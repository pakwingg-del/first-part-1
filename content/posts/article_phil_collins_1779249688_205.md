**Birmingham, UK** – A local resident is absolutely fuming after Phil Collins’ “In the Air Tonight” was played at a council meeting to signal the start of a public comment period.

“It’s common sense,” raged Darren Miller, 47, in the ‘Bearwood Banter & Best Buys’ Facebook group. “You don’t play a song about a man drowning his ex-wife’s lover when people are trying to talk about potholes and wheelie bin collections. It’s a tone-deaf, moody, woke mess. Where’s the respect for the tax payer? I can’t even hear the bloody drums over Brenda from number 32 moaning about her recycling.”

The post has since garnered 14,000 angry reactions and a petition to replace the track with “Sussudio” for “more family-friendly vibes.” Council officials have refused to comment, stating only that the choice was “a thematic nod to the tension of public speaking.”
