**VERDICT: MISLEADING**

**The Viral Headline:** *"BREAKING: 12 Million Americans Suddenly DROPPED from Obamacare - No Notice Given!"*

**What the Viral Posts Claim:**
Social media posts and fringe news sites are circulating claims that the Biden administration has "quietly purged" 12 million people from Affordable Care Act (ACA) plans "without warning," leaving families without coverage in the middle of prescription refills and ongoing treatments.

**What’s Actually True:**
- **Partially Accurate Number, False Implication:** The number 12 million is real, but it does *not* refer to current active enrollees losing coverage. The figure actually represents *total disenrollments* recorded over the last 12 months across all ACA marketplaces.
- **The Real Reason: Routine "Unwinding" (Not a "Purge"):** Since April 2023, states have been required to return to normal eligibility redeterminations after pandemic-era continuous coverage rules ended. This "unwinding" process was well-publicized.
- **Lost Coverage vs. Lost Eligibility:** Of those 12 million disenrollments, approximately 70% were dropped not for lack of notice, but because they **no longer qualified financially** (e.g., got a job with insurance) or **failed to return a renewal form** in the mail. Many of these individuals did not actually lose coverage—they were moved to Medicaid, CHIP, or employer plans.
- **"No Notice" is False:** Federal law requires multiple notices (by mail, email, and text) before termination. The real problem is that many people moved, changed phone numbers, or ignored the mail, leading to *administrative churn*—not a targeted purge.

**Why This is Viral (The Misinterpretation):**
Critics are conflating "disenrolled" with "thrown into the cold."
