**Viral News Snippet:**

**Title:** "Trump's 'RX' Initiative: Real Pill Bottle or Just a Placebo?"

**Body:**
A new image is circulating wildly on social media, purporting to show former President Donald Trump unveiling a new line of prescription medication called "TrumpRX" at a campaign rally. The image, which appears to be a mock-up of a pill bottle featuring a gold-foil label with his signature, claims the drug is "a proven cure for inflation, Biden’s brain fog, and election anxiety."

**The Verdict: FAKE**

Fact-checkers confirm this is a heavily edited digital hoax. The original photo is from a 2019 Oval Office event where Trump was signing a bill regarding lower drug prices. The "TrumpRX" label was added by a satirical account. No such drug exists, and there is no evidence Trump has endorsed any pharmaceutical product. The image is purely a meme designed to mock current prescription drug price debates.
