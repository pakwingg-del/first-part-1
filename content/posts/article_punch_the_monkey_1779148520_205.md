Posted in *Oakwood Valley Community Watch* 🚨

Can we please talk about the absolute circus unfolding at the dog park near the old water tower? Now the city is threatening to remove it because of one guy. Thanks a lot, Bob from the tie-dye hoodie. We can't have nice things. 🙄
