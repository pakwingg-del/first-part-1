**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**AI PIONEER ANDREJ KARPATHY ANNOUNCES NEW VENTURE FOCUSED ON EDUCATION AND DEMOCRATIZED LEARNING**

**PALO ALTO, CA** – Artificial intelligence researcher and former OpenAI founding member Andrej Karpathy has announced the launch of a new initiative designed to fundamentally alter how complex technical knowledge is disseminated and assimilated globally.

**What:** The new project, tentatively titled "Eureka Labs," is positioned as an AI-native school. According to Mr. Karpathy, the platform will leverage large language models to create deeply personalized, interactive learning experiences, effectively bridging the gap between static educational materials and live, one-on-one instruction.

**Who:** Dr. Andrej Karpathy, recognized for his pioneering work at OpenAI and as former Senior Director of AI at Tesla, is the founder and primary architect of the venture.

**Where:** The initiative is based in Silicon Valley and will operate as a digital-first platform, accessible to users worldwide.

**When:** Mr. Karpathy formally announced the project in a public statement earlier this week. The initial course, "LLM101n," focusing on the fundamentals of building a Large Language Model, is set to launch in pilot phase within the coming months.

**Why:** In his announcement, Mr. Karpathy stated that the mission is to "make education superhumanly accessible and engaging," addressing the global shortage of qualified instructors in cutting-edge technical fields. He posits that AI can serve as a "patient, infinitely knowledgeable teacher," allowing for a curriculum that adapts in real-time to a student's specific gaps in understanding.

**Key Details:** The first course, "LLM101n," will be an online, open-source, and freely available resource. Mr. Karpathy has confirmed he will personally be the primary instructor for the video components, while the AI aspects of the platform will handle supplementary tutoring and assignment
