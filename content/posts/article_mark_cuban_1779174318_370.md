**BREAKING NEWS: MARK CUBAN ANNOUNCES SUPPORT FOR BIDEN IN UPCOMING ELECTION**

**LOCATION:** Dallas, Texas | **DATE:** October 24, 2023

**WHO:** Billionaire entrepreneur and "Shark Tank" investor Mark Cuban.

**WHAT:** Cuban has publicly declared his intention to vote for President Joe Biden in the upcoming 2024 presidential election, citing economic stability and business concerns as primary motivators.

**WHERE:** The announcement was made during a live interview on CNBC’s "Squawk Box" from the New York Stock Exchange.

**WHEN:** Statements were made during the 9:00 AM EST broadcast.

**WHY:** Cuban stated that, while he has historically supported both parties, he believes President Biden’s policies on infrastructure, semiconductor manufacturing, and inflation control are more favorable for long-term business growth than the economic proposals from the Trump campaign. He specifically referenced the CHIPS Act and concerns over a potential "retaliatory tariff war" as decisive factors.

**DETAILS:** In a direct quote, Cuban asserted, "As a business owner, I need stability. I don’t need chaos in trade policy. I am voting for Joe Biden." This declaration marks a significant shift from his previous stated neutrality and is expected to influence debate within the independent and business-focused voter blocs.
