**GULFPORT, Mississippi (AP) –** A U.S. Senator faces a formal ethics complaint following a controversial incident that has sparked national debate. **Who:** Senator Tommy Tuberville (R-AL). **What:** Senator Tuberville has been formally accused of violating Senate ethics rules and conflict-of-interest statutes. **Where:** The complaint was filed with the Senate Select Committee on Ethics in Washington, D.C., with the alleged misconduct occurring in his home state of Alabama. **When:** The complaint was officially docketed on Monday, following weeks of public scrutiny. **Why:** The complaint alleges that Senator Tuberville utilized his official position and Senate staff to facilitate a business arrangement benefiting a family-owned investment firm, which subsequently received a federal contract. The Senator’s office has released a statement denying all allegations, describing the complaint as a politically motivated attack. The Committee on Ethics has yet to announce a timeline for its preliminary review.
