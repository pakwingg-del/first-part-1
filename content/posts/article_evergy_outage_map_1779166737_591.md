This isn’t an infrastructure problem; it’s a *character* problem. Evergy’s “live map” has become a metaphor for our hollowed-out society: a promised connection that delivers nothing but a loading wheel and a cynical shrug.

The true downfall isn’t the blackout. It’s that we have learned to expect the lie.
