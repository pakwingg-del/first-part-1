**FACT CHECK: Did Jake Shane’s “Therapuss” Podcast Suffer a Gargantuan $50 Million Sponsorship Loss After a Toxic Workplace Leak?**

**Verdict: FAKE (Satire Misread as News)**

**The Claim:** A post circulating on X/TikTok claims that Jake Shane’s popular podcast *Therapuss* lost a $50 million sponsorship deal with a major energy drink company after a disgruntled former producer leaked alleged “nightmare” text exchanges from Shane. The texts supposedly show Shane complaining about his co-host, fans, and calling the show a "pyramid scheme for catharsis."

**Our Investigation:**
We traced this rumor back to a satirical Instagram account called *“Podcast Drama Deconstructed (PDD).”* The account posts fictional, over-the-top narratives about internet personalities. The "leak" post was explicitly tagged #Satire and #FanFiction.

However, the post was screenshot and re-shared on four separate Facebook groups dedicated to "Internet Drama." In these groups, the #Satire tag was cropped out, and the post was presented as a breaking exposé.

**Key Context:**
- **Jake Shane** is real and his podcast *Therapuss* is highly popular. His brand is built on over-the-top, self-deprecating humor and chaotic energy.
- **The Numbers:** $50 million is an absurdly high figure for a single podcast sponsorship. The entire branded podcast sponsorship market for a show of *Therapuss’s* size is typically in the high six-to-low-seven figures annual.
