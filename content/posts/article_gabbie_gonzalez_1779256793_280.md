Then, in October of 2020, the signature flips. It’s not a data corruption. It’s a *re-render*. It’s like someone took the character model and swapped the texture pack. The glitch is clean.”

In the post-October footage, those blink rates become mathematically perfect. Too perfect. Like a simulation optimizing its assets.”

When pressed for evidence, LeVeque pointed to a single frame from a 2019 podcast where Gonzalez laughed, and a 2020 apology video where she cried. “The facial muscle displacement is identical to within 0.02% variance. Human faces don’t do that. It’s a re-used animation rig.”
