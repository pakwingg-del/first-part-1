**BREAKING NEWS — 14 February 2025**

**What:** A controversial new pharmaceutical initiative, provisionally dubbed “TrumpRx,” has been announced by former President Donald Trump and a consortium of private investors.

**Who:** Former U.S. President Donald J. Trump, alongside pharmaceutical executives and political allies, unveiled the plan during a press conference at his Mar-a-Lago residence. Regulatory oversight will fall to the Food and Drug Administration and the Federal Trade Commission.

**When:** The formal announcement occurred at 11:00 AM Eastern Standard Time today.

**Where:** The announcement was made at Mar-a-Lago, Palm Beach, Florida. Distribution of the proposed drug is currently slated to begin in select pharmacies across Ohio, Florida, and Texas by the third quarter of this year.

**How:** The TrumpRx plan leverages bulk purchasing agreements and direct-to-consumer telehealth prescriptions, bypassing traditional insurance middlemen. The Federal Trade Commission has confirmed it will investigate potential conflicts of interest, given the former president’s ongoing business holdings.
