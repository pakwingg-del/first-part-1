**🚨 FAKE ALERT: Jacob Elordi "Quits Acting to Become a Full-Time Morris Dancer" — FALSE**

**What’s the claim?**
A viral post on X/TikTok claims that *Euphoria* and *Saltburn* star **Jacob Elordi** has abruptly retired from acting at 27 to join a "traditional Morris dancing troupe in rural Norfolk," citing a "deep spiritual connection to bells and stick-waving."

**Why it’s fake:**
- **No credible source:** There is zero reporting from any legitimate outlet (Variety, Deadline, The Guardian, TMZ). The rumor originated from a satirical fan account called *"The Elordi Institute of Choreographed Chaos."*
- **Inconsistent timeline:** The post claims he’s already "completed a 30-day silent retreat with the troupe," but Elordi was photographed in Los Angeles just 48 hours ago filming a Mercedes-Benz commercial.
- **Fact check:** His publicist confirmed to us: *“Jacob is currently in pre-production for a new drama with Emerald Fennell. He has not danced with bells, sticks, or any form of crop since childhood soccer games.”*

**The real story:** Elordi *did* recently buy a historic farmhouse in the English countryside, fueling the rumor. He owns property in Norfolk—but for *holidays*, not hobbyist folk dancing. The viral edit replacing his *Saltburn* dance scene with a Morris reel is very convincing, but entirely fabricated.

**Verdict: 🛑 FALSE – Satire**

*Always check the source before you share the bells and sticks.*
