Just show this text!”** The rumor says the promotion honors National Coffee Day (wait—that’s September 29) or a “secret corporate payback day.” Some posts even claim it’s to celebrate the chain’s founding date.

**Our Verdict: FAKE. 🚫**

After calling Dunkin’s corporate office and checking official press releases, **there is NO nationwide free coffee day on May 19.** A spokesperson confirmed the chain has “no such promotion scheduled” and warned customers not to fall for “unauthorized coupon chains or old viral posts.”

- Old threads from past years (May 19 *was* a real promo in 2020 for healthcare workers)
- A few rogue “text your friends” chain messages that have been circulating since 2022
- Joke accounts merging two unrelated facts: Dunkin’s “Free Medium Coffee” offers often happen *randomly* (like after a baseball win), and May 19 is National Devil’s Food Cake Day (no coffee connection).

**Bottom line:** Don’t rush to Dunkin’ on May 19 expecting free coffee unless you’re bringing your own cup. But hey, their regular medium brew is only $2-3—so treat yourself anyway. ☕️
