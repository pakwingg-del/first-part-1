**Headline:** **JUST IN: Dominion Energy CEO Warns “Green Grid” Mandates Could Trigger “Catastrophic,” Nationwide Blackouts by 2027 — Critics Call It a “Moral War” on the Poor**

**VIRGINIA —** In a leaked internal memo that has sent shockwaves through Washington, Dominion Energy CEO Robert Blue allegedly warned state regulators that the company’s aggressive renewable portfolio mandates are “mathematically impossible” to meet without crashing the grid. The memo, obtained by a conservative watchdog group, claims that forcing the shutdown of natural gas and coal plants before battery storage is viable constitutes a “reckless gamble with human life.”

“This is no longer an environmental debate; it is a moral crisis,” said Dr. Helena Vance, a bioethicist at Liberty University. “We are trading the reliability of heat in winter and AC in summer for a symbolic victory against carbon. The people who will suffer first are the elderly, the poor, and the medically vulnerable. That is not progress—that is a calculated abandonment of the weak for the sake of virtue signaling.”

The leaked document describes a “rolling blackout scenario” affecting 15 million customers by 2027, painting a dystopian picture of hospitals running on diesel generators while suburban homes sit dark. Social media erupted with hashtags like #DominionDarkness and #GridGreed, with many calling the push for net-zero a “woke Ponzi scheme” that sacrifices tangible human safety for intangible global targets.

Critics argue that the real “downfall of society” isn’t climate change—but the moral failure of putting environmental dogma over basic, reliable energy. As one viral user posted: “You can’t save the planet if you let the people freeze.”
