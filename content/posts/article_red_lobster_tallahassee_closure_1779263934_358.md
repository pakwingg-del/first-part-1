**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**RED LOBSTER TALLAHASSEE RESTAURANT PERMANENTLY CLOSES; JOBS LOST**

**TALLAHASSEE, FL – April 4, 2025** – The Red Lobster restaurant located at 2524 North Monroe Street in Tallahassee has permanently closed its doors, effective immediately.

**What:** The closure of the established seafood chain location resulted in the termination of employment for approximately 45 local staff members. The property is now vacant and posted with a "Closed" notice.

**Who:** The action was executed by RL Investor, LLC, the parent company operating the franchise. Affected parties include 45 displaced hourly and salaried employees, as well as local suppliers and diners.

**When:** The restaurant ceased operations on March 31, 2025. Internal company memos confirmed the closure earlier this week.

**Where:** The eatery was situated at 2524 North Monroe Street, Tallahassee, Leon County, Florida, a location that had been in service for over 20 years.

**Why:** According to a corporate statement, the decision was driven by "ongoing market restructuring and significant shifts in consumer spending patterns." Legal filings related to the closure cite a 40% decline in foot traffic and unsustainable lease obligations.

**Status:** The restaurant is now fully shuttered. Company representatives have stated that affected employees are being offered severance packages and priority consideration for positions at other regional Red Lobster locations, subject to availability. No further closures in the Tallahassee area have been announced at this time.
