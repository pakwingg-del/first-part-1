- **The Victims:** The deceased has been identified as a 32-year-old male, pronounced dead at the scene. Two other victims, both in their late 20s, were rushed to a local trauma center with non-life-threatening injuries. Police have not released their names, but confirmed they are cooperating with investigators.

- **The Suspect:** A sole suspect remains at large, described as a male in his 30s wearing a dark hoodie and jeans. Authorities believe he fled the area on foot, vanishing into the riverbed trails. A massive police dragnet, including K-9 units and a helicopter, was deployed but failed to locate him.

- **The Motive:** San Diego Police Chief David Nisleit stated the shooting appears to be "targeted and gang-related," not a random act of violence. Detectives are combing through surveillance footage from nearby businesses and a gas station, searching for a vehicle the suspect may have used to escape.

- **Safety Warning:** Residents in the Mission Valley area are urged to remain vigilant as the manhunt continues. The riverbed trails, a popular spot for joggers and cyclists, will remain closed until further notice. Anyone with information is asked to call San Diego Crime Stoppers immediately.
