**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**From the Anchor Desk:**

**3:00 PM EST – February 19, 2026**

**LOCATION:** Plano, Texas; Nationwide Retail Outlets

**WHAT:** PepsiCo announced the permanent discontinuation of *Mountain Dew White Out*, a citrus-flavored variant first introduced to the market in 2010.

**WHO:** The decision was confirmed by a company spokesperson, citing a strategic shift in product portfolio management and evolving consumer taste preferences.

**WHERE:** The beverage will be removed from all retail shelves, convenience stores, and online distribution platforms effective March 31, 2026.

**WHEN:** The last production run was completed on February 10, 2026. Limited remaining stock is currently available in select regions, with no plans for a final "farewell" release.

**WHY:** Official statements attribute the removal to declining sales volume and the need to allocate production resources to higher-demand, limited-time offerings. Analysts also note the brand's focus on expanding its "zero-sugar" and "energy-infused" lines rather than legacy flavors.

**FOOTNOTE:** This marks the final chapter for a beverage that was famously voted back into permanent rotation by fans in 2011 via an online social media campaign.
