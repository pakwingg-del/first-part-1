Social media users are sharing the phrase #CBPGate, demanding to know whether this warning is about protecting travelers or engineering a narrative. Meanwhile, airline and travel stocks dipped slightly on the news — another coincidence, say watchdogs.

As Americans fire up their grills and pack their cars, the question remains: Is CBP warning us about a threat, or warning us to *behave*?
