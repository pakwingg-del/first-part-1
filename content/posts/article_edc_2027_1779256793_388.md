**EDC 2027 ANNOUNCED: LAS VEGAS MOTOR SPEEDWAY TO HOST RECORD-BREAKING FIVE-NIGHT MUSIC FESTIVAL FROM MAY 14-18**

**LAS VEGAS, NV – October 26, 2026** – Insomniac Events has officially confirmed the dates and expanded format for the Electric Daisy Carnival (EDC) 2027. Organizers announced today that the world-renowned electronic dance music festival will be held at the Las Vegas Motor Speedway from Friday, May 14 through Tuesday, May 18, 2027, marking the event's first-ever five-night run.

According to a press release issued this morning, the decision to extend the festival by one additional night was driven by unprecedented demand and a record number of artist submissions for the 2027 lineup. Officials state that the five-night schedule will feature over 300 performances across nine main stages, with production value expected to exceed $150 million—a 20% increase from the 2026 installment.

"We are thrilled to announce the largest EDC in our history," said Pasquale Rotella, founder and CEO of Insomniac Events, in a prepared statement. "The expansion to five nights allows us to curate a deeper, more immersive experience for the global headliner community, while maintaining the highest standards of safety and production."

The announcement comes after months of speculation following a leaked city planning document in July that suggested the Speedway had been booked for an extended period. Security protocols for 2027 will include enhanced screening checkpoints, expanded medical tent capacity, and a dedicated free shuttle system operating 24 hours a day from 12 off-Strip hotel partners.
