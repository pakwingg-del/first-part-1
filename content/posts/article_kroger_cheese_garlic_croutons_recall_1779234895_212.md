**SACRAMENTO, CA** – In a blow to salad enthusiasts and soup-dippers nationwide, Kroger has issued an urgent recall for its popular *Cheese & Garlic Croutons*, citing an "undeclared allergen" that has absolutely nothing to do with cheese or garlic. "After a routine quality check, we discovered that a certain batch of croutons—designed to be a crunchy, cheesy, garlicky beacon of hope—may have accidentally been produced without the correct label acknowledging the presence of the very ingredient we are advertising," a Kroger spokesperson said, stifling what appeared to be a laugh. "We apologize for the confusion regarding our cheese croutons tasting like cheese."

Consumers who purchased the croutons are advised to return them for a full refund, or, alternatively, to "just eat them and be happy you didn't get the ones with *secret* garlic." The recall is expected to last until the public collectively decides this is the funniest news of the decade.
