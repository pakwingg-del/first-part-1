**VERDICT: FAKE**

A viral post claiming that HBO has recast Harry Potter with a "gender-swapped, non-binary, wheelchair-using actress" named Riley Quinn-Dumbledore is completely fabricated.

**The Rumor:**
A screenshot of a fake press release from HBO circulated widely on X and TikTok, stating that the new Harry Potter TV show would feature a "reimagined" Harry, played by 16-year-old Riley Quinn-Dumbledore, who "uses a wheelchair for EDS and identifies as pansexual." The post claimed the show would be titled *Harry Potter: The Chosen One Remixed*.

**Why It's Fake:**
1. **No Official Announcement:** HBO has not cast any roles for the show yet. In March 2024, they opened a casting call for British and Irish children, specifically ages 9-11, to audition for Harry, Ron, and Hermione. Formal casting has not been finalized.
2. **Fake Source:** The image of the press release includes a typo ("Feburary") and a non-existent HBO logo variant. The post originated from a known satirical account, *The Wizarding World Observer*, which labels itself as "parody."
3. **Contradicts Previous Statements:** HBO has repeatedly stated the show will be a *faithful* adaptation of the books, not a "remix." The showrunner has said they intend to "tell the story exactly as J.K. Rowling wrote it."
4. **J.K. Rowling's Role:** Despite controversy around Rowling's views on gender, she is a producer on the show. It is extremely unlikely she would approve a version of Harry that deviates so drastically from the source material.

**What Is Real:**
- The Harry Potter TV show *is* happening. It was announced in 2023 and is planned for a 2027 release on Max (formerly HBO Max).
