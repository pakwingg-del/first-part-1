**Fact Check: VIRAL CLAIM — Stephen Colbert, Jon Stewart, Steven Spielberg, and David Byrne Planning a 'Mega-Show' to 'Fix America'? Rated: FAKE**

**The Rumor:** A viral post shared thousands of times on Facebook and X claims that Stephen Colbert and Jon Stewart have recruited filmmaker Steven Spielberg and musician David Byrne to co-create a one-time, four-hour "Mega-Show" called *The Great Unite*. The post alleges the event would air live on all major networks and streaming platforms on July 4, 2025, with the stated goal of "using comedy, film, and music to bridge the political divide and fix America."

**Fake Quote:** “We’ve spent years tearing each other apart on TV. Now it’s time to pick up the pieces,” the rumor attributes to Stewart, alongside a fabricated quote from Colbert claiming Spielberg is directing "a segment where David Byrne performs ‘Once in a Lifetime’ while wearing a giant flag suit made of 50 state flags."

**Why It’s Fake:**
1. **No Official Announcement:** Neither CBS (home of *The Late Show*), Comedy Central, nor any of the named individuals' representatives have announced or confirmed such a project. All four publicists contacted by this organization denied any knowledge.
2. **Fabricated Quotes:** The specific quotes cited in the viral post do not appear in any legitimate interview, press release, or social media post from Colbert, Stewart, Spielberg, or Byrne.
3. **Photoshopped Poster:** The viral image attached to the rumor—showing the four men smiling with the text "THE GREAT UNITE" in fireworks—is a digital manipulation. The original photo was taken at a charity event in 2019 and only featured Colbert and Stewart.
