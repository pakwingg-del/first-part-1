**FACT CHECK: Mountain Dew White Out’s Return? Rating: MIXED / CONTEXT-DEPENDENT**

**Headline:** *“Mountain Dew White Out VANISHES from shelves forever? Fans panic over ‘last chance’ grocery store sightings – but the real story is more complicated.”*

**Viral Claim:** A TikTok video with over 2.3 million views this week shows a user weeping in a Walmart aisle, claiming that White Out is “done for good” after July 2024. The caption reads: “RIP White Out. You were the best Dew. Grab ‘em if you see ‘em.” Several major fan accounts have reposted, warning that remaining stock is the “final batch” ever produced.

**Reality Check:**
- **What’s true:** PepsiCo officially discontinued White Out in late 2023 as part of a brand streamlining. Production has ceased. The cans currently on shelves are the very last residue of existing warehouse inventory.
- **What’s misleading:** The emotional “viral” video is genuine, but timing is key. A user *overlaying* the “last chance” panic is real; however, many commenters are *mistaking* a recent regional promotion for a permanent comeback. In late June, a **handful of Dollar General stores in the Midwest** accidentally received a mis-labeled pallet of White Out (a production overrun from a cancelled promo with a minor league hockey team). Those pallets were cleared out in 48 hours.
- **The actual viral rumor:** Some fans are claiming this “flood” of White Out means Pepsi is doing a “quiet re-release.” **This is false.** PepsiCo confirmed to DewDrinker.com that they have “no plans to bring White Out back” in 2024.
