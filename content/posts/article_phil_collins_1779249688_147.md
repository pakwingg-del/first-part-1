AITA for thinking Phil Collins’ solo career is peak Boomer midlife crisis, while pretending *Genesis* was the only good thing he ever did?

🚨 **BREAKING: PHIL COLLINS ANNOUNCES HE’S “KINDA SORRY” FOR THE 80s** 🚨

In a move that shocked absolutely no one with a functioning eardrum, Phil Collins has reportedly issued a “retroactive apology” for his entire solo discography, citing an “excessive use of gated reverb and questionable saxophone solos.”

“I looked back at ‘Sussudio’ and thought, ‘TL;DR, bro,’” Collins allegedly said from his secluded panic room, which is reportedly decorated entirely with framed *Tarzan* posters and a single, accusatory drum machine. “I spent 40 years telling everyone the *In the Air Tonight* drum fill was deep. It’s just a guy hitting a drum with a weird look on his face.”

The apology comes after a viral Reddit thread titled: “Phil Collins is the human equivalent of a dad joke that lasts four minutes.” Fans are divided. Some are calling it a “courageous act of self-awareness.” Others, mostly people who actually own a copy of *No Jacket Required*, are labeling him a “sellout to the woke mob.”

In a related story, Genesis has announced a reunion concert, but only if Collins agrees to sit silently in a drum cage while Peter Gabriel dons a flower costume and mimes the lyrics to “Land of Confusion” over a karaoke track.

**Verdict:** NTA. The man has 8 Grammys and a recording of “You’ll Be in My Heart” on loop in his head. He can apologize for whatever he wants. But we all know the real crime is that he invented the puffy shirt.
