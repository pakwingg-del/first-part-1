**VERDICT: FAKE** (with context)

A viral post claims: *"BREAKING: Sony announces PlayStation Plus will cost $199/year starting April 2025 – mandatory for online play even on PS4."*

**What's actually happening:** Sony *did* increase PlayStation Plus prices in September 2023 (Essential from $59.99 to $79.99/year, Extra from $99.99 to $134.99/year, Premium from $119.99 to $159.99/year). However, there is *no* credible report of another increase to $199 in 2025.

**Why it's fake:**
- Sony has not made any new price announcements since the 2023 increase.
- The claim of "mandatory for PS4 online play even without PS5" is misleading—PS Plus is already required for online multiplayer on both consoles.
- The $199 figure matches no current tier; it misrepresents Premium's current $159.99.

**Bottom line:** Prices did go up last year, but this specific viral claim is a rumored exaggeration. Always check Sony's official blog for legitimate updates.
