**HIGHWAY TO HEAVEN REBOOT: “Tell Your Story” Becomes a Viral Self-Care Challenge**

Life Coach Reality Check: The new *Highway to Heaven* reboot isn’t just nostalgia—it’s a psychological mirror. Every episode, angelic guide “Charlie” forces characters (and us) to name their deepest regret in under 60 seconds. Viral clips show him saying, “You can’t heal what you can’t say.” The internet is now flooded with #CharlieChallenge videos, where people confess one hidden pain in a traffic jam, a grocery line, or staring into a bathroom mirror. 

My take? This is cognitive defusion in real-time—the act of naming your shame out loud robs it of its power. The trend is therapy disguised as a game. But a warning: vulnerability without support can backfire. If you try it, end with “Now I release this” or call a friend. Don’t just name your hell—leave the highway.
