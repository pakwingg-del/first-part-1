**WHAT:** A flash flood warning is in effect, indicating that dangerous, life-threatening flooding is imminent or already occurring. Rapidly rising water levels are expected to overwhelm low-lying areas, roadways, and streams. The NWS advises that this is a "particularly dangerous situation" with potential for significant property damage.

**ADDITIONAL INFORMATION:** Officials urge all individuals to avoid any travel unless absolutely necessary. Do not attempt to drive or walk through flooded waters—just six inches of moving water can knock a person over, and twelve inches can carry away a vehicle. For real-time updates, listeners are advised to monitor NOAA Weather Radio and local emergency management channels.
