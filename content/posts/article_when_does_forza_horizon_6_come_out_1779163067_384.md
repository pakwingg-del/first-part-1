**DATE:** October 26, 2023 | **SOURCE:** Playground Games, Microsoft Gaming Division

**HEADLINE:** Playground Games Confirms No Forza Horizon 6 Release Window; Focus Remains on Horizon 5 Content Through 2025

**LONDON, UK** — In an official statement issued today to investors and the automotive gaming community, developer Playground Games has definitively stated that there is currently no planned release date for *Forza Horizon 6*.

**WHAT:** The next mainline installment in the open-world racing franchise, tentatively titled *Forza Horizon 6*, currently has no confirmed "when," "where," or "what" regarding its setting or release schedule.

**WHO:** Playground Games, the subsidiary of Xbox Game Studios responsible for the franchise, and Microsoft Gaming.

**WHERE:** Information was provided via a corporate fiscal year update regarding live-service titles.

**WHEN:** No release date exists. The studio has explicitly confirmed that *Forza Horizon 5* remains the active title and will receive continued "Satisfaction & Happiness" (S&H) content updates for the foreseeable future, with major expansions planned through late 2025.

**WHY:** The studio cited a strategic shift toward extended live-service support for *Forza Horizon 5*, which sits at over 40 million players, rather than transitioning development cycles to a full sequel this generation.

**HOW:** Gamers can access the franchise exclusively through *Forza Horizon 5* on Xbox Series X|S, Xbox One, and PC via Steam and the Microsoft Store. Playground Games confirms that *Forza Horizon 6* development is in the pre-production phase but will not enter active production until scheduled updates for Horizon 5 are complete. **No timeline for a sequel announcement exists.**
