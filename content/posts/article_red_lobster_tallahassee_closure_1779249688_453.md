**VERDICT: FAKE**

**The Viral Snippet:**
"BREAKING: Red Lobster in Tallahassee shuts down overnight after 'endless shrimp' disaster—employees claim the location ran out of shrimp and crab in under 48 hours, forcing a sudden closure with no warning to staff or customers."

**Clarification:**
This rumor is **fabricated**. While Red Lobster did file for bankruptcy in May 2024 and closed dozens of locations nationwide, the Tallahassee restaurant on Apalachee Parkway remains open. No credible local news source (e.g., *Tallahassee Democrat*, WCTV, WTXL) has reported a closure. The "endless shrimp" angle is recycled from a well-known 2023 viral hoax about a different location. Always verify with local business listings or health department records before sharing.
