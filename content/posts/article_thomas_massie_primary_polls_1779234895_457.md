**CLAIM:** A viral news graphic is circulating online claiming that **Kentucky Congressman Thomas Massie is trailing his primary challenger by 18 points** in a recent district-level poll.

**STATUS: FALSE / AI-GENERATED HOAX**

**The Facts:**
- **No Poll Exists:** The graphic explicitly cites a "Massie Victory Project / Emerson College" poll. However, the official Emerson College Polling team has confirmed to fact-checkers that **no such poll has been conducted.** The file name and numbers appear to be generated by an AI image tool.
- **Primary Date:** Kentucky's primary election is not until **May 20, 2025**. It is extremely rare for a credible district-level poll to be released 11 months before Election Day for a long-term incumbent.
- **The "Challenger" Problem:** The graphic names a challenger who, as of this date, **has not filed any official paperwork** with the Kentucky Secretary of State or the FEC to run against Massie. The supposed candidate appears to be a fictional placeholder.
- **Source Check:** The graphic’s origin has been traced back to a mainstream-sounding account that was actually created **three days ago** and was previously suspended for "platform manipulation."

**The Verdict:**
This is a disinformation operation attempting to create the illusion of weakness for a libertarian-leaning incumbent. **No legitimate polling challenges Thomas Massie's standing in his district.**
