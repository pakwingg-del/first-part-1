**CLAIM:** DTE Energy has secretly launched a "stealth outage map" that only shows power restored to neighborhoods with Democratic voters, while leaving Republican-leaning areas in the dark.

**VERDICT:** FALSE. No evidence supports this claim. The DTE outage map is a public, automated tool updated in real-time based on grid sensors and crew reports, not voter data.
