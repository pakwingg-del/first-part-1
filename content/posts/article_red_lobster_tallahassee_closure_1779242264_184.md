**CAN WE FINALLY TALK ABOUT THIS??**

Just drove past the Red Lobster on Apalachee Parkway and the sign is down. Empty lot. Another one bites the dust. And before anyone says "bless their hearts" or "used to love that place," let’s just call it what it is: this is what happens when you spend $30 on a plate of frozen shrimp and a bucket of Cheddar Bay biscuits that taste like they were baked in a 1995 microwave.

Common sense, people. You can’t charge steakhouse prices for Sysco truck leftovers and expect to survive in a town where folks know a good fried shrimp plate cost $12 at the Shell station down the road. Not to mention that "endless shrimp" deal they kept running? Yeah, that was bankrupting *them*, not helping you.

But hey, at least we still have the parking lot potholes as a memorial. R.I.P. Red Lobster. Maybe we can turn it into another vape shop or a mattress store—that's what Tallahassee really needs.

Let me know when we get a decent seafood place that doesn't taste like it came from a freezer. 🤷‍♂️
