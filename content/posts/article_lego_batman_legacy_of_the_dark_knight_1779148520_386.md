**HEADLINE:** LEGO ANNOUNCES "LEGACY OF THE DARK KNIGHT" SET: A COMPREHENSIVE TRIBUTE TO THE CAPED CRUSADER'S HISTORY

**BILLUND, DENMARK** — In a major announcement that has captured the attention of collectors and enthusiasts worldwide, the LEGO Group has officially unveiled its latest masterwork: the “LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight” set. This highly detailed and expansive model is designed to be the definitive tribute to Batman’s 85-year history across comics, film, and animation.

**WHAT:** The set is a 3,500-piece modular display featuring a central diorama of the Batcave, flanked by iconic scenes from Batman’s lore. Key components include a detailed Batmobile inspired by the 1989 Tim Burton film, a classic Batwing, and a series of micro-scale Gotham City skyline segments. The set also includes 10 exclusive minifigures, including multiple versions of Batman (from the 1960s TV series, the "Dark Knight Returns" comic, and the "LEGO Batman Movie"), along with key allies like Robin, Batgirl, and Alfred Pennyworth, and an extensive rogue’s gallery featuring the Joker, Catwoman, and Two-Face.

**WHEN:** Pre-orders open on September 10, 2023, with a global retail release scheduled for October 1, 2023. The official announcement was made earlier today via a live-streamed event on the LEGO YouTube channel.

**WHERE:** The set will be available at LEGO Brand Stores, the official LEGO website, and select authorized retailers worldwide. Regional pricing has been set at $349.99 USD, with equivalent pricing in other markets.

**WHY:** The launch coincides with the 85th anniversary of Batman’s first appearance in "Detective Comics #27" (1939).
