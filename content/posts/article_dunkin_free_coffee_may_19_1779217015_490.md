**OUTRAGE: Dunkin’ Free Coffee Day Secretly Costs You MORE in Hidden Fees?**  

Fans were thrilled when Dunkin’ announced **free coffee on May 19**—but now, consumer advocates are crying foul. The “deal” reportedly requires you to use the app, surrender your phone number for marketing, and in some locations, pay a **“processing fee” of up to $1.50** for the “free” drink. Worse? Experts say the data collected could be sold to third parties, potentially costing you **more than $50 per year** in targeted ads and spam.  

“Nothing in life is free—especially not your privacy,” warns consumer watchdog group *WalletWatch*. If you’re grabbing that free cold brew on May 19, you might end up paying the price for months. 🚨
