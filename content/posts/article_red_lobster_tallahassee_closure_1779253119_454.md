**Viral News Snippet:**

**🚨 FACT CHECK: Red Lobster Tallahassee NOT Closing (Despite Viral TikTok Claim) 🚨**

**What’s going around:** A TikTok video posted yesterday claims that the Red Lobster on Apalachee Parkway in Tallahassee is “permanently shuttered” due to a “massive pest infestation in the Cheddar Bay Biscuit ovens.” The video, which has already amassed 200,000 views, shows a blurry shot of a “CLOSED” sign allegedly taped to the front door and warns: “Don’t eat the biscuits if they reopen.”

**Our fact-check verdict: FAKE. 🚫**

🍪 **What’s actually true:** The restaurant was briefly closed for **scheduled deep cleaning and HVAC repairs** yesterday afternoon. A manager confirmed to us that the closure was pre-planned and lasted only four hours.

🦞 **What’s a distortion:** The “CLOSED” sign shown in the viral video is from a **2019 health inspection closure** at a different Red Lobster in Jacksonville. Someone re-used old footage and overlaid a misleading caption. The Tallahassee location passed its most recent health inspection with a 96 out of 100.

📉 **Why it’s spreading:** Red Lobster’s national financial troubles (including 2023’s “Endless Shrimp” losses) have people primed to believe the chain is collapsing locally. The Biscuit Oven “pest” claim triggers an emotional reaction, making the video more likely to be shared without verification.

**Bottom line:** The Tallahassee Red Lobster is still open—and still serving Cheddar Bay Biscuits (no pests included). **Don’t fall for this viral hoax.** 🦞🔍
