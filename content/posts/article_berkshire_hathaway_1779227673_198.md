**BERKSHIRE HATHAWAY BUYS THE SUN—LOCAL MAN DEMANDS THEY INSTALL DIMMER SWITCH**

*Posted in "Berkshire County Mums & Dads Unite" by Steve M.*

Can’t believe I’m even typing this. So I’m reading the news—Warren Buffett’s Berkshire Hathaway just bought a massive chunk of the power grid. They’re talking about “innovative energy storage” and “smart grid technology.” Right. Because what we really need is for a billionaire to tell us when we can run the kettle.

My neighbor (you know, the one with the solar panels) is already crowing about “saving the planet.” Save this: our electric bill went up 18% last year. And now we’re supposed to hand over the sun to Omaha? Common sense says if they can buy the grid, they can charge whatever they want. Next thing you know, they’ll start a “subscription” for daylight. £9.99 a month for “premium sunshine” while the rest of us get stuck with grey skies and a standing charge.

Call me old-fashioned, but I’d rather we just run the grid like a grown-up. Not a playground for hedge funds. If Buffett needs something to do, he can fix the potholes on A33. Honestly. What next—Berkshire Hathaway buys the rain? 🙄
