**WAUKESHA, WI – Fuming Local Resident Demands 'Common Sense' for Mandalorian and 'Baby Yoda'**

**Gordon "Skip" Kowalski**, a self-described "common sense real estate appraiser" and three-year member of the **Waukesha County Neighbors & Concerns** Facebook group, has penned a blistering open letter to Disney regarding the latest *Mandalorian & Grogu* trailer.

“I’ll tell you what’s wrong with Hollywood,” Kowalski wrote in a post that has since gone viral with 4.3k angry reacts. “That little green puppet, Grogu? He’s a blight. In my day, a bounty hunter followed the guild. You don’t haul a baby—a *force-sensitive* baby, mind you—into a cantina full of Trandoshan scum. That’s not a plot, that’s a daycare crisis waiting to happen.”

Kowalski went on to demand a “parental commonsense mandate” for all outer-rim bounty hunters, citing the high cost of beskar replacement and the “emotional toll” on IG-11 units.

“It’s time we stopped glorifying space dads who can’t even find a sitter. My neighbor Donna had to cancel her trip to the Mos Eisley Cantina because Grogu kept using the force on her chihuahua. It’s insanity.”

The post has 12,000 shares, mostly from other local residents chiming in with “THIS.” and “I saw it happen at Fleet Farm.”

**Disney has not yet responded.**
