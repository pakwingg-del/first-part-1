**🚨 BREAKING: Trump’s Plan to Cap Credit Card Interest at 10% Could Cost You More—Here’s the Catch**

As a consumer advocate, I’m here to tell you that what sounds like a win for your wallet might actually be a trap. Former President Trump’s latest proposal to cap credit card interest rates at 10% has everyone cheering—but here’s what the fine print doesn’t say.

**The Good News:** If you carry a balance, a 10% cap could save you hundreds a month compared to the current 20-30% average rates. That’s real money for rent or groceries.

**The Sneaky Catch:** Banks are already threatening to slash credit limits, jack up annual fees, and cut rewards programs. Worse—they could freeze thousands of low-income or subprime borrowers out entirely. If you rely on a card for emergencies, you might be left with nothing but payday loans at 400% interest.

**The Reality Check:** This isn’t a price cut—it’s a trade-off. You save on interest *if* you keep your card, but millions could lose access to credit just when they need it most. And with inflation still squeezing your budget, fewer options often mean more debt, not less.

**Bottom Line:** Don’t cheer yet. Demand details on who protects you when the banks retaliate. Your wallet’s safety net is only as strong as the fine print you read. 💸 #ConsumerWatch #CreditCardCatch #WalletAlert
