**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**DUNKIN' ANNOUNCES NATIONAL FREE COFFEE OFFER FOR MAY 19**

**CANTON, Mass. – May 17, 2025** – Dunkin' has officially confirmed a nationwide promotional event offering complimentary coffee to all guests on Monday, May 19, company officials announced today.

**Who** is involved: Dunkin' customers across all participating U.S. locations.

**What** is occurring: A one-day, no-purchase-required offer for a free medium hot or iced coffee.

**When** will this take place: Monday, May 19, 2025, during regular operating hours.

**Where** can customers redeem the offer: At any of the chain's more than 9,500 participating restaurants nationwide, excluding locations in airports, stadiums, and other licensed venues.

**Why** Dunkin' is providing this promotion: To celebrate the arrival of warmer weather and to thank its loyal customer base ahead of the summer season. No coupon or app download is required for redemption.

**How** can customers claim their free beverage: Offers are limited to one per guest while supplies last. Guests are encouraged to visit their local Dunkin' location directly.

Dunkin' representatives emphasize that the standard menu of flavor swirls, shots, and milk options remains available for an additional charge. The company advises customers to confirm participation with their local store prior to arrival.
