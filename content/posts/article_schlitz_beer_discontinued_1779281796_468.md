**Viral Claim:** "BREAKING: Schlitz Beer Permanently Discontinued After 175 Years – Last Shipment Leaves Milwaukee Tonight!"

**Fact Check: FALSE (Mostly).** While Schlitz is no longer the powerhouse it was in the 1970s, it has **not** been permanently shut down as of late 2024. The "The Beer That Made Milwaukee Famous" brand was acquired by Pabst Brewing Company and is still produced in limited quantities, primarily sold in the Midwest and as a nostalgic novelty. The rumor likely stems from a misinterpretation of a recent production pause or a hoax about Pabst cutting "zombie brands." Currently, Schlitz is still in production, though supply is highly limited and distribution is sparse.
