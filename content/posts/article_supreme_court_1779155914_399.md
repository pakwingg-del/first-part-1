**Top 5 Things You Need to Know About the Supreme Court's Blockbuster Ruling on Presidential Immunity**

This means every future case against a former president will now be slowed down by judges arguing over what counts as "official."

- **What Happens Next?** This ruling doesn’t just affect Trump. It sets a precedent for *all* future presidents. If you’re a candidate, you now have a powerful legal shield for actions taken while in office. The only question: will the next president push that boundary further? 🚨
