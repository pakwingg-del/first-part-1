**Top 5 Things You Need to Know About the San Diego Shooting**

- **Two Suspects at Large:** Police are urgently searching for two male suspects who fled the scene on foot immediately after the attack. Initial surveillance footage shows them wearing dark hoodies and masks, suggesting the ambush was pre-meditated.

- **Victims and Injuries:** Three people were struck by gunfire—two are in critical condition at a local trauma center, while a third sustained non-life-threatening injuries. A fourth victim was trampled in the ensuing stampede to escape.

- **Possible Gang Rivalry:** Law enforcement sources indicate a preliminary motive points to an ongoing gang dispute that boiled over into public violence. The location—a popular tourist and nightlife hub—has raised alarms about escalating street violence in the area.

- **Safety Warning Issued:** Police have urged all residents and visitors to avoid the Gaslamp Quarter and nearby downtown blocks until further notice. A heavy police presence remains, and the investigation is ongoing. Anyone with tips is asked to contact SDPD immediately.
