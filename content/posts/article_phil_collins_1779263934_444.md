**HEADLINE: Phil Collins Sells Entire Music Catalog to Universal Music Group in Record-Breaking $300 Million Deal**

**SNIPPET:**
The Phil Collins era just became a corporate asset. In a landmark deal shaking the music industry, the Genesis frontman and solo legend has offloaded his complete songwriting and recording catalog to Universal Music Group. The price tag? A staggering $300 million. This isn't just about nostalgia; it's a strategic liquidation by Collins, who has battled severe health issues, transforming decades of radio-dominating hits into immediate, illiquid capital. For Universal, it’s a defensive acquisition of a catalog that generates tens of millions annually. The subtext is brutal: in today's streaming wars, owning the rights to your past is a luxury, not a guarantee. Collins just cashed out, and the industry is watching.
