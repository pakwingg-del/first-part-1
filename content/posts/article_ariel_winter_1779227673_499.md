**Consumer Alert: Buttons Are Getting Busted—Ariel Winter Wants You to Ditch Dry Cleaning for Good**

The *Modern Family* star is sparking a wardrobe war this week, and your wallet is the real winner.

In a new, blunt interview, Ariel Winter revealed she’s done with the sky-high costs and hidden chemicals of traditional dry cleaning, calling it a “scam that’s ruining your clothes and your bank account.”

Here’s why this matters to you: A single trip to the dry cleaner can cost you $5-$15 per item. For a family, that’s often $100+ a month just to keep shirts and slacks “office-ready.”

Winter says she learned the hard way, dropping hundreds of dollars on delicate dresses only to have them shrink or fade. “We are being charged a premium for people to pour toxic solvents on our clothes and call it luxury,” she stated.

**The Viral Fix: The $3 “Freshen-Up” Trick**

Instead of carting your clothes to the cleaners every time they have a wrinkle or a whiff of last night’s dinner, Winter is championing a three-step method that’s blowing up on social media:

1.  **The Steamer, Not the Press:** A $20 handheld steamer kills bacteria and removes wrinkles in 30 seconds, with zero risk of shrinkage.
2.  **The “Freezer Jeans” Hack:** Pop your denim and wool sweaters in a Ziploc bag in the freezer for 2 hours to kill odors. No washing, no chemicals.
3.  **The White Vinegar Secret:** For underarm stains on “dry clean only” blouses? A 1:4 vinegar-to-water spray. You can thank the chemistry, not the cleaner.
