**VERIFIED FAKE: Ken Paxton "Arrested for Treason" at Austin Airport**

A viral video circulating on X claims Texas Attorney General Ken Paxton was arrested by federal marshals at Austin-Bergstrom International Airport on charges of "treason against the state."

**OUR VERDICT: FAKE.** The video is AI-generated. It falsely superimposes a 2023 mugshot of Paxton (from his separate securities fraud case) onto a generic airport arrest scene. No law enforcement agency—including the Travis County Sheriff or U.S. Marshals—has any record of this arrest. Paxton's office released a statement calling the clip "a deepfake designed to distract from his ongoing legal battles." The original audio is lifted from a 2019 DHS training simulation.

**Bottom line:** Do not share. This is a fabricated deepfake, not a breaking news story.
