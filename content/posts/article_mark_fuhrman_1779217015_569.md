**NEW YORK, NY** – In a move that has ignited a firestorm of outrage across the nation, former LAPD detective Mark Fuhrman has announced a new true-crime podcast titled *The Verdict’s Reckoning*, promising to ‘re-examine’ high-profile cases involving police misconduct—beginning with a whitewashed re-telling of the O.J. Simpson case. Social media has exploded with calls for boycotts, while cultural commentators warn that Fuhrman’s return to the public sphere marks a dangerous moral bankruptcy in our society, where even a perjurer and admitted racist can be rewarded with a platform for rewriting history. Critics argue we are witnessing the final, rotten harvest of a culture that has swapped justice for ratings, allowing a symbol of systemic corruption to profit from the very wounds he helped inflict. In an era starving for accountability, Fuhrman’s resurgence isn't just a comeback—it’s a chilling sign that our society has forgotten how to say ‘never again.’
