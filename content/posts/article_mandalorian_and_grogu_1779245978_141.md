**AITA for thinking the Mandalorian & Grogu movie announcement is just Disney's sad attempt to milk a dehydrated space cow?**

Okay, look, I get it. We all cried when the little green bean force-choked that frog lady's eggs. Adorable. But the moment they announced "The Mandalorian & Grogu" for theaters, my entire feed exploded with grown adults posting "The Child" Funko pops like they're planning a retirement fund.

TL;DR: Disney is going to slap a $20 ticket price on what will essentially be a 2.5 hour episode of "Dad Bod Protection Service." It's gonna be 90% helmet porn, 10% "aww" reactions, and 100% of Baby Yoda's screen time is just him drinking soup from a silver thermos.

And sure, call me a cynic, but this is the same studio that turned Boba Fett into a sad shopkeeper who needed a teenager to fix his ship. They're one "From the studio that brought you Space Wizards..." trailer away from having Grogu do a Fortnite dance in the middle of a firefight.

I'm supposed to be excited? The only emotion I have is the cold, hard realization that this movie will break a billion dollars and then we'll get a spinoff where Grogu opens a daycare for bounty hunter orphans.

Change my mind. Or don't. I'll be here watching Andor again.
