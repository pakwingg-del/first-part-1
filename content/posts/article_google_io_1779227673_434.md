**Subject: Google IO: The “Agentic Era” has arrived (and it will kill your inbox)**

**The News:**
At Google IO 2025, Sundar Pichai didn’t just announce updates. He declared an outright **war on workflow friction.**

**Why this matters in 30 seconds:**
- **Project Mariner 2.0:** Chrome now browses the web *and* takes actions for you. It doesn’t just find a flight; it books it, adds it to your calendar, and texts your assistant—all in one prompt.
- **AI Teammates:** Gemini is no longer a chatbot. It’s a permanent, contextual agent with memory. It sits in your Gmail, Drive, and Docs for months, learning your patterns. You don’t ask it questions; you set goals. It executes autonomously.
- **The “Proof of Humanity” mandate:** To slow down deepfakes and bot spam, Google is rolling out mandatory, invisible digital IDs for live video and voice calls. If you can’t prove you’re human, you’re muted.

**The CEO takeaway (bottom line):**
This is a direct blow to every SaaS productivity tool you currently own. If your team isn’t already using autonomous agents to close the gap between *thinking* and *executing*, your margin is shrinking. The real killer feature? This isn't a sandbox—it’s live.
