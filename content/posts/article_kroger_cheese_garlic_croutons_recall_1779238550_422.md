**Subject: ALERT: Kroger Pulls Garlic Croutons – Major Food Safety Breach**

Kroger has issued an urgent recall on its Private Selection Garlic Cheese Croutons over undeclared allergens—specifically soybean and wheat. This is a direct failure in labeling compliance, risking severe reactions for consumers with soy or wheat allergies. The affected batches have been pulled from shelves across multiple states.

**Bottom line for the C-suite:** This is not a minor mix-up. Mislabeling creates immediate liability exposure. The absence of a definitive cause raises questions about supply chain traceability and Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) adherence. Expect increased FDA scrutiny and potential class-action exposure if a consumer reaction occurs.

**Key figures:**
- Item: Kroger Private Selection Garlic Cheese Croutons (7 oz)
- Risk: Undeclared soy and wheat
- Status: Active recall, no cause specified

**Immediate action required:** Suppliers must verify labeling protocols and allergen controls. Legal and compliance teams should monitor for consumer reports. This is a reputational and financial risk, not a shelf-space problem.
