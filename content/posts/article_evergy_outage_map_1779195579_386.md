**HEADLINE: EVERGY OUTAGE MAP SHOWS WIDESPREAD POWER DISRUPTIONS ACROSS KANSAS CITY METRO**

**LOCATION:** KANSAS CITY, MISSOURI & EASTERN KANSAS

**WHAT:** A significant power outage event has impacted thousands of customers across the Kansas City metropolitan region. Evergy, the primary utility provider for the area, has activated its real-time outage map to provide updated information regarding affected zones and estimated restoration times.

**WHEN:** The outages began at approximately 2:30 PM Central Time, with peak disruption reported between 4:00 PM and 5:00 PM. The utility company indicates service restoration will continue through the evening hours.

**WHO:** Evergy, the region's electric utility, along with local emergency management agencies in Jackson, Johnson, and Wyandotte counties, are currently coordinating response efforts. Affected customers are advised to conserve resources and avoid downed power lines.

**WHY:** Preliminary assessment from Evergy officials attributes the widespread failure to a system disturbance involving a transmission line fault exacerbated by inclement weather conditions, including high winds and lightning activity in the area.

**HOW:** The Evergy outage map, accessible via the company’s official website and mobile application, allows customers to report outages and view a color-coded interface indicating severity. As of the latest update, approximately 48,000 customers remain without power. The company has deployed field crews and is prioritizing critical infrastructure sites, including hospitals and emergency services, for immediate restoration.
