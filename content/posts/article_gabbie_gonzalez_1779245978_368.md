**HEADLINE:** Former Digital Content Creator Gabbie Hanna Faces Multiple Legal Proceedings Following Social Media Controversy

**LOCAL & NATIONAL WATCH |** December 2024

**WHO:** Gabbie Hanna (formerly known as Gabbie Gonzalez), an American author, podcaster, and former Vine and YouTube personality.

**WHAT:** Hanna is currently the subject of multiple legal actions, including defamation countersuits and civil protection orders. These proceedings stem from a series of public disputes with other content creators regarding allegations of harassment, psychological distress, and contractual breaches. Legal documents also reference recent public statements regarding Hanna’s mental health and subsequent hospitalization.

**WHERE:** The legal actions are being processed in Los Angeles County Superior Court, California, with cross-complaints filed by Hanna against former associates. Concurrently, Hanna’s legal team has issued statements regarding her medical privacy following a widely publicized welfare check.

**WHEN:** The latest filings were submitted late last week. Hanna’s hospitalization and subsequent social media activity occurred throughout November and into early December of this year.

**WHY:** According to court documents, the lawsuits allege a pattern of online harassment and reputational damage, leading to demands for monetary damages and injunctions. Hanna maintains that the allegations are retaliatory and that her health and legal battles are being exploited for public entertainment.

**HOW:** The situation escalated when Hanna released a series of videos and audio recordings discussing private conversations with former friends and business partners. This led to formal cease-and-desist letters, which were followed by legal filings for temporary restraining orders. Hanna has since countersued, claiming defamation and tortious interference.
