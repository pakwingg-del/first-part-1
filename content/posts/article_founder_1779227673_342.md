In a development that has stunned both Silicon Valley and the world of ancient history, experts are drawing a startling parallel between the sudden and mysterious disappearance of tech founder Ethan Vance—who vanished from his $47 million yacht off the coast of Santorini last Tuesday—and the fall of the Republic of Athens in 403 BC.

“We mythologize the founder,” Rourke added. “But history shows: the tide that raises the island is the same one that wipes it from the map.”
