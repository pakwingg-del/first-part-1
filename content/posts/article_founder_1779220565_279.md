**MATRIX GLITCH DETECTED: The Founder Who Vanished from 1,200 Photos, But Not the Room**

In what analysts are calling the "Ghost in the Machine" anomaly, a viral forensic data audit has revealed a bizarre pattern: the CEO of a top AI startup was physically present—and talking—in 1,200 event photos. But his face? Completely missing. Not blurred. Not pixelated. *Blank.*

The glitch was first flagged by a graphic designer who noticed that while the founder’s colleagues, shadows, and even a coffee cup were captured clearly, the man himself appeared as a smooth, texture-less void in every single image—as if the camera actively refused to see him.

“It’s not a filter. It’s not a deletion. The metadata says he was there. The audio recordings from the same events confirm his voice. But visually? It’s like his file was corrupted in real-time,” said the lead analyst.

Conspiracy theorists are already calling it “The Erasure Protocol,” while digital forensics experts are baffled: the photos are RAW, untouched, and timestamped across three separate cameras.

The founder has yet to comment. The company’s website now redirects to a 404 error.
