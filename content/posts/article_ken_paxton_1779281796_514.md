**BREAKING: Life Coach Slams Ken Paxton’s “Winning” Mindset – Says Grudges Are a “Depression Trap”**

In a viral take that’s dividing the internet, prominent life coach Dr. Amelia Vasquez is speaking out about Texas Attorney General Ken Paxton’s recent acquittal and political resurgence. Rather than celebrating his “victory,” Vasquez is using the moment as a cautionary tale about the psychology of entitlement.

“We see a man who survived impeachment, investigations, and scandal,” says Vasquez. “But to a life coach, this isn’t resilience. This is deep, unprocessed trauma weaponized as a win. Holding onto that level of anger—’look what I got away with’—isn’t freedom. It’s a low-grade depression trap.”

Vasquez’s "Toxic Triumph" theory is trending on TikTok and Instagram, with many users claiming Paxton embodies a cultural obsession with “winning by any means” rather than “growing through integrity.”

Her advice? “Stop mistaking survival for success. If your victory requires you to stay in a state of war, you’re not a winner. You’re a hostage.”
